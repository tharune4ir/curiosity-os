# CURIOSITY OS: V2 ARCHITECTURE & PRODUCT PIVOT

**Status:** LOCKED DIRECTION (March 2026)
**Founder:** Tharun Gajula
**Core Shift:** From "Ontology Browser" to **"Teacher Operating System"**

---

## 🎯 V2 Product Identity
Curiosity OS is being rebuilt as a professional **Teacher Operating System** for educators, mentors, and schools—specifically targeting Class 9–10 supplementary education in India.

### Primary Audience
- **Educators:** Teachers, mentors, school administrators.
- **Students:** Class 9–10 (India focus).

---

## 🧠 The Core Atomic Pivot
| Layer | V1 Thinking (Legacy) | V2 Reality (Locked) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Backend Atom** | Node / Concept | **Node / Reasoning Unit** |
| **Frontend Atom** | Node / Universe | **Activity / Playbook Entry** |
| **Primary UX** | Graph-First Exploration | **Workflow-First Utility** |
| **User Intent** | "Explore the Map" | **"Run a Classroom Session"** |
| **Information Architecture** | Zettelkasten / Ontology | **Activity Library / Playbook** |

---

## 🚫 What We are NOT Optimizing For (Post-Pivot)
- **Do NOT** default to graph-first exploration or "ontological" language.
- **Do NOT** lead with overwhelming systems language or philosophical onboarding.
- **Do NOT** let the complexity of the 147-node backend dictate the simplicity of the Teacher UX.

---

## ✅ The V2 UX Optimization Goals
1. **Simplicity & Clarity:** High usability for first-time teachers.
2. **Activity-First Flows:** Quick answers to "What do I run today?"
3. **Low Friction:** Practical, classroom-ready materials and tracking.
4. **Calm & Confident UX:** Elegant and futuristic, but never intimidating.

---

## 📂 Information Model: The Activity Unit
The **Activity** is the primary visible unit. It bridges the gap between the teacher and the students.
Each activity maps internally (hidden/secondary) to:
- 147 Curriculum Nodes
- The 4 Wings (Decode, Cognition, Relate, Sandbox)
- Outcome Rubrics & Evidence Signals
- Methodology Notes

## 🚧 App Status & Legacy Strategy
- **Legacy V1 Surfaces:** Home cinematic, Enter Gateway, and 3D Universe Graph are now **Legacy V1 Artifacts**.
- **Preservation:** Legacy surfaces are moved/archived to safe areas (the `shelf/` or deprecated routes) rather than destroyed.
- **Incremental Restructuring:** Build new V2 surfaces cleanly while keeping the app running.
