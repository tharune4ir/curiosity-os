# Growth Loops: The 1% Compounding Simulator

## The Run Mode
### 1. The Setup
Two students (A and B) are simulated for 60 days.
- **Student A (Burst):** 10 points every 10 days.
- **Student B (Loop):** 1 point every day, but it *compounds* (next day is 1% better).
- Hand out simulation sheets to groups.

### 2. The Mission
Groups must run the simulation day by day.
1. Add points to Student A.
2. Add points to Student B using the compounding rule.
3. Apply "Friction Cards" (e.g., "Missed 2 days," "Fatigue penalty").

### 3. The Decompile
Focus on the **Power of Compounding**:
- Small gains accumulated consistently beat major, infrequent bursts.
- Time preference determines who cashes out early and who builds long-term assets.
