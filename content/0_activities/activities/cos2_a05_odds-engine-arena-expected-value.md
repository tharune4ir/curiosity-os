# Odds Engine Arena: Expected Value, Not Hype

## The Run Mode
### 1. The Setup
Each team starts with 100 tokens. 
Teacher displays two bets:
- **Bet A:** 10 to win 20 (Prob: 80%)
- **Bet B:** 10 to win 100 (Prob: 10%)

### 2. The Mission
Teams have 2 minutes per round to:
1. Choose a bet.
2. Write a one-sentence justification using "average result" or "risk".
3. Teacher reveals result (use a d20 or similar). 
4. Update bankroll. Run 8-10 rounds.

### 3. The Decompile
Focus on **Expected Value (EV)**:
- Luck can hide a bad bet in the short term, but it reveals the quality in the long term.
- Variance can wipe you out even if the average is good.
