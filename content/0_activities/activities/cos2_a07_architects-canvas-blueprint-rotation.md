# Architect’s Canvas Studio: Blueprint → 3D → Rotation

## The Run Mode
### 1. The Setup
Set up three stations in the classroom:
- **Station 1: Scaling Map** (Blueprint logic).
- **Station 2: Pack Problem** (3D volume logic).
- **Station 3: Net-to-Cube** (Mental rotation logic).

### 2. The Mission
Pairs have 25 minutes to complete the challenges:
1. **Net-to-Cube:** Predict which faces will touch without folding the paper yet. 
2. **The Fold:** Students fold and check their prediction. 
3. **Scaling:** Use similarity rules to resize a furniture layout for a smaller room.
4. **Packing:** Draw a plan to fit 10 irregular items into a box with maximum efficiency.

### 3. The Decompile
Focus on **Spatial Vision**:
- Spatial reasoning is the bridge between 2D representation and 3D reality.
- Transfer use: Packing, navigation, design, and blueprint reading.
