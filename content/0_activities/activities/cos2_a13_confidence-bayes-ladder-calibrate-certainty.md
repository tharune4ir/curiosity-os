# Confidence & Bayes Ladder: Calibrate Your Certainty

## The Run Mode
### 1. The Setup
Show a list of 10 trivia or estimation questions (e.g., "How many people live in Delhi?", "Which city is further north?").
- **The Catch:** Students must provide both an answer and a **Confidence Score (0-100%)**.

### 2. The Mission
1. **Calibration:** Students mark their answers and calculate their "Calibration Delta." (Am I overconfident or underconfident?)
2. **Bayes Wave:** The teacher reveals a "Base Rate" hint (e.g., "70% of northern cities share this climate trait").
3. **Update:** Students update their confidence score for specific questions.

### 3. The Decompile
Focus on **Precision Humility**:
- Truth-seeking is about accurately rating what you know.
- The Bayes Update is how smart minds stay updated with reality.
