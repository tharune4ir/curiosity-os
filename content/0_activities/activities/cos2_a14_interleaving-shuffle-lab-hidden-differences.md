# Interleaving Shuffle Lab: Spot the Hidden Differences

## The Run Mode

### 1. The Setup
Hand out two 'Problem Decks' (e.g., Geometry Area, Physics Vectors, or Sentence Correction).
- **Deck A (Blocked):** 10 problems of the same type.
- **Deck B (Interleaved):** 10 problems of three different types mixed together.

### 2. The Mission
Pairs study and practice:
1. Complete Blocked Deck A and note the 'Felt Ease'.
2. Complete Interleaved Deck B and note the 'Struggle'.
3. Run a **Transfer Test** with 3 completely new problems that look ambiguous.

### 3. The Decompile
Focus on **Strategic Selection**:
- Blocked practice builds 'Fluency' in execution.
- Interleaved practice builds the ability to **Select** the right tool.
- Discrimination is what wins on exams and in real life.
