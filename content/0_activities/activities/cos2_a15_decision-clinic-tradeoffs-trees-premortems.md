# Decision Clinic: Trade-Offs, Trees, and Pre-Mortems

## The Run Mode
### 1. The Setup
Each group picks a 'Decision Scenario' (e.g., "Join the debate team or focus on boards," "Partner with a friend or a high-performer").

### 2. The Mission
Groups work in 15-minute bursts to:
1. **Branching:** Draw a tree showing the 2-3 main paths. 
2. **Costing:** Attach 'Time' and 'Stress' costs + one **Opportunity Cost** ("What else could I have done?").
3. **Second-Order Effects:** "If I join the team, what happens to my gym time?" 
4. **Pre-Mortem:** "It's 6 months from now and the decision failed. What was the cause?"

### 3. The Decompile
Focus on **Rigorous Choosing**:
- Avoid emotional spirals by building **Decision Infrastructure**.
- The 'Correct' choice is the one that accounts for the most likely failure routes.
