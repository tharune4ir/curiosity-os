# Systems Dominoes: Causal Chains & Side Effects

## The Run Mode
### 1. The Setup
Each group picks a 'Causal Trigger' (e.g., "Free transit for all," "Ban all plastic," "Everyone gets an A").

### 2. The Mission
Groups draw a **Causal Domino Chain**:
1. **Direct Result:** What happens immediately? 
2. **Side Effect:** What happens to related parts of the system? (e.g., "Transit is free → Buses are crowded").
3. **Second-Order Ripple:** What happens *because* of the side effect? (e.g., "Crowded buses → More people bike").
4. **Third-Order Surprise:** "More people bike → City builds more paths → Bike shops flourish."

### 3. The Decompile
Focus on **Second-Order Thinking**:
- Smart minds look beyond step 1.
- Systems are connected; a pull here causes a push there.
