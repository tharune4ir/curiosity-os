# Model Stack Builder: Visual Notes & Synthesis

## The Run Mode
### 1. The Setup
Show the **Model Stack** template:
1. **Core Engine:** What is the main cause? 
2. **Ingredients:** What is it made of? 
3. **Historical Force:** What happened in the past? 
4. **Side Effects:** What happens to others?

### 2. The Mission
In pairs, students take a topic (e.g., 'The Rise of the Internet,' 'Photosynthesis,' 'The Industrial Revolution'):
1. **Identify the Core:** What is the 'engine' that makes it happen? 
2. **Layering:** Fill in the ingredients and historical forces. 
3. **Synthesis:** Connect the layers. 
4. **Conclusion:** State the final model in one visual diagram.

### 3. The Decompile
Focus on **Conceptual Synthesis**:
- Knowledge is a stack of related models, not a flat list.
- Synthesis is how you 'own' information and move it to long-term memory.
