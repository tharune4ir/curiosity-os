# Truth & Reliability Ledger: Reputation is a Score

## The Run Mode
### 1. The Setup
Show the **Trust Equation**:
- `Trust = (Reliability + Competence + Intimacy) / Self-Orientation`

### 2. The Mission
Students create their own **Reliability Ledger**:
1. **The Word:** Write 3 simple promises for the next 48 hours (e.g., "I will finish X," "I will arrive Y minutes early," "I will reply to Z").
2. **The Action:** At the end of 48 hours, mark: **Completed, Delayed, or Abandoned.**
3. **The Score:** Calculate your "Reliability Ratio." (The percentage of 'Words' that became 'Actions').

### 3. The Decompile
Focus on **Reputation and Trust**:
- Reliability is the easiest way to stand out in a noisy world.
- A high reliability score makes you 'low friction' for teammates and mentors.
