# Calibration Radar: Social Cue Reading & Adjustment

## The Run Mode
### 1. The Setup
Show a short social scenario (e.g., "A group project where one person is doing 90% of the talking").

### 2. The Mission
Groups use the **Calibration Radar**:
1. **Energy:** Is the group quiet or loud?
2. **Attention:** Are they looking at the speaker or their phones?
3. **Safety:** Do they seem comfortable sharing 'bad' ideas?
4. **Power:** Who is 'steering' the group right now?
5. **The Update:** Propose one specific change for the speaker: "Lower the volume," "Ask a question," "Pause for 5 seconds."

### 3. The Decompile
Focus on **Social Sensitivity**:
- Calibration is the bridge between intention and impact.
- Reading the 'unsaid' is what makes a great teammate and leader.
