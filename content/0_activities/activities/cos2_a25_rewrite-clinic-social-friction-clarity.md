# Rewrite Clinic: Social Friction & Clarity

## The Run Mode
### 1. The Setup
Show a highly emotional 'Friction Message' (e.g., "I'm sick of doing all the work while you just sit there and play games. I'm telling the teacher!").

### 2. The Mission
Pairs work together to "cool down" the message using **The Rewrite Rules**:
1. **Rule 1: No Labels.** Remove 'sick of,' 'just sit there,' and 'play games.' Use facts: "I have finished 3 tasks, and you have finished 0."
2. **Rule 2: Add a Request.** What do you want? "Can you finish Task 4 by tomorrow 5 PM?"
3. **Rule 3: The Why.** Brief context: "So we can submit on time for our marks."
4. **Conclusion:** Rated 'Storm Probability' 1-10. Original: 9. Rewrite: 2.

### 3. The Decompile
Focus on **Communication Hygiene**:
- Clarity is kindness.
- Reactive messages are expensive; rewrites are free.
