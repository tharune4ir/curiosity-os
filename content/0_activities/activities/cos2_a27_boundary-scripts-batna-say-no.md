# Boundary Scripts + BATNA: Say No Cleanly

## The Run Mode
### 1. The Setup
Show a scenario: "Someone asks to borrow your notes right before an exam. You don't have time to explain everything again."
- **The Ask:** How do you say no without making them angry?

### 2. The Mission
Pairs work together to build **BATNAs and Scripts**:
1. **Identify the 'Trap':** What is the pressure? (Borrowing, Gossip, Unfair Work).
2. **Build the BATNA:** "If they get angry, what is my fallback?" "I can still sit with my other friends." "I can focus on my own study."
3. **The Script:** Write a 5-word boundary script. (e.g., "I've gotta focus now." "I'm not doing that today." "Maybe another time.")
4. **The Swap:** Challenge another pair to 'break' your boundary without being aggressive.

### 3. The Decompile
Focus on **Power and Alternatives**:
- Boundaries only collapse when we feel like we have no other options.
- The **Best Alternative To a Negotiated Agreement (BATNA)** is the real engine of social safety.
