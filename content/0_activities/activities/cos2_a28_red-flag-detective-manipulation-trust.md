# Red Flag Detective: Manipulation, Gaslighting, Trust Logic

## The Run Mode
### 1. The Setup
Hand out a 'Social Case' card (e.g., "A friend says one thing in private and another in the group. When confronted, they say: 'You misremembered that, you're always so sensitive'").

### 2. The Mission
Groups work together in 20 minutes to:
1. **Red Label the Maneuvers:** (e.g., "Gaslighting: Distortion of reality," "Guilt Trap: Forced apology").
2. **Propose the Next Step:** "Ask a clarifying question," "Show the text record," "Step back from the group."
3. **Draft the Response:** "I remember the conversation differently. Let's look at the chat log."

### 3. The Decompile
Focus on **Reality Protection**:
- Manipulation works by making you doubt your own perception.
- Trust is about repeated alignment of words and behavior.
