# Skyline Outlaws: Risk Ladder Course

## The Run Mode
### 1. The Setup
Set up 4 physical challenges (use cones/tape/rope):
- **Balance Line** (Tightrope tape).
- **Control Jump** (Small, specific target).
- **Timed Navigation** (Obstacle course).
- **Stability Hold** (Plank/Hold).

### 2. The Mission
Groups work in teams with roles (Leader, Spotter, Risk Analyst, Operator).
1. **The Assessment:** Before any move, the Risk Analyst rating: **Consequence** (how bad?), **Control** (how skilled?), **Fallback** (what's the safety?).
2. **The Execution:** The Operator moves only when the team gives the 'Go'.
3. **The Score:** Groups gain points for 'Accuracy' of their risk ratings, not just for finishing.

### 3. The Decompile
Focus on **Risk Judgment**:
- Outlaws survive by being more prepared, not by being more reckless.
- Calm under motion is the bridge between thinking and acting.
