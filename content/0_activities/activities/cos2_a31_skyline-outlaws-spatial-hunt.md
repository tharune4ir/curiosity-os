# Skyline Outlaws: Spatial Treasure Hunt

## The Run Mode
### 1. The Setup
Hand out a 'Low-Fi Map' of the classroom/hallway/yard.
- **Constraints:** No-go zones (red tape), one-way paths (arrows), and 'Hidden Targets'.

### 2. The Mission
In groups of 4:
1. **The Representation Phase (15 min):** Teams must redraw the map to include their predicted 'Efficiency Path'. No movement allowed yet.
2. **The Run (20 min):** Teams navigate to find tokens at specific coordinates.
3. **The Score:** Calculated by: `(Tokens Found) / (Time Taken) * (Path Accuracy)`.

### 3. The Decompile
Focus on **Spatial Intelligence**:
- The map is an abstraction; navigation is the test of the abstraction.
- High-level planning kills low-level chaos.
