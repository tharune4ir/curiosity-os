# Alliance Arena: Communication Under Load Relay

## The Run Mode
### 1. The Setup
Each team has 3-4 roles in a chain: **The Coder (gives the signal)**, **The Relay (repeats it)**, **The Operator (executes it)**.

### 2. The Mission
Teams must transmit a "Code" (e.g., "3 cups up, 2 pens left") through the chain with constraints:
1. **Round 1 (Calm):** 30 seconds, no noise.
2. **Round 2 (Noise):** 15 seconds, music or group chatter.
3. **Round 3 (Constraint):** Blindfolded operator or relay cannot use specific words.

### 3. The Decompile
Focus on **Communication Clarity**:
- Stress shrinks the "attention window."
- Confirmation ("Did you copy?") is the only way to catch errors before they explode.
