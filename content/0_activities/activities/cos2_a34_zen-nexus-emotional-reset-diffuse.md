# Zen Nexus: Emotional Reset + Diffuse Thinking Protocol

## The Run Mode
### 1. The Setup
A quiet, 15-minute sanctuary after a stressful event (e.g., test, argument, transition).

### 2. The Mission
Individuals follow the **4-Step Reset**:
1. **Downshift:** Find a comfortable posture. 3 deep, controlled breaths.
2. **Dump:** Fill half a page with whatever's in your head. No grammar, no spelling, no logic. Just get it out.
3. **Name:** Circle 1 word that describes the "Triggering Pattern." (e.g., "Unexpected change," "Loud noise," "Unfair feedback").
4. **Act:** Write 1 small thing you will do next. (e.g., "Ask a question," "Drink water," "Check my notes").

### 3. The Decompile
Focus on **Attention and Regulation**:
- You can't think clearly while your "Red Alert" is on.
- The dump "clears the buffer" of your working memory.
- Naming the pattern turns an "emotion" into "data."
