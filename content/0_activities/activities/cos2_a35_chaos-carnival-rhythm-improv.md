# Chaos Carnival: Rhythm, Improv, Turn-Taking

## The Run Mode
### 1. The Setup
Whole class stands in a circle. 
- **The Initial Sync:** Pass a simple clap rhythm around the circle.

### 2. The Mission
Teacher introduces **The Chaos Rules**:
1. **Rule 1: Mirroring.** One student makes a small movement; everyone must mirror it perfectly within 2 seconds. 
2. **Rule 2: The Add-On.** Each person adds one sound or move to the sequence. 
3. **Rule 3: The Recovery.** When someone makes a mistake, everyone must clap once and say "Again!" loudly. No shaming; just repair.

### 3. The Decompile
Focus on **Social Sync and Repair**:
- Synchronizing is how groups build trust.
- Quick repair is more important than avoiding mistakes.
- Reading the 'timing' of others is a superpower in a team.
