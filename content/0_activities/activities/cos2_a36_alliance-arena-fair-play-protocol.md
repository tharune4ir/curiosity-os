# Alliance Arena: Fair Play Protocol (Prisoner’s Dilemma Live)

## The Run Mode
### 1. The Setup
Two teams (A and B) must choose "Cooperate" (Both get 3 points) or "Defect" (Winner gets 5, Loser gets 0). 
- If both defect, both get 1 point.
- **The Catch:** 10 iterated rounds.

### 2. The Mission
Teams have 2 minutes per round to:
1. Make a silent choice (C or D).
2. Report results publicly.
3. Update scores.
4. **Mid-Game Twist:** A 1-minute "Peace Talk" after Round 5.

### 3. The Decompile
Focus on **Trust Logic and Fairness**:
- Cooperate-First is the optimal social start; Tit-for-Tat is the optimal social defense.
- Defection creates a "Spiral of Death" where everyone loses.
- Fairness is what makes systems stable over 100 rounds.
