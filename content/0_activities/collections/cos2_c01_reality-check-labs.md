# Reality Check Labs: The Defense Suite

This collection is designed to be the "immune system" of the Curiosity OS. It starts with simple headline autopsies and moves into the deeper logic of statistics and evidence.

## Learning Objectives
- Detect axis truncation and denominator blindness.
- Distinguish between a 'signal' and 'noise' in a distribution.
- Rank claims by evidence strength instead of loudness.
- Understand the 'Reliability Ratio' in reputation.
