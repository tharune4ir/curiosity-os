# Build the Study Engine: Memory, Attention, Habits

This collection targets the most common failures in learning: rereading, multitasking, and ignoring error patterns.

## Learning Objectives
- Master **Retrieval Practice** as the primary study tool.
- Understand and reduce **Cognitive Load** in study materials.
- Implement a 15-minute **Attention Lock** sprint.
- Categorize mistakes into a **DEBUG Log** instead of a 'silly' bucket.
- Experience the power of **Compounding Habits** vs. Burst study.

# Decision Gym: The Strategic Thinking Suite

This collection moves students from "story-based" choosing to "evidence-based" deciding.

## Learning Objectives
- Map a choice as a **Decision Tree** with branches and costs.
- Attach **Confidence %** to declarations and update with Bayes logic.
- Understand the **Expected Value (EV)** of a bet vs its flashy story.
- Identify **Second-Order Effects** of a single policy change.
- Spot **Goodhart's Law** when chasing a single number.
