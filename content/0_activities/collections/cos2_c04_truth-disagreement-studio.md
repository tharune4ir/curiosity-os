# Truth & Disagreement Studio: Synthesis & Steelman

This collection is about mapping the disagreement territory and finding the 'Deep Truth'.

## Learning Objectives
- **Steelman** the opposing view before critique.
- Draw an **Argument Map** of an ambiguous debate.
- Rank claims on the **Evidence Ladder** (consensus vs anecdote).
- Build a **Model Stack** of complex historical or scientific forces.
- Update a verdict publicly in **Bias Court** as new facts arrive.
