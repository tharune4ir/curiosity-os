## The Invisible Reality
VFX artists add magical details to films and games using computers. They make things like explosions, monsters, or spaceships look real by combining digital images with live footage. It's like painting with light: they layer digital effects (like smoke or lasers) onto scenes. They use green screens (plain green backdrops) so the computer can replace the background with anything.

## The Mind Blowing Hook
You’ve seen VFX in action in superhero movies or rain on Mars in space films. Today, even news shows use VFX: like weather maps behind anchors. VFX artists often team up worldwide, working on enormous movie projects like those in Hollywood or Bollywood.

## The Players Life
You work with directors to understand the scene's needs. You use software (like After Effects or Houdini) to create effects frame by frame. You match the lighting and camera angle so the effect looks real. You also fix any mistakes, making sure added elements blend seamlessly with real actors.

## The Skill Tree
Creativity to envision effects, technical skill with compositing software, and patience (effects can require adjusting thousands of tiny details).
