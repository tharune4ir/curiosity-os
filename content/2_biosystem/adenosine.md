## The Simple Truth
Adenosine is a chemical molecule that builds up in your brain the longer you stay awake, like waste piling up in a jar. When adenosine is high, it makes you feel sleepy and tells your body it’s time to rest.

## Everyday Example
After playing football all day, you feel very sleepy at night because adenosine has accumulated in your brain.

## The Superpower
Understanding adenosine means you listen to sleepiness signals. Going to bed when you feel tired lets adenosine clear out and recharges your brain fully by morning.

## The Danger Zone
If you ignore strong sleepiness (staying up late to play video games), adenosine keeps building up and the next day you feel foggy and cranky. Chronic sleep debt disrupts many body functions.

## Action Step
If you're very tired, take a short 5-minute rest or nap rather than pushing through. This helps clear some adenosine.
