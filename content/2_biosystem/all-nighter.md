## The Simple Truth
An all-nighter is staying up all night without sleep, like skipping a full charge. Your body misses its needed rest and crashes on no energy.

## Everyday Example
If you stay up until dawn studying, you’ll be so tired the next day that you might do worse on the test and feel sick.

## The Superpower
Avoiding all-nighters ensures your brain is fresh each day, making your studying effective.

## The Danger Zone
An all-nighter leaves you exhausted and unfocused. Doing it often can hurt your health and grades.

## Action Step
Plan to study earlier so you can sleep before midnight and get at least 7 hours of sleep.
