## The Simple Truth
A balanced diet is like mixing the right ingredients in a recipe: it includes carbs, protein, fat, vitamins, and water in good proportions. This gives your body all it needs.

## Everyday Example
A plate with roti (carb), chicken or beans (protein), veggies, and a little oil is a balanced meal. You feel satisfied and energetic after eating it.

## The Superpower
Eating a balanced diet means you grow strong, stay healthy, and have energy throughout the day.

## The Danger Zone
Eating too much of one thing (like sweets) or skipping food groups causes nutrient gaps, weakness, and health issues.

## Action Step
Half-fill your plate with vegetables/fruit, a quarter with protein (dal, fish), and a quarter with whole grains.
