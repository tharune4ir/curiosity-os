## The Simple Truth
Blood pressure is the force with which your heart pushes blood through vessels, like how hard water flows in a hose. It rises with exercise or stress and falls with rest.

## Everyday Example
After running, your heart pumps harder, raising blood pressure. When you relax on the couch, it goes down.

## The Superpower
Regular exercise and low salt keep your blood pressure normal, helping your heart and brain stay healthy.

## The Danger Zone
High blood pressure (from too much salt or stress) strains your heart and can lead to serious problems later.

## Action Step
Eat more fruits and vegetables and less salty snacks to help keep your blood pressure in a healthy range.
