## The Simple Truth
Blood vessels (arteries and veins) are like highways and roads for your blood. Arteries carry oxygen-rich blood from your heart to your body; veins bring it back to the heart.

## Everyday Example
If you press your finger and see it turn white, then red again, blood vessels are filling and emptying. Good blood flow brings oxygen and takes away waste from cells.

## The Superpower
Clear blood vessels let your heart easily deliver oxygen and energy to muscles and brain when active.

## The Danger Zone
If vessels get clogged (poor diet) or stiff, your heart works harder. It can cause high blood pressure and slow blood flow, making you tired.

## Action Step
Add fruits or nuts to your diet to help keep vessels clear and flexible.
