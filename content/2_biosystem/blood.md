## The Simple Truth
Blood is the red fluid in your body that carries oxygen and nutrients to cells and takes away waste. It's like a delivery truck network keeping every cell fueled and clean.

## Everyday Example
When you cut your finger a little, it bleeds. That blood is running through your body delivering oxygen everywhere all the time.

## The Superpower
Healthy blood means every part of your body gets fuel and oxygen, so you feel energetic and alert.

## The Danger Zone
If blood is low on red cells (iron), you feel tired (anemia). If it’s clogged or dehydrated, you feel weak.

## Action Step
Eat iron-rich foods (spinach, beans) with vitamin C (lemon juice) to help your blood carry oxygen.
