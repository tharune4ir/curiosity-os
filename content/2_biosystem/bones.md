## The Simple Truth
Bones are like the steel frame of a building. They give your body shape and support so you can stand and move. They also protect organs (like skull protects brain) and store minerals like calcium.

## Everyday Example
Your thigh bone (femur) is a strong beam that holds your weight when you walk. Kids who drink milk and play outside build strong bones.

## The Superpower
Strong bones let you grow tall and avoid fractures. They support your muscles so you can run and jump safely.

## The Danger Zone
Without enough calcium or vitamin D, bones become weak (like rusting metal). You might get leg pain or be more likely to break a bone if you fall.

## Action Step
Drink a glass of milk or eat a calcium-rich snack (like yogurt or cheese) and spend some time in sunlight today for healthy bones.
