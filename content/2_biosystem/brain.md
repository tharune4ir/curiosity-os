## The Simple Truth
The brain is like the command center or CEO of your body. It processes all information from your senses and sends out orders. Your brain uses more energy than any other organ, so it needs good fuel and rest.

## Everyday Example
When you learn something new, your brain forms connections. If you try to study while sleepy, your brain won’t absorb information well.

## The Superpower
A rested, well-fed brain helps you solve problems, learn new things quickly, and handle emotions wisely.

## The Danger Zone
Ignoring your brain’s needs (no sleep, junk food) leads to brain fog and irritability, and you won’t perform well in school or sports.

## Action Step
Give your brain a boost: drink water and eat a healthy snack when studying so it has energy to work.
