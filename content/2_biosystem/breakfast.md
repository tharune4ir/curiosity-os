## The Simple Truth
Breakfast is your first fuel of the day. After sleeping for hours, your body needs new energy to start moving, just like charging a phone battery in the morning.

## Everyday Example
Students who eat breakfast (like porridge or eggs) feel more awake in morning classes. Skipping breakfast makes you feel sluggish and unable to concentrate.

## The Superpower
A good breakfast kick-starts your brain and body, improving focus and memory in school.

## The Danger Zone
Skipping breakfast crashes your blood sugar and energy. You get tired quickly and may overeat later.

## Action Step
Have something simple each morning, even a banana or a glass of milk, to power up your brain and body.
