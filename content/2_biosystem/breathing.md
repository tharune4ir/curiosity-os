## The Simple Truth
Breathing is the process of taking air in and out of your lungs. You inhale oxygen and exhale carbon dioxide. Good breathing is deep and regular to fully refresh your body.

## Everyday Example
When you yawn or breathe deeply after waking, you bring lots of oxygen in and wake up your brain and body.

## The Superpower
Good breathing (slow deep breaths) keeps you calm and gives more oxygen to brain and muscles, improving focus and endurance.

## The Danger Zone
Shallow rapid breathing (when anxious) can make you feel lightheaded or anxious. Bad breathing habits lower oxygen flow.

## Action Step
Practice breathing deeply right now: inhale for 4 seconds, exhale for 6 seconds, repeat 5 times.
