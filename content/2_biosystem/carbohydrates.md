## The Simple Truth
Carbohydrates are sugars and starches that give your body quick energy. They break down into glucose in your blood. Think of carbs as fast-burning fuel.

## Everyday Example
Eating a banana or a chapati gives you quick energy to play or study. Eating candy does too, but very quickly, and then you might crash.

## The Superpower
Eating whole grains and fruits (good carbs) gives you steady energy and keeps your concentration up.

## The Danger Zone
Eating too many simple carbs (sodas, sweets) causes a sugar rush and crash, making you sleepy and moody.

## Action Step
Choose whole grains like brown rice or multigrain bread for your next meal instead of white bread or sugary snacks.
