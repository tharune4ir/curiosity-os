## The Simple Truth
A consistent daily routine (wake time, meals, exercise, sleep) helps all your body systems run smoothly, like trains running on schedule.

## Everyday Example
If you eat lunch at the same time every day, your digestion learns that schedule and works better.

## The Superpower
A good routine makes you alert in class and strong in sports, because your body knows what to expect and when to recharge.

## The Danger Zone
A chaotic routine (late nights, irregular meals) confuses your body clock, making you tired, forgetful, or anxious.

## Action Step
Try going to bed and waking up at the same time for a week to establish a strong daily rhythm.
