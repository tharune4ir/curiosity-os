## The Simple Truth
Diabetes is when your body can’t control blood sugar properly. It’s like a broken thermostat: either insulin isn't working or there isn't enough, so sugar stays too high.

## Everyday Example
Someone with diabetes may feel very tired, thirsty, and have to go to the bathroom often because their body isn't using sugar for energy correctly.

## The Superpower
Keeping blood sugar under control with diet and exercise keeps you healthy now and avoids diabetes later.

## The Danger Zone
Untreated diabetes makes you always tired and sick. It can damage organs (eyes, heart) in the long run.

## Action Step
Limit sweets and move more to help prevent diabetes.
