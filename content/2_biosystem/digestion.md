## The Simple Truth
Digestion is how your body breaks down food into nutrients. It’s like a food factory: your mouth chews food, your stomach mashes it into a paste, and intestines extract energy and nutrients into the blood.

## Everyday Example
After you eat a chapati, your stomach churns it into mush and your intestines absorb the sugar and nutrients into your blood, giving you energy.

## The Superpower
Good digestion means you get all the nutrients from your food and feel strong and energetic after meals.

## The Danger Zone
If digestion is poor (like eating too fast or too much junk), you get stomach pain, feel bloated, or waste nutrients and feel weak.

## Action Step
Chew your food slowly and thoroughly; this gives enzymes time to start digestion in your mouth and helps your stomach.
