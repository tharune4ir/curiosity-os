## The Simple Truth
Electrolytes are minerals (like sodium and potassium) in your body fluids that carry electric charges. They help your muscles and nerves work properly and keep your water balance.

## Everyday Example
When you sweat a lot (like in summer), you lose electrolytes. Drinking a sports drink or water with a pinch of salt replaces them to prevent cramps.

## The Superpower
Balanced electrolytes make sure your heart beats steadily and your muscles contract correctly.

## The Danger Zone
Loss of electrolytes (dehydration) can cause cramps, weakness, or irregular heartbeat.

## Action Step
When sweating, drink some water with a pinch of salt or an electrolyte drink.
