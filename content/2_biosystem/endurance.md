## The Simple Truth
Endurance is like your body’s battery life. It’s how long you can keep going without getting tired. The more you train (running, swimming), the more endurance you build.

## Everyday Example
Someone with good endurance can run around the playground many times without stopping, while others get winded after a short sprint.

## The Superpower
High endurance lets you play sports or do homework longer without needing breaks.

## The Danger Zone
Low endurance makes simple activities feel exhausting and can discourage you from being active.

## Action Step
Try to increase your daily activity a bit: walk briskly or bike a little longer each time.
