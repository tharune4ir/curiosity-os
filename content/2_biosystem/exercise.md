## The Simple Truth
Exercise is any physical activity that makes your heart beat faster and your muscles work. It’s like a workout for your whole body, strengthening muscles, bones, heart, and even your brain.

## Everyday Example
Playing cricket, running, or dancing are exercises. After exercise you feel tired but also happier and sleep better at night.

## The Superpower
Regular exercise boosts your energy, makes your heart and lungs stronger, and even improves mood and memory.

## The Danger Zone
Not exercising enough makes you weak and tired easily. You’ll get out of breath doing simple tasks and risk gaining unhealthy weight.

## Action Step
Do at least 15 minutes of fun exercise today (like jogging or jumping jacks). Consistency strengthens your body.
