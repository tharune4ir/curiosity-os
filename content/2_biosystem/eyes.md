## The Simple Truth
Eyes are like cameras that let you see the world. They capture light and color and send images to your brain.

## Everyday Example
When you read a book or see the board in class, your eyes and brain work together. If your eyes get tired from too much screen time, you might squint or have a headache.

## The Superpower
Good eyesight helps you learn and play without strain.

## The Danger Zone
Weak eyesight can cause headaches and make learning hard if you can’t see clearly.

## Action Step
Take screen breaks and spend time outdoors to rest your eyes.
