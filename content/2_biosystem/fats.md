## The Simple Truth
Fats are a concentrated form of energy and also keep your body warm and protect organs. Good fats (from nuts, fish, oils) provide long-lasting fuel and help your body absorb vitamins.

## Everyday Example
Adding a handful of nuts or a drizzle of olive oil to your meal gives you healthy fats for energy that lasts, and it helps you stay full longer.

## The Superpower
Healthy fats keep your brain sharp and your energy steady. They also keep your skin and cells healthy.

## The Danger Zone
Too much unhealthy fat (like from fried chips or fast food) can clog your arteries and make you gain weight. Too little fat (cutting all fat out) can hurt your brain and energy levels.

## Action Step
Use small amounts of healthy oils or eat some nuts to include good fats in your diet.
