## The Simple Truth
Fiber is a part of plant foods (like fruits, veggies, grains) that your body can’t digest. It acts like a broom in your gut, cleaning out waste and keeping everything moving.

## Everyday Example
Eating an apple or whole-wheat bread adds fiber that helps you poop regularly.

## The Superpower
Fiber keeps your digestion healthy, helps prevent constipation, and even slows sugar absorption (preventing spikes).

## The Danger Zone
Low fiber diet leads to constipation, stomach pain, and blood sugar spikes.

## Action Step
Add one high-fiber food today (like whole grains or beans) to your meal.
