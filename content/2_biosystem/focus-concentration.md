## The Simple Truth
Focus or concentration is like shining a flashlight on a single task. It keeps your mind from wandering and helps you learn or do things faster.

## Everyday Example
Turning off your phone and closing your eyes for a second when studying helps you concentrate on homework better.

## The Superpower
Good concentration means finishing tasks quickly and learning more in class.

## The Danger Zone
Lack of focus causes mistakes, poor grades, and more study time needed.

## Action Step
Put away distractions (phone, games) before studying and take a deep breath to center your attention.
