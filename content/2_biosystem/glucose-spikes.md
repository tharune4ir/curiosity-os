## The Simple Truth
When you eat a lot of sweets or white rice quickly, sugar floods your blood like rocket fuel. Your body sends insulin (the delivery boy) to clear it out. After the surge, you hit a crash and feel exhausted.

## Everyday Example
You eat a heavy lunch of rice and sweets. In the next class, you feel so sleepy that you can’t focus on the teacher at all. This is the sugar crash.

## The Superpower
If you eat vegetables and protein before rice or sweets, the sugar enters your blood slowly, giving you steady energy for hours. You won’t feel sleepy during classes.

## The Danger Zone
Constant sugar spikes wear out your body’s delivery system (insulin), which can lead to diabetes later. In the short term, it causes mood swings and tiredness.

## Action Step
Drink a glass of water and eat vegetables or dal before eating rice or sweets to slow the sugar rush.
