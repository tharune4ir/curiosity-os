## The Simple Truth
Homeostasis is your body’s way of keeping everything in balance (like temperature, hydration, fuel). It’s automatic: your body adjusts (sweat to cool, shiver to heat, drink when thirsty) to stay in range.

## Everyday Example
When you get thirsty, your brain tells you to drink. You drink water and balance returns.

## The Superpower
Your body constantly works on homeostasis so you stay healthy without even thinking about it.

## The Danger Zone
If you push your body too far (no sleep, no water, too much heat), homeostasis can fail, making you faint or sick.

## Action Step
Pay attention to signals like thirst or hunger and act on them; it keeps your body in balance.
