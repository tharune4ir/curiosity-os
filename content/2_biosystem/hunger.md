## The Simple Truth
Hunger is your body’s way of saying it needs food, like a low-fuel warning light. Hormones signal your stomach to grumble and your brain to feel hungry.

## Everyday Example
If you skip breakfast, you feel your stomach growling and become irritable by mid-morning. Eating wakes you up and makes you feel better.

## The Superpower
Listening to hunger and eating on time keeps your energy and concentration steady all day.

## The Danger Zone
Ignoring hunger (skipping meals) makes you feel weak, dizzy, and then you overeat later, causing sugar crashes.

## Action Step
Set regular meal times and try to eat when you first feel hunger.
