## The Simple Truth
Hydration means giving your body enough water. Water is like the oil in a machine: it helps everything run smoothly (digestion, energy delivery, temperature control).

## Everyday Example
If you don’t drink water while playing in the sun, you feel very thirsty, dizzy, and tired. Drinking water keeps you alert and ready to play.

## The Superpower
Staying hydrated keeps your brain alert and your muscles working well. It prevents headaches and helps you think clearly.

## The Danger Zone
Not enough water (dehydration) makes you feel weak, gives you headaches, and even cramps. Your body overheats more easily.

## Action Step
Carry a water bottle and take a sip every 15-30 minutes, especially during hot weather or exercise.
