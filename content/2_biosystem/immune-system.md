## The Simple Truth
The immune system is like your body’s army of defenders. It fights germs and keeps you from getting sick. Good sleep and nutrition strengthen your immune soldiers.

## Everyday Example
Eating fruits with vitamin C and sleeping well means fewer colds. If you stay up late and eat poorly, you might catch a bug more easily.

## The Superpower
Strong immunity means you stay healthy and rarely miss school.

## The Danger Zone
Weak immunity (from no sleep or poor diet) means you get infections easily and recover slower.

## Action Step
Eat vitamin-rich foods (like citrus fruits) and wash your hands to help your immune system.
