## The Simple Truth
The large intestine is where your body reclaims water and the leftover becomes stool (waste). It’s like a recycling center for your food waste.

## Everyday Example
If you drink lots of water, your stools are softer. If you don't drink enough, your stools become hard because the large intestine takes more water from them.

## The Superpower
A healthy large intestine means no cramps or stomach pain and regular bowel movements.

## The Danger Zone
If it isn’t working well (like in constipation), you feel bloated and uncomfortable.

## Action Step
Eat fiber and drink water to keep things moving through your large intestine smoothly.
