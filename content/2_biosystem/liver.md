## The Simple Truth
The liver is like a large processing plant. It cleans toxins from blood, stores extra sugar as glycogen, and processes nutrients. It makes important chemicals for digestion.

## Everyday Example
After a sweet meal, the liver helps store excess sugar. It also stores vitamins and helps clear medicines from your system.

## The Superpower
A healthy liver keeps toxins out and supplies energy stores to keep you going.

## The Danger Zone
Overloading your liver (too much junk food or drink) can make you tired easily and get sick more often.

## Action Step
Limit junk food and drink plenty of water to help your liver stay healthy.
