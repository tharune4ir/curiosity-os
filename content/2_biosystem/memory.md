## The Simple Truth
Memory is how your brain stores and recalls information, like saving files on a computer. Good sleep and nutrition help your brain save memories.

## Everyday Example
Studying in the evening and getting a good night’s sleep helps you remember what you learned the next day.

## The Superpower
A strong memory means better grades and faster learning, because you remember facts and skills easily.

## The Danger Zone
Skipping sleep or studying in a noisy place weakens memory. You might forget lessons or tasks you just learned.

## Action Step
Review what you studied right before bed; it helps your brain lock that information in memory.
