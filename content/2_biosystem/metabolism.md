## The Simple Truth
Metabolism is how fast your body burns energy from food, even at rest. A fast metabolism burns more calories, keeping you lean, while a slow one stores more fat.

## Everyday Example
Kids often have fast metabolism and stay slim even if they eat a lot. If you stay active, your metabolism stays higher.

## The Superpower
With a good metabolism, you efficiently use food energy for growth and activities.

## The Danger Zone
A slow metabolism (from inactivity or too little food) leads to weight gain and feeling sluggish.

## Action Step
Stay active and eat small frequent meals to keep your metabolism revved up.
