## The Simple Truth
Micronutrients are vitamins and minerals needed in small amounts, but they are essential. They help your body work properly (immune system, bone strength, nerve signals) like tiny tools.

## Everyday Example
Eating fruits and veggies gives you vitamins like C (helps immunity) and minerals like iron (helps blood carry oxygen).

## The Superpower
Getting all needed micronutrients means strong bones, good eyesight, and a body that fights off germs easily.

## The Danger Zone
Lacking vitamins or minerals can make you tired and sick easily (like anemia from low iron, or weak bones from low vitamin D).

## Action Step
Include colorful fruits and vegetables in your meals each day to get a range of vitamins and minerals.
