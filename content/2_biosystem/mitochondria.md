## The Simple Truth
Mitochondria are tiny power plants inside your cells. They burn nutrients (from food) with oxygen to make ATP, your energy molecule. More mitochondria means more energy.

## Everyday Example
Athletes train to increase mitochondria in their muscles, giving them more endurance in games.

## The Superpower
Strong mitochondria let you produce lots of energy, helping you stay active and focused all day.

## The Danger Zone
Poor diet or lack of exercise can make mitochondria less efficient, making you feel weak and easily tired even with enough food.

## Action Step
Exercise regularly, even a short run, to build more mitochondria in your muscle cells.
