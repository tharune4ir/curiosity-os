## The Simple Truth
Mood swings are sudden changes in how you feel (happy one moment, upset the next). They often happen when your body is tired, hungry, or stressed because your brain chemistry changes.

## Everyday Example
Feeling grumpy and hungry an hour before lunch, then happy after eating, is a simple mood swing linked to hunger.

## The Superpower
Knowing what triggers your mood swings (like hunger or tiredness) lets you handle them and stay balanced.

## The Danger Zone
Frequent mood swings can strain friendships and make learning hard, and long-term can lead to anxiety or depression.

## Action Step
When you feel a sudden mood change, pause and check if you’re hungry, tired, or thirsty, and address it (eat or rest).
