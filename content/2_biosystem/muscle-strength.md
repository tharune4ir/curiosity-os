## The Simple Truth
Muscle strength is how strong your muscles are. Strong muscles can lift heavy weights or do many push-ups. You get stronger by using them (like lifting or climbing).

## Everyday Example
If you can easily carry your schoolbag filled with books, your muscles are strong. Weak muscles make even carrying a small weight hard.

## The Superpower
With stronger muscles, you can do hard tasks easily (like playing football or carrying heavy schoolbags). It also protects you from injuries.

## The Danger Zone
Weak muscles make you prone to pulling or hurting yourself when doing normal activities, and you tire easily.

## Action Step
Add push-ups or squats to your routine: for example, do 5 push-ups or squats every other day to build strength.
