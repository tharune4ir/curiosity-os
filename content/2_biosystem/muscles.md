## The Simple Truth
Muscles are like engines or rubber bands in your body. They pull on bones to make you move. When you exercise, muscles need fuel (ATP) and oxygen to work. Strong muscles come from training.

## Everyday Example
When you swing a cricket bat, your arm muscles power it. After playing, your muscles might feel tired or sore because they used up energy.

## The Superpower
Strong muscles let you run faster, jump higher, and carry heavy things easily.

## The Danger Zone
Without exercise, muscles get weak (like an engine rusting). You may feel tired doing simple tasks. Overworking muscles without rest causes strain or injury.

## Action Step
Do 5 push-ups or 10 squats now to wake up your muscles and keep them strong.
