## The Simple Truth
Neurons are special cells that carry electrical messages in your nervous system, like wires carrying electricity. Each thought or movement is a signal passed from neuron to neuron.

## Everyday Example
If you touch a hot stove, a neuron in your finger sends a message to your spinal cord, then a message back to your finger muscles to pull away.

## The Superpower
Healthy neurons (with good sleep and food) let you learn fast and react quickly.

## The Danger Zone
If neurons are damaged (by toxins or extreme lack of sleep), signals get scrambled. You might have memory trouble or slow reflexes.

## Action Step
Eat foods rich in omega-3 (like fish or nuts) which help protect brain cells.
