## The Simple Truth
Nutrition means giving your body the right food fuel. Good nutrition includes all the main nutrients (carbs, protein, fats, vitamins, minerals, water) to help your body grow, move, and fight disease.

## Everyday Example
Eating chapati with vegetables, dal, and a glass of milk gives your body energy and nutrients. Eating only chips and soda gives lots of bad calories and makes you feel weak.

## The Superpower
With good nutrition, you feel strong, have steady energy, and a sharp mind for learning. It keeps your bones, muscles, and immune system healthy.

## The Danger Zone
Bad nutrition (junk food, skipping meals) makes you tired, sick often, and you won't grow well. You may feel weak and unable to concentrate.

## Action Step
Add one fruit or vegetable to at least one meal today. It's a simple start to improving your nutrition.
