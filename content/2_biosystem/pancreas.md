## The Simple Truth
The pancreas is an organ that helps in two ways: it makes insulin (the sugar delivery boy) and digestive enzymes. It’s like a control center for blood sugar and digestion.

## Everyday Example
When you eat, the pancreas releases insulin to handle the sugar. If you skip it, sugar stays high.

## The Superpower
A healthy pancreas means steady blood sugar levels and good digestion.

## The Danger Zone
Pancreas problems can cause diabetes (high sugar) or indigestion.

## Action Step
Maintain a balanced diet to keep your pancreas working well.
