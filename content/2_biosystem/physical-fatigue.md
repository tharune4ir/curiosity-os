## The Simple Truth
Physical fatigue is when your muscles feel too tired to keep working. It’s like a car running low on fuel after a long trip. Your muscles have used up their immediate energy.

## Everyday Example
After running in a long race, your legs feel very heavy and you need to sit down.

## The Superpower
Recognizing fatigue means you rest when needed, letting your body recover for the next activity.

## The Danger Zone
Ignoring fatigue (overtraining) can cause injury like cramps or strains. You may feel sore and unable to move properly.

## Action Step
When you feel muscle fatigue, stop and rest or drink water for a few minutes before continuing any activity.
