## The Simple Truth
Posture is how you hold your body when sitting or standing. Good posture keeps your spine and muscles aligned properly, like having a strong spine support.

## Everyday Example
Sitting up straight at your desk keeps your back straight. Slouching makes your back muscles tired and can cause back pain.

## The Superpower
Good posture prevents back and neck pain, helps you breathe better, and even lets you look confident.

## The Danger Zone
Bad posture (slouching) can lead to pain and fatigue in your back and shoulders, and can make breathing shallow.

## Action Step
Check your sitting position now: straighten your back and pull your shoulders back, keeping feet flat on the floor.
