## The Simple Truth
A power nap is a short sleep (about 15-30 minutes) to give you an energy boost. It’s like a brief pit stop charging; it won’t replace overnight sleep, but it can reduce sleepiness and improve alertness.

## Everyday Example
If after school you nap for 20 minutes, you might feel refreshed and more awake for homework without feeling groggy.

## The Superpower
A quick nap can improve your mood, memory, and alertness. It’s like hitting the refresh button so you have energy for the rest of the day.

## The Danger Zone
Sleeping too long during the day (like an hour or more) can make you feel even more tired (because you enter deep sleep and wake up groggy).

## Action Step
If you need rest, set an alarm for 20 minutes and lie down in a quiet place right after school.
