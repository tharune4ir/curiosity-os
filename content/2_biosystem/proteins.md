## The Simple Truth
Proteins are like building blocks for your body. They repair muscles and organs and help make enzymes and hormones. Think of protein as the bricks that build and fix your body.

## Everyday Example
Eating dal, eggs, or paneer gives your muscles and bones the protein to grow and repair after exercise.

## The Superpower
With enough protein, your muscles get stronger and your body heals faster from activity or illness.

## The Danger Zone
Not enough protein (like only eating carb foods) can make you feel weak and slow to recover from exercise or illness.

## Action Step
Add a protein food (like yogurt, lentils, eggs or nuts) to your meals to ensure you get enough protein each day.
