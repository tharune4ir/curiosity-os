## The Simple Truth
Rest-and-digest is your body’s recovery mode after stress. It slows your heart, makes muscles relax, and turns on digestion. It’s like letting your body take a break to recharge.

## Everyday Example
After eating lunch and relaxing, you might feel sleepy. Your body is digesting and storing energy now that the stress is gone.

## The Superpower
Activating rest-and-digest (through calm activities or breathing) improves digestion and sleep. You recover quickly from stress and feel rejuvenated.

## The Danger Zone
If you never switch to rest mode, you can get ulcers, poor digestion, and insomnia because your body never gets a chance to relax.

## Action Step
Spend 10 minutes quietly after meals (no screens). This helps activate rest-and-digest and improves digestion.
