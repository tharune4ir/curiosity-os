## The Simple Truth
The spinal cord is a thick bundle of nerves connecting your brain to the rest of your body, like a major highway. It transmits signals (like reflexes) quickly between brain and body.

## Everyday Example
If you step on a thorn, pain signals travel up the spinal cord to your brain, and then your brain sends a message back to lift your foot.

## The Superpower
A healthy spinal cord means fast reflexes and smooth body movements.

## The Danger Zone
Damage to the spinal cord (injury) can block signals, causing paralysis or loss of feeling below the injury.

## Action Step
Protect your spine by sitting straight and wearing a helmet or seatbelt to avoid injuries.
