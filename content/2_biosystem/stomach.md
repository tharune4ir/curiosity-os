## The Simple Truth
The stomach is like a mixer. It uses strong muscles and acids to mash up food into a liquid, breaking it into smaller pieces.

## Everyday Example
Eating slowly gives your stomach time to mix food properly. If you eat too fast, you may get stomach ache because your stomach isn't ready.

## The Superpower
A well-working stomach helps extract maximum energy from food so you feel full and fueled.

## The Danger Zone
Overloading your stomach or eating spicy/fatty food can cause pain and indigestion.

## Action Step
Take smaller bites and chew well to help your stomach do its job easily.
