## The Simple Truth
Sweating is your body’s natural air conditioner. When you’re hot (exercise or sun), you sweat. As sweat evaporates, it cools you down.

## Everyday Example
After playing under the sun, you feel damp and cooler. That’s your body sweating to stay at the right temperature.

## The Superpower
Sweating effectively prevents overheating during sports or hot weather.

## The Danger Zone
If you sweat a lot and don't drink, you can become dehydrated and feel weak or dizzy.

## Action Step
When you sweat, drink water to replace the fluid and help your body cool down.
