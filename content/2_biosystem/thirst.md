## The Simple Truth
Thirst is your body’s signal that it needs water, like a low-battery warning. When you are dehydrated, your brain tells you to drink.

## Everyday Example
After running around, your mouth feels dry and you want water. That’s your body telling you it needs to cool down and replenish.

## The Superpower
Listening to thirst and drinking water keeps you balanced and alert.

## The Danger Zone
Ignoring thirst leads to dehydration: headaches, low energy, and poor concentration.

## Action Step
If you feel thirsty, drink a glass of water immediately.
