## The Simple Truth
Urine is liquid waste your body makes after kidneys filter your blood. It carries away extra water and waste like urea. The color and amount show how hydrated you are.

## Everyday Example
If you drink a lot of water, you see frequent clear urine. If you drink little, urine is dark and strong-smelling.

## The Superpower
Regular clear urine means your body is well-hydrated and flushing out waste properly.

## The Danger Zone
Dark or low urine means dehydration, which makes you dizzy and weak.

## Action Step
Monitor urine color: drink water if it’s yellow or dark.
