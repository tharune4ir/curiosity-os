## The Simple Truth
Vitamin D helps your body use calcium and strengthens bones. You get it from sunlight on your skin or from foods like milk and eggs.

## Everyday Example
Spending time in the sun or drinking milk helps your body absorb calcium so bones grow strong.

## The Superpower
Good vitamin D levels mean strong bones and a better immune system to fight germs.

## The Danger Zone
Not enough vitamin D makes bones weak and can cause pain, and your mood might suffer (you feel down).

## Action Step
Spend about 10 minutes outside in sunlight each day (safely) and include vitamin D foods like milk or fortified cereal.
