## The Invisible Rule
Trying to retrieve information strengthens memory more than passive restudy. This is the testing effect / retrieval practice idea

## The Matrix Trap
You reread notes and highlight. It feels smooth, so you think you learned. But the exam asks you to pull answers from memory.

## The Escape Hatch
Close the book and pull answers out: write, speak, or quiz yourself. Struggle (a little) is the point

## The Superpower
Less exam fear—because you trained the exact skill the exam uses: recall under pressure.

## The Level Up Action
Do the “Blank Page Test” for 5 minutes on one topic right now.
