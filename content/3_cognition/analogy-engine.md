## The Invisible Rule
Learning improves when new ideas connect to existing knowledge. Self-explaining helps this integration, and analogies are a simple way to do it

## The Matrix Trap
You treat school like ‘random facts.’ So every chapter feels separate and heavy.

## The Escape Hatch
Make one analogy: “This is like ___ in a game/cricket/phone.” Even a rough analogy creates a hook for memory and understanding.

## The Superpower
You remember faster and can explain better, because your brain has a ‘bridge’ to walk on.

## The Level Up Action
Take one science concept and write: “This is like ____ because ____.”
