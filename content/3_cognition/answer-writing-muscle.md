## The Invisible Rule
School exams reward retrieval plus clear expression. Writing-to-learn research supports writing as a tool for understanding, and retrieval practice strengthens what you can produce

## The Matrix Trap
You understand the concept in your head, but your answers are short, messy, and lose marks.

## The Escape Hatch
Use an answer template: Definition → Reason → Example → Diagram/Equation → Conclusion. Practice makes it automatic.

## The Superpower
You stop losing marks from poor presentation and incomplete answers.

## The Level Up Action
Rewrite ONE answer using the template above. Keep it under 10 lines but complete.
