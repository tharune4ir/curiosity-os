## The Invisible Rule
After you switch tasks, part of your mind keeps thinking about the old task. That leftover “residue” makes you slower on the new task

## The Matrix Trap
You stop maths in the middle, reply to a message, come back, and reread the same line 5 times.

## The Escape Hatch
Do a 10-second “close-out”: write one line—“I stopped at step ___; next I will ___.” Then switch

## The Superpower
Even when life interrupts (doorbell, parents), you can return without losing your place.

## The Level Up Action
Right now, write: “Current task: ____. Next step: ____.” Keep it on top of the page.
