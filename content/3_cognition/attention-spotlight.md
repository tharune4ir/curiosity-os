## The Invisible Rule
Attention is narrow. When you try to do two hard things, the brain usually switches back and forth, and performance drops

## The Matrix Trap
You keep the phone beside you “just in case.” Every 2 minutes your spotlight gets pulled away.

## The Escape Hatch
Use the spotlight on purpose: one task, full screen, for a short sprint. If a thought comes, write it on a “parking list” and return

## The Superpower
You start finishing homework faster than friends who study longer but keep breaking focus.

## The Level Up Action
Make a tiny “parking list” on the side. Next 15 minutes: each time you want to check something, write it there instead.
