## The Invisible Rule
Breaking at the right time helps attention and learning. Some studies also find that nature-like breaks can restore attention

## The Matrix Trap
You take a ‘5-minute break’ on Instagram and return after 45 minutes with a fried brain.

## The Escape Hatch
Use a clean break: water + stretch + look far away (window) + 5 deep breaths. Save phone breaks for AFTER the study block ends

## The Superpower
You keep your study momentum. Your brain feels ‘fresh’ more often.

## The Level Up Action
Next break: keep the phone in the same place. Stand up and drink water instead.
