## The Invisible Rule
Flow studies often find a link between feeling challenged and feeling skilled (the balance matters). It’s not perfect, but it’s a useful rule

## The Matrix Trap
You always stay in the comfort zone (only easy sums), so you never grow. Or you go too hard and quit.

## The Escape Hatch
Use level scaling: Warm-up (easy) → Main (medium) → Boss (hard). This keeps you in the ‘growth zone.’

## The Superpower
You build confidence AND real skill, not fake confidence.

## The Level Up Action
Choose 3 questions: 1 easy, 1 medium, 1 hard. Do them in that order.
