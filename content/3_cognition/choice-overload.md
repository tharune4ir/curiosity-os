## The Invisible Rule
When options are many and the choice feels complex, people can feel overloaded and delay choosing; meta-analytic work shows the effect depends on factors like complexity and uncertainty

## The Matrix Trap
You keep saving 50 “best study tips” videos and 20 timetables. You feel busy, but you don’t study.

## The Escape Hatch
Limit choices on purpose: choose ONE plan for 7 days. Treat other options like ads—ignore.

## The Superpower
You move from ‘planning addict’ to ‘execution machine.’

## The Level Up Action
Pick ONE study method for today (example: active recall + 25-min sprints). Commit for 24 hours.
