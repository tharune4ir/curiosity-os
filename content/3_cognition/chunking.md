## The Invisible Rule
Chunking helps because working memory has a small capacity (a few chunks). Better chunks = less overload

## The Matrix Trap
You keep rewriting notes because you can’t hold the full process in mind.

## The Escape Hatch
Compress: group steps into named blocks (like ‘setup’, ‘solve’, ‘check’). Then practice recalling the blocks first, details later

## The Superpower
You can do long problems without losing the thread mid-way.

## The Level Up Action
Pick one long answer and convert it into 4 headings only. No extra words.
