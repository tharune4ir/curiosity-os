## The Invisible Rule
Flow and learning improve when you know the goal and you get feedback. Without feedback, your brain can’t correct errors

## The Matrix Trap
You ‘study’ by staring at pages with no target—no questions, no checking, no score.

## The Escape Hatch
Play with a scoreboard: “10 questions → check → mark wrong → fix.” Feedback turns studying into skill-building

## The Superpower
You improve faster because you train exactly what the exam asks you to do.

## The Level Up Action
Write today’s goal as a number: “I will do ___ questions / ___ flashcards / ___ answers.”
