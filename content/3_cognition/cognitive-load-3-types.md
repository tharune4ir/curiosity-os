## The Invisible Rule
Learning fails when your mental load is too heavy. Some load is real difficulty (intrinsic), and some is “waste load” from messy studying (extraneous). Good study reduces the waste load

## The Matrix Trap
You study with 5 tabs open, WhatsApp pings, and loud lyrics. You feel tired fast and blame yourself.

## The Escape Hatch
Make it easier to focus: one resource, one page, one question at a time. Remove distractions first; then your brain spends energy on understanding, not on fighting noise

## The Superpower
Same study time, but 2× understanding—because your brain stops “wasting” power.

## The Level Up Action
Right now: close extra tabs. Keep only ONE book/app open for the next 15 minutes.
