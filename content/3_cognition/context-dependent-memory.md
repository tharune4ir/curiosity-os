## The Invisible Rule
Recall can improve when the learning context and testing context match (encoding specificity). Classic work showed better recall when study and test environments matched

## The Matrix Trap
You always study on the bed with music. In the exam hall (silence + desk), you feel strange and forget.

## The Escape Hatch
Do some study in ‘exam mode’: sitting at a desk, quiet, timed writing. You’re training the same context cues

## The Superpower
Your brain feels familiar in the exam hall, so recall becomes smoother.

## The Level Up Action
Do one 10-minute ‘exam mode’ mini-test today: no music, no phone, write answers.
