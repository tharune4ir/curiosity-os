## The Invisible Rule
Decision quality can be influenced by extraneous factors like time and breaks; some findings suggest patterns that look like “fatigue,” but newer work shows effects can be smaller or inconsistent depending on context

## The Matrix Trap
You spend 30 minutes deciding: “Which chapter? Which app? Which timetable?” Then you feel tired and do nothing.

## The Escape Hatch
Pre-decide your defaults: same study time, same first subject, same first task. Reduce decisions, save battery for solving and writing.

## The Superpower
You start studying automatically, like brushing teeth—no daily drama.

## The Level Up Action
Decide your default first 10 minutes: “I will always start with ____.” Write it and follow it today.
