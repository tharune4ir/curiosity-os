## The Invisible Rule
Some difficulties are ‘desirable’: they feel harder during study but create stronger long-term learning (spacing, retrieval, interleaving)

## The Matrix Trap
You only use easy methods that feel good (rereading, copying). You feel progress, but it disappears later.

## The Escape Hatch
Choose a little struggle on purpose: recall before looking, mix questions, space sessions. If it’s slightly uncomfortable, it’s often working

## The Superpower
You build memory that lasts beyond the exam—useful for competitive exams and real life.

For the next concept, try to answer first for 30 seconds before you peek at notes.
