## The Invisible Rule
Asking “Why is this true?” builds connections and deeper processing. It’s a researched learning technique (useful especially with facts and concepts)

## The Matrix Trap
You memorize lines like a parrot. Two weeks later, it feels like a foreign language.

## The Escape Hatch
For each key line, ask: “Why?” and “How do I know?” Then answer in your own words with one example

## The Superpower
You can write better answers because you actually understand reasons, not just words.

## The Level Up Action
Pick one definition. Write: “This is true because ____.” Add one real-life example.
