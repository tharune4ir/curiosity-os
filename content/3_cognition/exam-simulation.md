## The Invisible Rule
Your study should match your test demands. Retrieval practice builds recall skill, and matching contexts can help memory

## The Matrix Trap
You only read notes. Then in writing exam, your hand and brain are not trained to produce answers fast.

## The Escape Hatch
Simulate: timer + past questions + no notes. Then check, correct, and rewrite the weak part

## The Superpower
Exam becomes just another practice session, not a scary first-time experience.

## The Level Up Action
Set a 12-minute timer and answer ONE past question fully (even if imperfect).
