## The Invisible Rule
When you break a topic into basic parts and rebuild it, you create stronger schemas and deeper understanding than copying steps

## The Matrix Trap
You memorize a formula without knowing what each part means. One twist in the question and you panic.

## The Escape Hatch
Ask: “What are the parts? What does each part do? Why does it change the result?” Then rebuild the formula in words

## The Superpower
You can solve unfamiliar questions because you understand the engine, not just the button.

## The Level Up Action
Take one formula and write a one-line meaning for each symbol/term.
