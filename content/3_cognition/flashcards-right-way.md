## The Invisible Rule
Flashcards work when they force retrieval practice and when you space reviews. Research reviews rate practice testing and spaced practice as strong techniques

## The Matrix Trap
You spend 2 hours making colorful cards and 0 minutes testing. Or you write huge answers on one card and hate reviewing.

## The Escape Hatch
Make small cards: one clear question, one short answer. Review daily in small batches. Keep it boring and fast

## The Superpower
Daily 15 minutes keeps your memory alive without marathon revision.

## The Level Up Action
Create 5 cards from one page: ‘Q’ on front, ‘A’ in 1–2 lines on back.
