## The Invisible Rule
Flow is deep focus where you feel fully absorbed. It is more likely when distractions are low and the task is challenging but doable

## The Matrix Trap
You either do easy copying (boring) or jump to super-hard questions (stress). Both kill flow.

## The Escape Hatch
Make a ‘Goldilocks mission’: slightly hard, clear goal, quick feedback (like checking answers). Keep the environment quiet and simple

## The Superpower
You can study longer with less pain because the work feels like a game level, not torture.

## The Level Up Action
Pick ONE ‘slightly hard’ question right now. Start a 15-minute timer and try it without help.
