## The Invisible Rule
When something feels easy while reading, you may overestimate learning. Some research on “illusion of competence” shows students’ confidence can mis-match actual performance, especially with weak strategies

## The Matrix Trap
You stop practicing because “I understand everything.” Then the paper asks you to write/solve, and you can’t.

## The Escape Hatch
Make learning slightly harder on purpose: timer, mixed questions, blank-page recall. Difficulty is feedback, not failure

## The Superpower
You become accurate about what you know. That accuracy is a superpower in exams.

## The Level Up Action
For one topic you feel ‘easy,’ do a 3-question quiz without notes to prove it.
