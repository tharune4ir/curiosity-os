## The Invisible Rule
Background music with lyrics can hurt reading and verbal tasks, while instrumental music is often less distracting. Effects depend on task and person

## The Matrix Trap
You play loud songs with lyrics while reading English/history. Your brain starts processing lyrics instead of the chapter.

## The Escape Hatch
Use instrumental, steady music (many game soundtracks are designed to loop without grabbing attention). If you’re reading, keep it low or use no music.

## The Superpower
You can build a ‘focus ritual’: headphones on = study mode, so starting becomes easier.

## The Level Up Action
Try this once: 20 minutes reading with no lyrics (instrumental only). Compare your recall with a 5-question quiz.
