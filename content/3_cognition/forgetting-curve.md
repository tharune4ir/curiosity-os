## The Invisible Rule
Memory drops quickly after learning if you don’t revisit it. The classic forgetting curve was replicated in modern analysis, showing rapid early forgetting

## The Matrix Trap
You study today, feel confident, then touch the chapter again only at exam time—when your brain says, “Who are you?”

## The Escape Hatch
Do tiny reviews before forgetting wins: 2–5 minutes of recall today, then again after a few days, then again later

## The Superpower
You don’t need last-night panic. Your memory stays alive with small effort.

## The Level Up Action
Pick one topic you studied this week. Without notes, write 5 points from memory right now.
