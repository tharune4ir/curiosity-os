## The Invisible Rule
Gamification in education is often associated with improved motivation and learning outcomes in meta-analytic research (effects depend on design, but the direction is often positive)

## The Matrix Trap
Studying feels like punishment, so you avoid it and rely on cramming.

## The Escape Hatch
Turn study into a game: +10 XP per pomodoro, +30 XP per past-paper question, streak bonus if you study 5 days. Reward yourself after checkpoints.

## The Superpower
You study more consistently because your brain gets small wins every day.

## The Level Up Action
Create an XP rule now: “1 pomodoro = 10 XP.” Track XP for just one day.
