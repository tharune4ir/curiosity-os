## The Invisible Rule
When you generate an answer yourself (even imperfectly), you often remember better than when you only read the answer

## The Matrix Trap
You watch solution videos and feel smart. But your brain did not ‘generate,’ so it didn’t build strong memory.

## The Escape Hatch
Before seeing the answer, guess for 30–60 seconds. Then check and correct. Wrong guesses become sticky lessons

## The Superpower
You learn quickly from fewer questions because each question becomes a stronger memory.
