## The Invisible Rule
Willpower is unreliable. Changing the environment (making distractions harder to access) often works better than “trying harder.” Planning “if-then” actions also helps you start and stick

## The Matrix Trap
You trust your mood: “I’ll study seriously today.” Then one notification comes and the day is gone.

## The Escape Hatch
Add friction: keep phone far; keep book and notebook already open. Also set an if-then: “If I feel urge to check, then I will stand up and drink water.”

## The Superpower
You don’t need to be ‘motivated.’ Your setup does the work for you.

## The Level Up Action
Decide your friction trick now: drawer, high shelf, or give phone to a family member for 30 minutes.
