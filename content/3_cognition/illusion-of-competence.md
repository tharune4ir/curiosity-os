## The Invisible Rule
Rereading and highlighting can feel effective, but research reviews rate them as low-utility compared to practice testing and spacing. Feeling familiar is not the same as being able to recall

## The Matrix Trap
You read your chapter 5 times the night before. You feel prepared. In the exam hall, you look at the question and your mind goes blank.

## The Escape Hatch
The ‘Blank Page Test’: close the book and explain on a blank sheet (or to your wall). If you get stuck, that’s the real gap

## The Superpower
You stop wasting time on fake study and start focusing only on what you can’t recall yet.

## The Level Up Action
Tonight: study one page and then close the book and teach it out loud for 60 seconds.
