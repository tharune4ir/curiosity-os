## The Invisible Rule
Forgetting often happens because memories interfere with each other (proactive and retroactive interference), especially when material is similar

## The Matrix Trap
You study two similar topics back-to-back (like two biology cycles) and later mix steps and names.

## The Escape Hatch
Use ‘compare & contrast’ and spaced recall. Interleave with clear labels: “Cycle A vs Cycle B: 3 differences.”

## The Superpower
You stop mixing similar topics and start choosing the correct concept quickly.

## The Level Up Action
Pick two similar topics and write 3 differences from memory (then check).
