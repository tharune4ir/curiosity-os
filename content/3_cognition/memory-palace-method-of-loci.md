## The Invisible Rule
The method of loci (memory palace) uses familiar places to store items in imagined locations. Reviews and meta-analyses find it can enhance recall

## The Matrix Trap
You try to memorize lists (biology, history) in plain order. Your brain mixes items.

## The Escape Hatch
Use your house route: gate → hall → kitchen → bedroom. Put one crazy image per spot. Walk the route in your mind to recall.

## The Superpower
You can remember long lists and sequences that other students struggle with.

## The Level Up Action
Choose 5 places in your house and store 5 terms there with silly images.
