## The Invisible Rule
Good learners monitor what they truly know using feedback (like practice tests), not just feelings of familiarity. Practice testing is a top-rated technique in major reviews

## The Matrix Trap
Your plan is based on vibes: “This chapter is easy so I’ll skip.” Then it becomes your weak chapter in the exam.

## The Escape Hatch
Use a scoreboard: quiz yourself and track scores. Red/yellow/green should be based on results, not confidence

## The Superpower
You study the right thing at the right time—so you improve faster than rote learners.

## The Level Up Action
Make a 3-column list: Red (weak), Yellow (ok), Green (strong). Put ONE topic in each now.
