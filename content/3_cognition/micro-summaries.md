## The Invisible Rule
Summarization can be weak if it becomes copying. Research reviews rate summarization as lower-utility than practice testing and spacing when done poorly

## The Matrix Trap
You write 4 pages of summary that looks exactly like the textbook. Your hand worked, your brain slept.

## The Escape Hatch
Use tiny summaries that force choices: “3 facts + 2 reasons + 1 question.” Keep it short enough that you must understand

## The Superpower
You create notes that are actually usable for revision and actually improve understanding.

## The Level Up Action
For one page, write a 6-line summary: 3 facts, 2 why, 1 question.
