## The Invisible Rule
Tracking mistakes gives you targeted feedback and improves self-monitoring. Retrieval practice works best when it guides what you review next

## The Matrix Trap
You keep doing questions, but repeat the same silly mistakes because you never study your mistakes.

## The Escape Hatch
Make a 3-line log: (1) Mistake type (2) Why it happened (3) Fix rule. Review the log before the next practice set.

## The Superpower
Your weak points shrink quickly because you attack them directly.

## The Level Up Action
From today’s work, write ONE mistake log entry (even if small).
