## The Invisible Rule
People usually can’t do two demanding tasks at once; they switch. Switching reduces efficiency and can create big time costs

## The Matrix Trap
You say, “I can study with YouTube on.” But your brain keeps paying switching fees, so recall becomes weak.

## The Escape Hatch
One-tab rule: one task only. If you need music, choose instrumental and keep volume low

## The Superpower
You become faster than ‘multitaskers’ because you don’t waste brainpower.

## The Level Up Action
For the next 20 minutes, close all extra tabs/apps. Only one stays open.
