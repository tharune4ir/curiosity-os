## The Invisible Rule
Interruptions force task switching, and switching has costs. Each ping is like an enemy that pulls you out of your mission

## The Matrix Trap
You study with notifications on and keep “quick checking.” Your focus never becomes deep.

## The Escape Hatch
Nuke notifications: DND + airplane mode + only allow calls from parents. You decide when to check, not the phone

## The Superpower
You can enter deep focus faster, so even 30 minutes feels powerful.

## The Level Up Action
Turn on DND right now for 30 minutes. Put the phone screen facing down and away.
