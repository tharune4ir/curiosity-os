## The Invisible Rule
Practice testing improves learning, and classroom work shows frequent quizzing can improve retention. Past papers are practice tests with perfect realism

## The Matrix Trap
You collect notes like Pokémon cards but never battle. Then the exam becomes your first real fight.

## The Escape Hatch
Treat each past paper as a boss fight: timer + full focus + score. After, study only your mistakes (boss fight diary).

## The Superpower
You gain exam instincts: time control, question selection, and calm under pressure.

## The Level Up Action
Do ONE past-paper question today under a timer. Mark it strictly.
