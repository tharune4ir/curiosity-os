## The Invisible Rule
Some research links higher perfectionism with more academic procrastination—fear of mistakes can cause delay instead of action

## The Matrix Trap
You say, “I will start when I can do it perfectly.” So you don’t start. Then you panic later and cram.

## The Escape Hatch
Use the ‘ugly first draft’ rule: first attempt is allowed to be messy. Perfection comes on attempt 2 or 3, not attempt 1.

## The Superpower
You start faster, practice more, and improve more—because you actually do the reps.

## The Level Up Action
Do one ‘ugly’ attempt now: solve one question without caring about neatness. Neatness comes later.
