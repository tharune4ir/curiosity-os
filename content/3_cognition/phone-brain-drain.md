## The Invisible Rule
Some studies found that just having your smartphone nearby can reduce available mental capacity, even if you don’t use it. But at least one close replication found the effect did not show up the same way, so treat it as “often risky,” not magic

## The Matrix Trap
Phone on the desk, screen down. You tell yourself, “I won’t check.” Your brain still keeps watching it like a baby watching candy.

## The Escape Hatch
Put the phone out of sight and out of reach (bag/drawer/other room). If it’s harder to grab, you interrupt yourself less

## The Superpower
More brainpower for maths and writing—because your brain stops “guarding” the phone.

## The Level Up Action
For the next 25 minutes: phone in another room or inside a closed bag. Not on the table.
