## The Invisible Rule
Attention gets depleted during learning. Rest breaks can restore directed attention and support learning—especially on complex topics

## The Matrix Trap
You force a 3-hour sitting. After 40 minutes, you start doom-scrolling as a ‘break.’

## The Escape Hatch
Do rounds: 25–45 min focus → 5–10 min real rest (no phone). Treat rest as part of training, not a guilty pleasure

## The Superpower
You can study longer in total because you don’t burn out early.

## The Level Up Action
Set one timer: 25 minutes. One task only. When it rings, stand up for 2 minutes.
