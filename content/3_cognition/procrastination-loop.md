## The Invisible Rule
Procrastination is often tied to emotions (fear, stress) and can be linked with perfectionism. Planning tools like if-then intentions can help people start actions more reliably

## The Matrix Trap
You delay for ‘just 10 minutes’ and suddenly it’s 11 pm and you hate yourself.

## The Escape Hatch
Break the loop with a tiny start + clear plan: “If it is 7 pm, then I will do 2 minutes of Q1.” Starting is the victory

## The Superpower
You become consistent even on bad days, so you don’t need last-week panic.

## The Level Up Action
Right now: do a 2-minute start on your hardest subject (no pressure to finish).
