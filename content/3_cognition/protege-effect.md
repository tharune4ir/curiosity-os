## The Invisible Rule
When you expect to teach, you organize and understand better. Research on learning-by-teaching shows benefits from preparing to teach and from explaining

## The Matrix Trap
You study silently and never practice explaining, so knowledge stays fuzzy.

## The Escape Hatch
Teach someone (or pretend): “I’m the teacher, you’re the student.” Teaching forces structure and clarity.

## The Superpower
You become confident in viva, oral answers, and long writing because you practiced explaining clearly.

## The Level Up Action
Take 2 minutes and teach ONE idea to a sibling/friend (or imaginary student).
