## The Invisible Rule
Your brain stores “patterns” (schemas), like a game skill tree. When you build a schema, problems feel easy because your brain sees the pattern quickly

## The Matrix Trap
You memorize steps like a robot. If the question changes a little, you get stuck.

## The Escape Hatch
Build a mini skill tree: “If I see THIS kind of question → I use THIS method → I check THIS.” That turns random facts into a pattern

## The Superpower
You stop panicking with “new” questions because you can recognize the category and choose the right tool.

## The Level Up Action
After solving one problem, write: “This problem type = _____. Steps = 1) __ 2) __ 3) __.”
