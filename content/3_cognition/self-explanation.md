## The Invisible Rule
Explaining to yourself helps you integrate new info with what you already know and improves understanding, especially with worked examples

## The Matrix Trap
You read a solved example silently. It feels obvious. Then you can’t solve a similar one alone.

## The Escape Hatch
Be a commentator: “Why did they do this step? What does it mean?” If you can’t explain, you don’t fully understand yet

## The Superpower
You stop memorizing steps and start understanding reasons. That’s what top students do secretly.

## The Level Up Action
Solve one example and speak every step in simple words (even quietly).
