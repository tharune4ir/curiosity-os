## The Invisible Rule
When you keep one goal in mind, the brain doesn’t pay repeated “switching fees.” Switching is costly, even if it feels small

## The Matrix Trap
You study in “battle royale mode”: maths + Insta + YouTube + snacks + random thoughts.

## The Escape Hatch
Go single-player: choose ONE mission (example: ‘10 MCQs’). No switching until the mission ends

## The Superpower
You get more done in less time, and you feel less tired after studying.

## The Level Up Action
Write your next mission in 7 words or less. Example: “Solve 8 linear equations + check.”
