## The Invisible Rule
Sleep supports memory consolidation—helping stabilize and organize what you learned. Cutting sleep can reduce learning quality

## The Matrix Trap
You do an all-nighter, thinking “more hours = more marks,” then forget and make silly mistakes next day.

## The Escape Hatch
Sleep first, then study. Do a quick 3-minute recall before bed, sleep, then do a 3-minute recall in the morning

## The Superpower
You feel sharper, recall faster, and your mood is steadier during exams.

## The Level Up Action
Tonight: stop studying 20 minutes earlier and do 10 minutes of revision + sleep instead of scrolling.
