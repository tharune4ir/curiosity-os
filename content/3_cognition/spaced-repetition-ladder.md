## The Invisible Rule
Spacing study sessions beats massed cramming across many experiments (meta-analysis). The timing matters, but the big idea is simple: spread it out

## The Matrix Trap
You do ‘revision day’ like a festival and forget most of it two weeks later.

## The Escape Hatch
Use a ladder: Day 0 (learn) → Day 1 (quick recall) → Day 3–4 (recall) → Day 7 (mixed quiz)

## The Superpower
You remember chapters months later, not just “for tomorrow.”

## The Level Up Action
Make 3 reminder dates for one chapter (write them in your calendar/diary).
