## The Invisible Rule
Processing information in a story format can improve recall compared to shallow processing. Story structure gives your brain a path to follow

## The Matrix Trap
History/civics becomes 50 disconnected facts that fall out of your head after the exam.

## The Escape Hatch
Convert to story: Who is the main character? What do they want? What problem happens? What changes after? Now the facts become scenes.

## The Superpower
You can write long answers with flow because you remember the story order.

## The Level Up Action
Pick 5 facts and connect them into a 4-line story: Start → Problem → Action → Result.
