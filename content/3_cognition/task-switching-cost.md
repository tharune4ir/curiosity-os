## The Invisible Rule
Switching tasks creates “switch costs” (slower time, more errors). Heavy media multitaskers can be more distracted by irrelevant info

## The Matrix Trap
You do 2 minutes of physics, then 2 minutes of chatting, then back. After 1 hour, you covered almost nothing.

## The Escape Hatch
Switch only at checkpoints: finish a small unit, then switch. Planned switching is okay; random switching is the real enemy

## The Superpower
You gain “extra time” every day without waking up earlier.

## The Level Up Action
Before you start: decide your next switch time (example: after 25 minutes). Don’t switch earlier.
