## The Invisible Rule
Learning-by-teaching can work even with a fictitious peer (like recording an explanation). What matters is that you create and organize an explanation

## The Matrix Trap
You say, “I have nobody to teach, so I can’t use this technique.”

## The Escape Hatch
Teach your wall, teddy, or camera. Record a 90-second explanation. If you stammer, that’s your exact weak point.

## The Superpower
You build strong understanding without needing a tutor or group study.

## The Level Up Action
Record ONE 60-second voice note explaining today’s concept in simple words.
