## The Invisible Rule
Testing improves later retention, not just grades. Classroom studies also show quizzing can improve learning over time

## The Matrix Trap
You avoid quizzes because they feel scary, so you only read and feel safe.

## The Escape Hatch
Do low-stakes quizzes: tiny, frequent, no pressure. Wrong answers are data, not shame

## The Superpower
You become that student who stays calm in exams because you’ve ‘been there’ many times.

## The Level Up Action
Make 5 questions from today’s topic and answer them without looking.
