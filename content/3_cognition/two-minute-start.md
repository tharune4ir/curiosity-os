## The Invisible Rule
Starting improves when you pre-plan the exact moment and action (if-then planning). Tiny actions reduce the mental barrier to begin

## The Matrix Trap
You think you need 2 hours to start, so you never start.

## The Escape Hatch
Make it stupidly small: open the notebook, write the date, and solve just Step 1. Once moving, continuing is easier.

## The Superpower
You become the student who starts early and wins over time.

## The Level Up Action
Set a 2-minute timer. Start your task until the timer ends. Then decide if you want to continue (most people continue).
