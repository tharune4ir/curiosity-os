## The Invisible Rule
Research on noise is mixed, but some work finds small attention benefits from certain noise conditions, especially for people with ADHD or high ADHD symptoms; for others, benefits may be small or none

## The Matrix Trap
You try to study in a noisy house and keep getting pulled away by small sounds and conversations.

## The Escape Hatch
Use low-volume white/brown noise to mask sudden distractions. If it makes you sleepy or irritated, stop—your brain decides.

## The Superpower
You can focus even in imperfect environments (hostel, joint family home) without fighting every sound.

## The Level Up Action
Test it: 15 minutes with low brown noise while doing maths. If you feel worse, switch it off.
