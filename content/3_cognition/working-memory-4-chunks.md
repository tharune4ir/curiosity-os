## The Invisible Rule
Most people can hold only a few “chunks” in mind at once (around 4). If you try to juggle 10 pieces, you drop them

## The Matrix Trap
You read a solution and think, “Yes yes I get it,” but when you try alone, you forget Step 2 and Step 3 instantly.

## The Escape Hatch
Turn many pieces into fewer chunks: give a name to a group. Like in PUBG: “loot → rotate → fight.” Now your brain holds 3 chunks, not 30 items

## The Superpower
You become faster in maths/science because your brain doesn’t overflow mid-problem.

## The Level Up Action
Take any 10-line answer. Group it into 3 headings and name each heading in 1 word.
