## The Invisible Rule
Writing-to-learn research shows writing can support understanding and engagement, and meta-analytic work finds small positive effects on achievement

## The Matrix Trap
You keep everything in your head. It feels ‘understood’ until you try to write answers and nothing comes.

## The Escape Hatch
Do tiny writing: explain the concept, list steps, make a mini-argument. Writing forces clear structure, not fuzzy thinking.

## The Superpower
Your answers become sharper, longer, and clearer—exactly what board exams reward.

## The Level Up Action
Write a 5-sentence explanation of one concept without looking at the book.
