## The Invisible Rule
Zettelkasten is a method of building a personal slip-box of ideas where notes are small and linkable, so you can grow knowledge like a network, not a pile

## The Matrix Trap
One huge notebook becomes a graveyard: you wrote notes once, then never used them again.

## The Escape Hatch
Make atomic notes: ONE idea, in YOUR words, plus one example. This makes review and linking easy.

## The Superpower
Your notes become a ‘second brain’ you can actually use for revision, essays, and understanding.

## The Level Up Action
Create ONE atomic note today: title + 3 lines + 1 example.
