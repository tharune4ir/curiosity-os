## The Core Joy
Your brain loves small safe risks. Climbing, balancing, jumping-your body says 'I survived!' and confidence grows.

## The Memory Maker
You try the monkey bars at the park. First you slip. Then you figure it out. When you reach the end, you feel like you beat a mini-boss.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build courage, body trust, and risk judgment (knowing what's safe and what's not).

## The Matrix Trap
Chasing fake adrenaline with shocking videos that only stress your brain.

## The Weekend Mission
Do a 'playground circuit' (safe): 3 rounds of climb, hang, balance, jump. Stop if it hurts. Bring a friend to cheer.
