## The Core Joy
Cycling gives you control + speed. Your body moves, your brain wakes up, and the world feels bigger-like you unlocked a new map area.

## The Memory Maker
Late afternoon. You ride with a friend to the park, racing over speed breakers, laughing when your uniform gets dusty, feeling like the main character.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build stamina, courage, and real independence (planning routes, handling tiny problems, staying calm).

## The Matrix Trap
Playing 'open world' games for hours but never feeling the wind or learning real-world confidence.

## The Weekend Mission
With permission, plan a safe 20-40 minute cycle loop. Rule: one new lane/park you've never explored, and one water break.
