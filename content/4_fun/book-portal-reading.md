## The Core Joy
Good stories give you 'safe adventure.' Reading can reduce stress and also grows your brain's imagination and empathy.

## The Memory Maker
Exam holiday. Fan on full speed. You read one chapter and forget time. Suddenly it's dark outside and you feel peaceful, not drained.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build attention span, vocabulary, and empathy-skills that help in school and friendships.

## The Matrix Trap
Jumping between 10 short clips, never sinking into one deep story, and feeling scattered.

## The Weekend Mission
Pick one book/comic/story. Read 20 minutes daily for 3 days. After reading, write one line: 'Best part was...'
