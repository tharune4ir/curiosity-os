## The Core Joy
Boredom is your brain saying, 'I want new stimulation.' If you don't feed it with screens, your mind starts creating its own ideas.

## The Memory Maker
Power cut. No Wi-Fi. You stare at the ceiling, then suddenly you get a cool idea for a comic, a prank, or a new way to study.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build creativity and problem solving through mind-wandering-your brain connects dots in the background.

## The Matrix Trap
Killing every empty second with scrolling, so your brain never has space to create.

## The Weekend Mission
Do a 'boredom challenge': 10 minutes with no phone and no task. Then write down 5 random ideas that appear.
