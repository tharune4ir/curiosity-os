## The Core Joy
After you move hard, your body wants to settle. Slow breathing tells your nervous system: 'We are safe.' That calm feels like a reward.

## The Memory Maker
Match ends. You sit on the boundary wall, drink water, breathe slowly, and feel your heart go from 'drum solo' to 'peace mode.'

## The Hidden Upgrade
You learn emotion regulation: how to come down from anger, stress, or exam panic using your own breath.

## The Matrix Trap
Jumping from high-energy content to more high-energy content, never letting your brain settle.

## The Weekend Mission
After any play, do 6 slow breaths: inhale 4 counts, exhale 6 counts. Notice how your mood changes.
