## The Core Joy
Designing rules gives you god-mode creativity. Your brain loves systems: if you create the rules, you feel smart and powerful.

## The Memory Maker
A4 paper, markers, coins as tokens. You make 'School Escape' where you must dodge homework monsters. Everyone argues about rules like real game designers.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You learn logic, fairness, and how to explain ideas clearly so others can play.

## The Matrix Trap
Buying skins/upgrades in games that don't change your real-world skills at all.

## The Weekend Mission
Design a simple board game with 12 squares and 6 action cards. Test it with family for 15 minutes and fix one rule.
