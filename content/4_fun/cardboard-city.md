## The Core Joy
Making something with your hands turns ideas into reality. Your brain feels powerful: 'I can create, not just consume.'

## The Memory Maker
You collect Amazon boxes, tape, and markers. In two hours you have a tiny city: roads, a 'tea stall', a stadium, and secret tunnels.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build creativity, problem solving, and patience. Also: you learn to start messy and improve later.

## The Matrix Trap
Scrolling perfect DIY videos and never starting because your first version won't look 'Instagram-ready.'

## The Weekend Mission
Build ONE thing from waste: a mini city, a pen stand, or a phone holder. Rule: no buying materials-only what’s at home.
