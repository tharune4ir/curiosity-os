## The Core Joy
Carrom is focus + mini victory. Small wins release a 'yes!' feeling, and the calm competition keeps everyone close without shouting.

## The Memory Maker
Power cut night. Candle on the side. You flick the striker, the queen miraculously drops, and the whole family goes 'OHHHH!' together.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build patience, hand-eye control, and calm confidence. Also: you learn to win without showing off.

## The Matrix Trap
Hyper-fast mobile games that give fake wins every 10 seconds, then leave you restless and irritated.

## The Weekend Mission
Teach someone younger (or older) one carrom trick. Then play best-of-3 with a 'good game' handshake at the end.
