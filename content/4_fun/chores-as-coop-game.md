## The Core Joy
When home work becomes teamwork, it stops feeling like punishment. Shared effort creates belonging-and the home mood becomes lighter.

## The Memory Maker
You and sibling wash dishes with a timer. One dries, one washes. You argue about soap, then laugh, then finish faster than expected.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build responsibility and teamwork. Bonus: parents trust you more, which often means more freedom later.

## The Matrix Trap
Escaping chores by going online, then still getting scolded and feeling trapped.

## The Weekend Mission
Choose one chore and do it co-op with music for 15 minutes. End by cleaning the 'battlefield' (the mess).
