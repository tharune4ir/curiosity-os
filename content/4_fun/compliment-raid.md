## The Core Joy
Making someone feel seen gives you social power in a good way. Kindness boosts connection, and connection boosts joy.

## The Memory Maker
Your friend is quiet after a bad test. You say, 'Bro, you always help everyone. That's rare.' Their face changes like a light turned on.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build empathy, leadership, and the ability to create a 'safe squad' where people relax and be real.

## The Matrix Trap
Fishing for compliments online, refreshing notifications, and feeling worse when nothing comes.

## The Weekend Mission
Send 3 real compliments in 24 hours: one to a friend, one to a sibling/cousin, one to a parent/teacher. Make it specific, not generic.
