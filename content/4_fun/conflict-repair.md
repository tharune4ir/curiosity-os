## The Core Joy
Fights happen in real groups. Repairing after conflict feels good because your brain loves safety and trust returning. It's like fixing a crack before it spreads.

## The Memory Maker
You snapped at a friend during a match. Later you message: 'Sorry, I went too far. Are we good?' They reply with a joke, and the tension melts.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You learn apology skills, boundary respect, and emotional control-super useful for friendships, family, and future relationships.

## The Matrix Trap
Silent unfollowing, ghosting, or posting indirect statuses instead of solving the real issue.

## The Weekend Mission
Pick one small conflict (even at home). Do a 3-step patch: (1) name it, (2) say sorry for your part, (3) ask what would help.
