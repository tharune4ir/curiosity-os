## The Core Joy
Giving a handmade thing feels good because it creates connection. Your brain gets joy from making someone else smile.

## The Memory Maker
You make a small card for your mom, or a friendship bracelet for your cousin. They keep it in a drawer like treasure.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build empathy, generosity, and confidence in your ability to bring joy to others.

## The Matrix Trap
Buying random stuff online to feel happy, then feeling guilty or empty later.

## The Weekend Mission
Make a tiny gift with zero money: a note, DIY bookmark, tiny drawing, or snack plate. Deliver it like a secret agent.
