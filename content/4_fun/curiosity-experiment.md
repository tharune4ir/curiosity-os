## The Core Joy
Experiments feel fun because your brain loves 'what will happen?' Curiosity is a natural happiness engine-like opening loot boxes in real life (but healthier).

## The Memory Maker
You test what floats in water, or make a simple paper bridge strong enough for coins. When it works, you feel like a wizard.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build scientific thinking: observe, test, improve. This makes school learning easier too.

## The Matrix Trap
Watching 'satisfying experiments' videos without ever touching or trying anything yourself.

## The Weekend Mission
Do one safe experiment: paper airplane contest, coin bridge, or vinegar+baking soda in a bottle (with adult permission). Write what you expected vs what happened.
