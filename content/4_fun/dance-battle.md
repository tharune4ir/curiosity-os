## The Core Joy
Dancing is joy because it mixes rhythm, expression, and social energy. Your body gets to 'speak' without perfect words.

## The Memory Maker
Family function. Someone plays a Bollywood hit. You and cousins do one dumb move, it becomes a signature, and everyone copies it.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build confidence, body comfort, and emotional release. Also: you become the person who can lift the room's mood.

## The Matrix Trap
Watching dance reels and judging yourself instead of letting your body have fun.

## The Weekend Mission
Create a 30-second 'signature move' with a friend or sibling. Record it if you want, but the goal is laughter, not perfection.
