## The Core Joy
Turning real moments into comics turns embarrassment into comedy. Your brain feels control: 'I can rewrite the story.'

## The Memory Maker
You draw a 4-panel comic about tuition drama or sibling fights. Your friend reads it and laughs so hard they snort.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build storytelling, humor, and emotional resilience-because you learn to laugh at problems.

## The Matrix Trap
Only reposting memes made by strangers, slowly losing your own creativity voice.

## The Weekend Mission
Make one 4-panel comic from a real event this week. Stick figures are allowed. Bonus: give it a title like a movie.
