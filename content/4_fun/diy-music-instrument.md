## The Core Joy
Making rhythm together syncs people. Even alone, tapping a beat gives your brain a steady, calming pattern.

## The Memory Maker
You fill a dabba with rice to make a shaker. Someone uses steel plates like a drum. Suddenly the kitchen becomes a concert.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build timing, confidence, and group energy skills (starting, stopping, listening).

## The Matrix Trap
Only consuming music with no output-like eating without ever cooking.

## The Weekend Mission
Build 1 instrument (shaker, box drum, spoon claps). Teach 1 beat to someone. Do a 60-second jam.
