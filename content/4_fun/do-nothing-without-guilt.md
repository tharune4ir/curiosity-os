## The Core Joy
Real rest feels good because your brain finally stops fighting. Rest is not laziness; it's recovery-like charging your phone so it can work again.

## The Memory Maker
You just sit near the window with chai and do nothing for 7 minutes. No music, no scrolling. Your shoulders drop and your mind unclenches.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build self-trust: 'I don't need constant stimulation to be okay.' That's a superpower against stress.

## The Matrix Trap
'Fake rest' where you scroll for an hour and still feel tired and more noisy inside.

## The Weekend Mission
Try a 7-minute 'nothing break' once a day for 3 days. Set a timer. If thoughts come, let them pass like clouds.
