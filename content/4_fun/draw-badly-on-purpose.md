## The Core Joy
When you allow yourself to be bad, you become free. Freedom is fun. Your brain stops judging and starts playing.

## The Memory Maker
You draw your friend as a potato with glasses. Everyone laughs. Then they draw you as a flying chai cup. It becomes a wall of terrible legends.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build confidence, creativity, and the habit of trying-without waiting to be 'talented.'

## The Matrix Trap
Comparing your normal life to perfect creator content and feeling 'not enough.'

## The Weekend Mission
Do a 10-minute 'ugly art challenge': draw 5 things as badly as possible (fan, auto, teacher, dog, your own face). Share with one safe person.
