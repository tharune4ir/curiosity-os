## The Core Joy
Exploring makes your brain release 'curiosity energy.' New streets and tiny discoveries feel rewarding because novelty makes memories stronger.

## The Memory Maker
You find a shortcut lane, a quiet temple tree, and a new stationery shop. Suddenly your 'boring area' feels like a hidden level.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build observation, navigation, and confidence to move through the world (not just through screens).

## The Matrix Trap
Endless travel vlogs that make you feel like you traveled, but you never left your chair.

## The Weekend Mission
Make a 'local map' walk: 30 minutes, phone only for safety. Find 3 things you never noticed and name them like game locations.
