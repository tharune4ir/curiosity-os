## The Core Joy
When family plays, the hierarchy softens. For a moment, everyone is just players, and laughter rewires the home mood.

## The Memory Maker
You convince parents to play cards or carrom. Dad gets too competitive. Mom makes a comeback. You realize they are funny humans, not only rule-machines.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build closeness and communication. Also: you practice handling authority figures in a friendly way.

## The Matrix Trap
Only doing 'parallel play': everyone near each other but each person trapped in their own screen world.

## The Weekend Mission
Pick one easy game and invite family for 20 minutes. Your job: be the host (rules, fairness, snacks).
