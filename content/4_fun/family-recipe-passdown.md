## The Core Joy
Food is love you can taste. Cooking a family recipe connects you to home and builds comfort + pride at the same time.

## The Memory Maker
You learn to make your dad's chai style or your mom's poha/upma tricks. You feel like you unlocked a family secret.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build life skills, patience, and teamwork. Also: you start respecting how much effort 'simple food' takes.

## The Matrix Trap
Comparing your life to perfect influencer photos and feeling like your real life is 'too normal.'

## The Weekend Mission
Pick ONE family recipe and learn one step (not whole dish). Example: cutting onions safely, tempering, or making chutney. Ask for tips like a student.
