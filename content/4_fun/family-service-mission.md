## The Core Joy
Helping others creates 'earned happiness.' Your brain feels meaning: 'I mattered today.' That kind of joy lasts longer than quick entertainment.

## The Memory Maker
You help a neighbor carry groceries, teach a kid a topic, or clean a small shared space. People smile at you like you upgraded as a human.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build purpose, social courage, and pride that doesn't depend on likes or grades.

## The Matrix Trap
Watching 'inspiration' videos and feeling emotional, but doing nothing in real life.

## The Weekend Mission
Do one service mission with family permission: help at home extra, assist a neighbor, or donate old books/clothes. Keep it simple and real.
