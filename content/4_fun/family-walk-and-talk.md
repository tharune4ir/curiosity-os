## The Core Joy
Walking side-by-side makes talking easier. No eye contact pressure, just movement and shared scenery-perfect for real conversation.

## The Memory Maker
Evening walk after dinner. You point at random dogs, buy a small ice cream, and suddenly your parent tells you a funny story from their childhood.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build connection and trust with family, which becomes a safe base when life gets stressful.

## The Matrix Trap
Sitting in the same house but everyone separated by screens and silence.

## The Weekend Mission
Invite one parent/guardian for a 15-minute walk. Your mission: ask them ONE question about their teenage life.
