## The Core Joy
Rituals feel joyful because they create meaning and togetherness. Doing rituals with your hands makes it real, not just 'watching.'

## The Memory Maker
You help make rangoli, tie toran, light diyas, or set up a small puja corner. Everyone is busy, and the house feels alive.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build identity, belonging, and a sense of 'I know where I come from.' That confidence is quiet but strong.

## The Matrix Trap
Only watching festival content online, then feeling FOMO while doing nothing at home.

## The Weekend Mission
Create one tiny home ritual this week: chai-on-terrace, diya at window, rangoli sticker art, or cleaning one corner with music.
