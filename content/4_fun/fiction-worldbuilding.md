## The Core Joy
Imagination is a safe playground. Building a world lets your brain explore without real danger, and it feels deeply satisfying.

## The Memory Maker
You create a city where exams are illegal, or a cricket academy on Mars. You name streets, heroes, and villains like a game designer.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build creativity, problem solving, and emotional processing (your brain quietly sorts feelings through stories).

## The Matrix Trap
Only consuming other people's worlds and never practicing making your own.

## The Weekend Mission
Build one world page: 1 map, 3 rules, 2 characters, 1 conflict. Keep it messy. Show nobody if you don't want.
