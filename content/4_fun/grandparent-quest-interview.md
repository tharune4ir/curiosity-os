## The Core Joy
Humans love stories and belonging. When you learn your family history, you feel rooted-like you're part of a bigger team across time.

## The Memory Maker
You ask Nani/Nana: 'What games did you play at 14?' They tell you wild stories about school, bus rides, and old festivals. You realize they were teenagers too.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build empathy, listening skills, and confidence talking to adults. Also: you collect life wisdom without boring lectures.

## The Matrix Trap
Ignoring family in the room while you watch strangers' lives online.

## The Weekend Mission
Ask 5 questions to one elder: best friend, biggest trouble, favorite food, one fear, one proud moment. Record notes and share one story to someone else.
