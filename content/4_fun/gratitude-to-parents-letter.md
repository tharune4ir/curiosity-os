## The Core Joy
Gratitude creates warmth because it strengthens bonds. Saying 'I see you' makes relationships safer and happier for both sides.

## The Memory Maker
You write one small note: 'Thanks for waking me up for school every day.' Your parent reads it, pretends it's nothing, but you can tell it hit the heart.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build emotional maturity and the skill of strengthening relationships on purpose, not by accident.

## The Matrix Trap
Only expressing feelings through status posts and hoping someone guesses it's about them.

## The Weekend Mission
Write a 3-sentence gratitude note to one parent/guardian/elder. Hide it in a book, wallet, or tiffin bag like a secret loot drop.
