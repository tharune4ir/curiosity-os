## The Core Joy
Photos lock a moment. Your brain loves meaning and nostalgia, so 'capturing the squad' turns normal days into 'this mattered.'

## The Memory Maker
After a colony match, everyone is sweaty and happy. You take a silly photo with the bat like a trophy. Next month you see it and instantly smile.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You train gratitude and memory-building. You also learn to notice good moments while they are happening.

## The Matrix Trap
Posting for strangers' likes, deleting if it doesn't perform, and forgetting the real fun that happened.

## The Weekend Mission
Make a 'time capsule' album: 10 photos only. Rule: no filters-needed, just real moments (friends, family, terrace, street).
