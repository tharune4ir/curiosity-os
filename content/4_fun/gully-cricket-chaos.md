## The Core Joy
Team games mix two happiness fuels: belonging (tribe) and challenge (skills). Every ball gives instant feedback, so your brain stays fully awake.

## The Memory Maker
Evening in the lane. One slipper is the crease. The 'six' hits aunty's gate, everyone freezes, then runs, then laughs like it's a movie scene.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You learn teamwork, leadership, fairness, and how to handle pressure when everyone is watching you bat.

## The Matrix Trap
Watching match highlights for hours and feeling like you played, even though your body never moved.

## The Weekend Mission
Organize a 20-over (or 20-minutes) gully match. Make clear rules: safe bowling, respect neighbors, and stop if someone is annoyed.
