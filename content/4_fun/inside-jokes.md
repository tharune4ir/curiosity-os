## The Core Joy
Inside jokes feel like a secret badge: 'We get each other.' Your brain reads that as safety + belonging, so it feels warm and powerful.

## The Memory Maker
In class group chat, one silly typo becomes a legendary meme. Two weeks later, someone says one word and your whole squad starts giggling for no reason.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build friendship glue, group identity, and the skill of making people feel included (not excluded).

## The Matrix Trap
Spamming random memes to strangers for likes, but feeling empty because no one really knows you.

## The Weekend Mission
Create ONE wholesome inside joke: a funny codeword for 'let's meet' or 'I need backup.' Make sure everyone understands it and no one gets targeted.
