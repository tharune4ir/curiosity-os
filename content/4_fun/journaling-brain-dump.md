## The Core Joy
Writing clears mental clutter. When thoughts leave your head and land on paper, your brain feels lighter, like closing 20 tabs.

## The Memory Maker
Night before school. You feel stressed. You write: 'What I'm worried about' and 'What I can do.' After 5 minutes, your chest feels less tight.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build self-awareness and problem solving. You learn to talk to yourself like a supportive coach.

## The Matrix Trap
Overthinking in circles while scrolling, getting more anxious and doing nothing to solve it.

## The Weekend Mission
Do a 5-minute brain dump: (1) mood today, (2) one win, (3) one worry, (4) one tiny next step. Keep it private.
