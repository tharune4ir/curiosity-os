## The Core Joy
Savoring is when you replay a good moment on purpose. Your brain learns: 'Good things exist.' That boosts joy and resilience.

## The Memory Maker
You remember your friend's laugh from the match and you smile again. The day feels less heavy, even if homework is waiting.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build gratitude and emotional strength. It helps you not get trapped in only negative thinking.

## The Matrix Trap
Replaying cringe moments and comparing yourself to others, until your mood drops.

## The Weekend Mission
Each night for 3 nights, write 1 good moment from the day and relive it for 10 seconds in your mind like a highlight clip.
