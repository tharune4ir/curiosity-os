## The Core Joy
Kites mix skill, wind-reading, and suspense. You are literally playing with the sky, which feels epic even from a terrace.

## The Memory Maker
Makar Sankranti vibes (or any windy day). You shout 'kati!' as threads cross, and the whole terrace becomes a stadium.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build patience, focus, and quick reactions. Also: you learn to enjoy waiting, not just clicking.

## The Matrix Trap
Watching other people's 'epic wins' and feeling jealous instead of building your own.

## The Weekend Mission
If you have a kite: fly for 30 minutes with safety (watch wires). If not: make a paper glider and run 20 throws, improving each time.
