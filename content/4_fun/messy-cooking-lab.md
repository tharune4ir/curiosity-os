## The Core Joy
Cooking experiments hit all senses-smell, taste, heat, sound. When you create food, your brain feels pride plus comfort.

## The Memory Maker
You try 'Maggi but spicy-chaat style' or 'bread pizza.' It's not perfect, but everyone steals bites and gives reviews like judges.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You learn planning, patience, and confidence. Also: you learn to clean up your own chaos (rare hero skill).

## The Matrix Trap
Ordering junk while watching mukbang, then feeling heavy and bored.

## The Weekend Mission
Cook one small thing with permission: lemonade, egg bhurji, toast sandwich, or fruit chaat. Rule: you must plate it like a restaurant.
