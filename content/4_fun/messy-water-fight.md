## The Core Joy
Water play is pure sensory fun. It's safe chaos, and laughter in a group boosts bonding fast.

## The Memory Maker
Summer afternoon. Bucket, mugs, maybe a small pichkari. Everyone is wet, the floor is a lake, and the only rule is: no targeting eyes.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build playfulness, stress release, and the skill of setting safety rules while still having fun.

## The Matrix Trap
Watching prank videos where people get humiliated, then feeling gross and still bored.

## The Weekend Mission
Plan a 10-minute water game with family/cousins. Make 3 safety rules first. End with a towel race and chai/juice.
