## The Core Joy
Breathing slowly tells your body, 'No danger right now.' That creates calm. Calm is not boring-it's the base that makes fun and focus possible.

## The Memory Maker
You're angry after a scolding. You step aside, do slow breaths, and feel the anger cool down like hot tea becoming drinkable.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build self-control. This helps with exam stress, fights, and even stage fear.

## The Matrix Trap
Trying to 'calm down' with more stimulation-more videos, more noise, more chaos.

## The Weekend Mission
Try the '4-6' breath: inhale 4 counts, exhale 6 counts, 6 times. Do it once after school, once before sleep.
