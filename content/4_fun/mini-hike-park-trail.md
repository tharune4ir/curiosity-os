## The Core Joy
Nature gives your attention a break. Walking with trees and open sky can restore focus and reduce stress, like rebooting your brain.

## The Memory Maker
You walk in a nearby park, notice ants carrying food, smell leaves, and suddenly your mind feels less crowded.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build mental clarity, patience, and the habit of using movement to reset your mood.

## The Matrix Trap
Sitting all day, then trying to 'relax' with more screen time and feeling even more full in the head.

## The Weekend Mission
Go to the greenest place you can reach safely. Walk 20 minutes. No headphones for the first 5 minutes-listen to the world.
