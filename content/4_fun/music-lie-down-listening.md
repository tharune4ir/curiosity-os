## The Core Joy
Music can shift emotions fast. Lying down and listening deeply turns it into a mini-reset, not just background noise.

## The Memory Maker
You put one song, close eyes, and imagine a movie scene. For 3 minutes, life feels softer and your mind stops racing.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build emotional regulation: choosing music to calm, energize, or process feelings without drama.

## The Matrix Trap
Playing random autoplay for hours while multitasking and still feeling empty.

## The Weekend Mission
Pick 3 songs: calm, hype, comfort. Try one per day. After the song, name your emotion in one word.
