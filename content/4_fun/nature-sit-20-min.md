## The Core Joy
Nature is 'softly interesting.' It holds your attention without forcing it. That helps your brain recover from heavy focus and stress.

## The Memory Maker
You sit near a tree or on the terrace with plants. You watch leaves move and birds hop. After 10 minutes, your mind feels cleaner.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build focus recovery and calm. This can improve attention for studying later.

## The Matrix Trap
Trying to rest with short videos that keep pulling your attention every 5 seconds.

## The Weekend Mission
Do a 20-minute nature sit this week (park, balcony plants, terrace). Rule: no phone. If bored, just count leaves or sounds.
