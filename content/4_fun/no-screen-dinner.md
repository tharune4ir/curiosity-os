## The Core Joy
Shared meals create connection and emotional safety. Talking while eating turns a normal day into a small 'we are together' ritual.

## The Memory Maker
Dinner table. No TV. Someone tells a school story, someone laughs, someone complains about onions. It's messy, real, and weirdly comforting.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build conversation skills, family trust, and a calmer nervous system at home.

## The Matrix Trap
Eating alone with videos, then feeling like the day disappeared without any real moment.

## The Weekend Mission
Do one meal with a 'phone basket' rule for 15 minutes. Ask one fun question: 'If our family was a movie, what genre?'
