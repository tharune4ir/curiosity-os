## The Core Joy
Looking at old photos creates gratitude and connection. Your brain stitches past and present into one story: 'I belong here.'

## The Memory Maker
You open an old album. You see your parents in school uniforms. Everyone laughs at haircuts. Then someone tells the story behind the photo.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build perspective and empathy. You also learn that everyone had awkward phases-and survived.

## The Matrix Trap
Comparing your life to perfect influencer photos and feeling like your real life is 'too normal.'

## The Weekend Mission
Spend 20 minutes with family photos (digital or paper). Pick 3 photos and write 1 line each: where, who, what funny detail.
