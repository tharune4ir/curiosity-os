## The Core Joy
Safe messiness feels amazing. Rain changes the world's texture, smell, and sound-your brain loves that sensory reset.

## The Memory Maker
First rain after summer. You step outside, feel the cool drops, jump over small puddles, and come home smelling like wet mitti.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build flexibility: enjoying imperfect days instead of waiting for 'perfect' days. That's a happiness skill.

## The Matrix Trap
Watching rain 'ambience' videos while staying indoors and feeling more bored.

## The Weekend Mission
If it rains, do a 10-minute rain walk with slippers and permission. If not, do a 'water-splash mission' by washing the cycle/car with music.
