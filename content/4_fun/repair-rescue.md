## The Core Joy
Repair feels awesome because you reverse sadness. Your brain loves turning 'dead' into 'alive'-it feels like real power.

## The Memory Maker
A toy car wheel is loose. You find tape, glue, or a clip. It works again. Someone says, 'Wow, you fixed it!' Instant pride.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build problem-solving, patience, and responsibility. Also: you learn that many problems are fixable.

## The Matrix Trap
Throwing things away fast and buying new stuff, never learning the skill of saving.

## The Weekend Mission
Fix ONE small thing at home: a loose button, a wobbly chair screw, a broken pen. Ask permission, be safe, and clean up.
