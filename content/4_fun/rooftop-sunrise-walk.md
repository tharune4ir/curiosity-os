Early light + movement gives your brain a clean restart. Quiet mornings reduce noise, so you can actually hear your own thoughts.

You wake up before everyone, walk on the terrace, watch the sky turn orange, and feel strangely powerful-like you unlocked a secret time slot.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build self-control, calm focus, and a strong morning mood that can protect your whole day.

## The Matrix Trap
Staying up late on shorts, waking up groggy, and feeling like the day already beat you.

## The Weekend Mission
One day only: wake up 30 minutes earlier. No phone. Terrace walk + 5 minutes just staring at the sky.
