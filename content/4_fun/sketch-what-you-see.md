## The Core Joy
Sketching makes you slow down and notice details. Noticing is a joy skill-your brain starts seeing beauty in normal things.

## The Memory Maker
You draw your balcony plant, your school bottle, or the street dog sleeping. It looks messy, but you feel calm and present.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build attention, patience, and creative confidence. Also: you train your brain to focus without needing loud entertainment.

## The Matrix Trap
Watching aesthetic videos and feeling like life is only beautiful on other people's screens.

## The Weekend Mission
Sketch 3 'boring' objects in 15 minutes. Rule: no erasing. Write one funny caption under each drawing.
