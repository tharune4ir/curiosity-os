## The Core Joy
Learning a physical skill is satisfying because your brain loves progress. Tiny improvements feel like XP points.

## The Memory Maker
You practice football juggling or a cycle turn. Day 1: 3 tries. Day 3: 10 tries. You feel proud in a quiet, solid way.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build patience, grit, and confidence that comes from practice-not from praise.

## The Matrix Trap
Only consuming other people's highlight reels and feeling like you are 'behind' in life.

## The Weekend Mission
Pick one skill: skipping rope, juggling, dribble, or a dance step. Practice 8 minutes daily for 5 days. Track your 'high score.'
