## The Core Joy
A solo walk is freedom + calm. You get space from everyone's opinions, and your brain can reset and sort thoughts.

## The Memory Maker
You walk to a nearby shop alone, notice small details-smells, sounds, sky color-and feel grounded like you returned to yourself.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build independence, emotional clarity, and confidence being alone without feeling lonely.

## The Matrix Trap
Being alone physically but glued to a screen, still feeling anxious and restless.

## The Weekend Mission
With safety and permission, do a 15-minute solo walk in a safe area (or on terrace). No headphones for first 5 minutes. Observe 10 details.
