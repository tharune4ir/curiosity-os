## The Core Joy
Hard movement can flip your mood. After a good run, your body releases chemicals that reduce stress and can create a calm, proud glow.

## The Memory Maker
You and friends play pakda-pakdi in the park. You're dying, then suddenly you feel light, fast, and unstoppable for 30 seconds.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build stamina, stress relief skills, and the belief: 'I can push through discomfort and still smile.'

## The Matrix Trap
Doomscrolling when stressed, getting more tired, and still feeling anxious.

## The Weekend Mission
Do a 12-minute run/walk mix: 30 seconds run, 60 seconds walk. Finish with 2 deep breaths and water.
