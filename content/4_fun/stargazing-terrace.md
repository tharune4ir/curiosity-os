## The Core Joy
Looking at the night sky creates awe. Awe makes your problems feel smaller, not by denying them, but by zooming you out to a bigger picture.

## The Memory Maker
You lie on the terrace. One star moves-it's a plane. You spot Orion or just make your own constellations. The air feels quiet and infinite.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build calm, perspective, and curiosity. It's like mental stretching that makes life feel lighter.

## The Matrix Trap
Late-night scrolling until your eyes burn, then sleeping with a busy, over-stimulated brain.

## The Weekend Mission
One night this week: 10 minutes on the terrace/balcony. No phone. Count 20 stars (or as many as your city allows).
