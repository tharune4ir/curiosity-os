## The Core Joy
Making a movie teaches your brain long attention. You create movement out of stillness-like magic you control.

## The Memory Maker
You use toys or erasers as characters. 50 photos later, the 'eraser monster' walks. You and friends scream like you created Pixar.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build patience, planning, and teamwork. Also: you learn that boring steps create cool results.

## The Matrix Trap
Auto-playing endless videos where you never build anything that lasts.

## The Weekend Mission
Make a 10-second stop-motion: a coin 'walking', a paper plane 'flying', or snacks 'dancing'. Keep it simple and fun.
