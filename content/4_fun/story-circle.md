## The Core Joy
Stories create shared imagination. When your group imagines the same scene, it feels like you all stepped into one world together.

## The Memory Maker
Terrace evening. Power cut. Someone starts, 'Bro, the funniest thing happened in tuition...' and suddenly everyone is acting out the characters.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build communication, humor, confidence, and the skill of making boring moments entertaining.

## The Matrix Trap
Bingeing random stories alone until 2 a.m., then waking up tired and blank.

## The Weekend Mission
Start a 15-minute story circle: each person tells one 'small story' from the week (funny, awkward, or proud). No interrupting.
