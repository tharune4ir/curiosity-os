## The Core Joy
A tiny quest makes normal life feel like a story. Mixing movement, novelty, and friends turns your brain from 'routine mode' into 'adventure mode.'

## The Memory Maker
After exams, you and two friends walk to the market for pani puri or momos. You debate the best stall like it's a world championship and take one epic group selfie.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You learn planning, budgeting, social courage (talking to vendors), and how to enjoy simple things deeply.

## The Matrix Trap
Food reels + ASMR eating videos that make you hungry, jealous, and still stuck on the bed.

## The Weekend Mission
Plan a Rs 50-Rs 100 food quest with permission. Rule: you must walk (not ride) at least 15 minutes, and you must try one new item.
