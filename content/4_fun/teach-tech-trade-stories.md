## The Core Joy
Teaching someone makes you feel competent. And trading stories makes elders feel seen. Both sides win, like a perfect co-op trade.

## The Memory Maker
You help Dadi save a contact or use WhatsApp call. In return she tells you how she made friends without phones and what mischief they did.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build patience, communication, and respect. You also learn to explain stuff clearly-great for teaching anyone anything.

## The Matrix Trap
Calling elders 'outdated' and living in an online bubble where you only see your own age group.

## The Weekend Mission
Teach one simple tech skill to an elder (volume, torch, call). Then ask them to teach you one 'old school' skill or story.
