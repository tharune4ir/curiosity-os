## The Core Joy
Turning boring work into a game gives you agency. Your brain likes 'missions' with clear start and finish, especially with teammates.

## The Memory Maker
Saturday cleaning. You put on one song, set a 10-minute timer, and everyone races to fold clothes or arrange the bookshelf. Chaos, jokes, and a surprise dance break.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build discipline without feeling punished. You also learn coordination and how to lead without being bossy.

## The Matrix Trap
Watching productivity videos while your room stays the same and guilt grows bigger.

## The Weekend Mission
Pick ONE chore. Make it co-op: 2 people, 12 minutes, timer ON. Winner chooses the next snack or music.
