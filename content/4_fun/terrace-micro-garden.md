## The Core Joy
Growing something gives long-term joy. Your brain likes watching progress over days-it feels meaningful, not instant and empty.

## The Memory Maker
You plant methi or coriander in an old container. One week later, tiny green shoots appear and you feel weirdly proud like a parent.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build patience, care, and responsibility. Also: you learn that small daily actions create big change.

## The Matrix Trap
Chasing instant dopamine hits and then feeling bored again in 30 seconds.

## The Weekend Mission
Start a micro garden: 1 pot only. Use kitchen seeds (methi, mustard) or a small plant. Name it like a pet.
