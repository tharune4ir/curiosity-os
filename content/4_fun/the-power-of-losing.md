## The Core Joy
Real fun includes small losses. Your brain learns: 'I can feel bad, and still be okay.' That's emotional strength, like a character leveling up after defeat.

## The Memory Maker
You miss an easy catch. Everyone groans. You feel the heat in your face. Then someone says 'next ball!' and you try again... and later you laugh about it.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You learn resilience, sportsmanship, and self-control. This helps in exams too: one bad test doesn't end your story.

## The Matrix Trap
Only choosing content where you always 'win' instantly, so real life feels unbearable the moment it gets hard.

## The Weekend Mission
Play any game where you might lose. Your rule: if you lose, you must say 'GG' (good game) and ask for one tip to improve.
