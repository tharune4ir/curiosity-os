## The Core Joy
Useless inventions are secretly great because there is no pressure. Play + curiosity makes your brain explore new solutions.

## The Memory Maker
You make a 'biscuit lifter' from a spoon and rubber bands. It's silly. It works badly. Everyone laughs. You feel like a mad scientist.

## The Hidden Upgrade
You build innovation muscles: experimenting, failing safely, and not getting scared of mistakes.

## The Matrix Trap
Copy-paste hacks that look cool online but teach you nothing because you didn't think or test.

## The Weekend Mission
Invent something ridiculous for home life: a cool-bookmark, a cable organizer, or a snack tray. Bonus: give it a dramatic name.
