## The Cheat Code
Anchoring means relying too much on the first number or info you hear. That first ‘anchor’ stays in your mind and biases your decisions.

## The Casino Trap
If first salary offered is 50000, you adjust your expectations around it, even if you could get more.

## The Real World Hack
At a market, a shopkeeper first says 500 for an item. You bargain from there, even if fair price was 200.

## The Superpower
You question initial offers or info. You negotiate better by setting your own reference points.

## The Mission
Next time you hear a price or statistic, ask yourself if it’s just a starting point or the real value.
