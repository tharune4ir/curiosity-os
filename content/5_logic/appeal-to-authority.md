## The Cheat Code
Just because someone famous or an 'expert' says something doesn’t make it true. Check the facts, even from authorities.

## The Casino Trap
Trusting blindly can mislead you. A celebrity or official might recommend a product, but that doesn’t mean it works.

## The Real World Hack
A coach tells you to spend pocket money on supplements, but you research and see they’re useless without exercise. You didn’t just trust their word.

## The Superpower
You always seek evidence. You don’t get fooled by “everyone knows” and you become well-informed.

## The Mission
Question a fact from a popular figure. Research it yourself instead of accepting it blindly.
