## The Cheat Code
Just because we lack proof of something doesn’t prove it. Absence of evidence is not evidence of absence.

## The Casino Trap
Believing wild claims without proof, like "Ghosts exist because no one proved they don’t." You’re using lack of evidence as proof.

## The Real World Hack
If someone says "Aliens exist because you can’t prove they don’t," you know that’s not valid. You ask for actual evidence.

## The Superpower
You demand evidence before believing something. You don’t accept claims just because they’re not disproven.

## The Mission
Think of a claim you heard. Ask: "Is there actual proof? Or is it just not disproven?"
