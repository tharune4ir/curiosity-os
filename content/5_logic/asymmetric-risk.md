## The Cheat Code
Asymmetric risk is when the worst you lose is small but the best gain is huge. Think lottery or startup: you risk little, but could win big.

## The Casino Trap
Fear of small loss stops big wins. People avoid small bets with big prizes, ignoring huge upside. They choose little gain with big safety.

## The Real World Hack
You buy a 10 lottery ticket. You can only lose 10, but you could win 1 Crore. Big upside for little cost.

## The Superpower
You spot deals with high reward vs low cost. You take small risks that others avoid and often win big.

## The Mission
Find a contest or bet where the entry fee is low but prize is high. Even if chance is small, it’s worth the shot.
