## The Cheat Code
Availability bias is thinking events are more common because they are easier to recall. If something is big or reported a lot, you assume it happens often.

## The Casino Trap
You watch news of plane crashes and think flying is dangerous, ignoring that car accidents are much more frequent.

## The Real World Hack
You hear about a student winning a contest. You think prizes are easy, forgetting that most participants win nothing.

## The Superpower
You check actual data, not just headlines. You make decisions on facts, not just scary stories.

## The Mission
Name something scary (like a shark attack). Look up how often it happens each year compared to something safe (like bike accidents).
