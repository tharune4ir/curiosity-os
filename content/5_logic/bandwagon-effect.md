## The Cheat Code
Bandwagon effect is doing something just because others are. You follow the crowd without checking if it’s good for you.

## The Casino Trap
Peer pressure leads to bad choices. Just because everyone says a movie is great doesn’t mean you’ll like it. You might waste time and money.

## The Real World Hack
All kids in class buy the newest smartphone. You buy it too, but soon regret it because you didn’t really need it.

## The Superpower
You think for yourself and only follow trends you truly like. You avoid unnecessary risks or expenses from peer pressure.

## The Mission
Think of a trend you follow. Ask: 'Do I really want this, or just because others do?'
