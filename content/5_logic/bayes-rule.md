## The Cheat Code
Bayes' Rule shows how to update probabilities with new evidence. If something was unlikely, but you see a clue, you recalculate its chance.

## The Casino Trap
Ignoring evidence or being fooled by test accuracy. People often forget that rare things can still be unlikely even if test is positive.

## The Real World Hack
A disease test says 99% accurate. If only 1% of people have the disease, then even a positive test gives about 50% chance. You see how to update with base rate.

## The Superpower
You combine old and new info to see the real picture. You don’t get tricked by raw numbers without context.

## The Mission
If 1 out of 1000 students is a champion and you hear someone won a contest, think: what’s the chance they’re the champion? Update using new info.
