## The Cheat Code
Budgeting means planning how to use your money over time so you don’t run out. Track income and expenses and save for goals.

## The Casino Trap
Spending impulsively until money is gone. Many students waste pocket money and have none left for needs.

## The Real World Hack
You allocate 200 per week for snacks and 100 for savings. By tracking, you always have money for important needs.

## The Superpower
You know exactly where your money goes. You save for big things easily and avoid debt.

## The Mission
Make a simple budget for your next pocket money: list essentials, fun money, and savings.
