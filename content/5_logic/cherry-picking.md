## The Cheat Code
Cherry picking is choosing only the evidence that supports your view and ignoring the rest. It’s like showing only your best scores.

## The Casino Trap
You brag "I never failed any test!" but ignore the times you did fail. You pick only the positives.

## The Real World Hack
You only remember the times a friend said something nice, forgetting all the times they were mean.

## The Superpower
You look at all the data, not just highlights. You spot when someone only shows the good parts.

## The Mission
When someone makes a claim, ask for all the evidence, not just the good examples.
