## The Cheat Code
To find the chance of multiple independent events happening, multiply their probabilities. For example, flipping heads then heads (each 1/2) gives 1/2 * 1/2 = 1/4.

## The Casino Trap
Not multiplying, people often guess wrong. They might think two 50% chances are still 50%, but the math is 25%.

## The Real World Hack
You flip a coin and roll a die. Chance of heads AND a 6 is 1/2 * 1/6 = 1/12. You calculate combined chances.

## The Superpower
You calculate chances of multi-step events correctly. You won’t be fooled by games with several steps.

## The Mission
Flip a coin and roll a die. Calculate the chance of getting heads and a 6. Then try it to check your math.
