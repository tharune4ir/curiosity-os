## The Cheat Code
Conditional probability is the chance of something happening when you have extra information. For example, knowing today is Monday might change the chance it will rain later.

## The Casino Trap
Ignoring given info can mislead you. Eg: assuming any card draw has 1/4 chance of being hearts, even if someone already showed a heart.

## The Real World Hack
You have a 1% chance to win a random gift, but if you have a special coupon, it jumps to 50%. Knowing you have the coupon changes the odds.

## The Superpower
You update probabilities when you get new clues. You make sharper predictions using all info you have.

## The Mission
Imagine there are 100 balls, 50 red, 50 blue. If you know one random ball drawn is red, what’s the chance the next is red? Think how info changed the odds.
