## The Cheat Code
Confirmation bias means noticing only facts that match what you already believe and ignoring the rest. You end up only finding proof for your own ideas.

## The Casino Trap
If you only watch news that agrees with you, you never learn other perspectives and stay wrong.

## The Real World Hack
You think your friend is bad at sports, so you only remember the times they missed a shot, forgetting all the times they scored.

## The Superpower
You search for evidence on both sides. You become a balanced thinker and make fair judgments.

## The Mission
Take one belief you have. Find one fact that goes against it. Force yourself to consider it.
