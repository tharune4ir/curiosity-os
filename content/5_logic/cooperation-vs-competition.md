## The Cheat Code
Cooperation means working together to win. Competition means trying to beat others. Sometimes working with others gets a better result than fighting for yourself.

## The Casino Trap
Thinking you must always compete can hurt you. If everyone competes alone, few succeed. Team effort can give higher rewards for all.

## The Real World Hack
In a relay race, runners hand the baton to help win. If each ran only for personal glory, the team loses. By cooperating, everyone wins a medal.

## The Superpower
You know when to team up or go solo. You switch modes and get the best outcome by picking the right approach.

## The Mission
Choose a project where you can work with a classmate. Notice how cooperating changes the result.
