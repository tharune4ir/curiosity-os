## The Cheat Code
Coordination means working together so everyone’s actions match. When people coordinate, they all do better, like picking a meeting time everyone can make.

## The Casino Trap
If people fail to coordinate, they miss out. Imagine two classmates doing the same homework without talking: they do duplicate work and waste time.

## The Real World Hack
You and friends plan to meet at 5 PM for a movie. If everyone agrees and comes together, you all watch the movie happily. That’s coordination.

## The Superpower
You become great at arranging group plans or projects. Your team always succeeds because you make sure everyone does their part.

## The Mission
Try planning an activity with a friend. Make sure both know what to do and when. See how smooth it goes.
