## The Cheat Code
Just because two things happen together doesn’t mean one causes the other. They may just coincide or be caused by something else.

## The Casino Trap
You jump to conclusions. Eg: you see many students with new phones got A’s, so you think new phone made them smart, ignoring studying.

## The Real World Hack
When ice cream sales and sunburn cases rise together, it’s summer causing both, not ice cream causing sunburn.

## The Superpower
You find the real reasons behind patterns. You avoid blaming the wrong thing for an outcome.

## The Mission
Look at two events that happen together. Ask: 'Does one really cause the other, or is it coincidence?'
