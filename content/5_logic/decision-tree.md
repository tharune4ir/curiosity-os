## The Cheat Code
A decision tree is like drawing out your choices and what might happen after each. You map steps and results to choose the best path.

## The Casino Trap
Without a plan, you might randomly guess and feel confused. People forget to map consequences and waste time figuring out what to do.

## The Real World Hack
Before taking a new course, you write down: if I pass, then next step is this; if I fail, another step is that. This map helps decide what to do next.

## The Superpower
You make clear plans even when situations are complex. You never get surprised because you saw most outcomes beforehand.

## The Mission
For a simple choice (like studying vs playing), sketch what could happen with each choice on paper.
