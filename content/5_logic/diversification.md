## The Cheat Code
Diversification spreads risk. By having many smaller bets or plans instead of one, you protect from total loss. If one fails, others might succeed.

## The Casino Trap
Risking everything on one bet. Students do this by focusing only on one idea for career, ignoring other options.

## The Real World Hack
You apply to 3 college courses instead of just 1. Even if one application fails, others might succeed.

## The Superpower
You reduce risk by balancing choices. Even if one plan fails, others support you.

## The Mission
Find one goal and plan two different ways to achieve it, so if one path fails, you have another.
