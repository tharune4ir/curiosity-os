## The Cheat Code
A dominant strategy is a choice that gives the best outcome no matter what others do. It’s like always choosing your strongest card in a game.

## The Casino Trap
If you don’t see it, you might keep switching actions for no reason. If you do have it, sometimes people predict you.

## The Real World Hack
Studying could be your dominant strategy: it helps your score no matter what other students do. You always choose to study.

## The Superpower
You discover and use strategies that win in any situation. You play your best move every time.

## The Mission
Think of a choice where one option is clearly always best. Will you take it every time?
