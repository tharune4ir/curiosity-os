## The Cheat Code
Expected Value (EV) is a math trick to check if a gamble is worth it. You multiply each prize by its chance, add them up, then subtract the cost. If positive, it's a good bet in the long run.

## The Casino Trap
Buying a lottery ticket seems fun because the prize is huge. But the chance is tiny. For example, a 100 ticket with 1 in 1,000,000 chance to win 1 Crore has EV just 0.10. You lose 99.90 on average.

## The Real World Hack
You spend 30 minutes playing a game instead of studying. The 'prize' is fun time, but the 'cost' is risking your exam grade. The expected value of skipping study is negative, so you say no and study instead.

## The Superpower
You become like a poker champion: only take risks that math says are good deals, not just because they feel exciting.

## The Mission
Pick a choice (like skipping homework for fun). Write down the best and worst things that happen. Estimate how likely each is. Compare benefits vs costs.
