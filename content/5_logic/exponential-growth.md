## The Cheat Code
Exponential growth means something grows faster and faster. Like doubling each step: 1 becomes 2, then 4, 8, 16... It becomes huge quickly.

## The Casino Trap
We think linearly. A small 10% yearly growth sounds small, but over many years it becomes huge. People underestimate compounding.

## The Real World Hack
You save 100 in a bank with 10% interest. After 7 years, it’s nearly 200 because of compounding interest.

## The Superpower
You use compound benefits (like saving money or practicing skills steadily) to get big results later.

## The Mission
Try folding paper: each fold doubles thickness. Fold a few times and see how thick it gets (if you could!).
