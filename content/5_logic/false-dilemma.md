## The Cheat Code
False dilemma is thinking only two outcomes exist. There may be many options besides 'either/or'.

## The Casino Trap
You say "Either I pass or I fail this class" and ignore solutions like asking for help. You might think only two extremes exist.

## The Real World Hack
Someone says, "Either you study science or you’re a failure." You know you could also choose arts or commerce. Life isn’t just those two paths.

## The Superpower
You look for creative solutions. You find options others miss.

## The Mission
Find a problem with an "either/or" claim. Come up with a third choice.
