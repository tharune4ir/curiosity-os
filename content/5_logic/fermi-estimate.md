## The Cheat Code
Fermi estimation breaks big unknowns into smaller guesses. Estimate each part roughly and multiply/add. It’s like solving hard questions with easy math.

## The Casino Trap
We often say “I have no idea” without trying to guess. Missing a quick, useful estimate leaves us stuck.

## The Real World Hack
Estimating how many students in school: count one classroom and multiply by number of classes. You get a good ballpark without counting everyone.

## The Superpower
You confidently answer tough questions by breaking them down. You can estimate populations, costs, distances with simple math.

## The Mission
Estimate something: how many books are in your city’s library? Break it into shelves × books per shelf.
