## The Cheat Code
First-order thinking means only looking at the immediate result. You pick what seems best right now, without thinking about later consequences.

## The Casino Trap
People trick themselves by focusing on instant pleasure. For example, skipping exam study to play a video game sounds fun now, but leads to failure later.

## The Real World Hack
Imagine your friends invite you to party the night before exam. First-order thinking says 'fun now!' but second-order thinking warns of flunking.

## The Superpower
You catch yourself before making a short-sighted choice. You think long-term, like a chess player planning moves ahead.

## The Mission
Next time you make a quick decision, pause and ask: 'What happens tomorrow?'
