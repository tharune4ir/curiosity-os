## The Cheat Code
Gambler’s fallacy is believing that a random event is ‘due’ after a streak. But in fair games (like coin flips), past results don’t affect the next chance.

## The Casino Trap
After flipping heads 5 times, you think "tail is due". This is wrong for independent events. You might bet on the wrong outcome.

## The Real World Hack
On a cricket coin toss, if two tails came up, people might say "heads must come next". But it’s still 50-50 each toss.

## The Superpower
You trust math, not superstitions. You know each event is independent, so you make better choices.

## The Mission
Flip a coin 10 times. Notice each flip is 50% heads, regardless of previous flips.
