## The Cheat Code
Hindsight bias is thinking you predicted something after it happened. You say 'I knew it!' even if you didn’t.

## The Casino Trap
You feel overconfident after an event and don't learn from mistakes, because you think the outcome was obvious.

## The Real World Hack
After the cricket match, you say 'I knew they would win,' even though before the match you weren't sure. Recognizing this bias helps you prepare better next time.

## The Superpower
You remember your actual predictions. This helps you learn and not be fooled by past events.

## The Mission
Next time, write down your prediction before an event. After it happens, compare without changing your answer to 'I knew it.'
