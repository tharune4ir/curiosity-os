## The Cheat Code
Incentives are rewards or punishments that make us act. People usually do something if they get more good things than bad. Understanding them helps predict choices.

## The Casino Trap
If you ignore what someone wants, you get fooled. For example, you think your brother studies out of love for math, but he actually wants your parents’ praise (points for good grades).

## The Real World Hack
Your coach promises bonus practice time for good performance. You work harder because of that extra playtime. You spot that the promise (incentive) made you train.

## The Superpower
You can motivate others or yourself by using incentives. You become a leader who knows how to make people do the right thing.

## The Mission
Find out why someone works hard or not. Then try using a small reward or penalty to change an outcome.
