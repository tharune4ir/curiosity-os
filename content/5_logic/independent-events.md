## The Cheat Code
Independent events are unrelated: the result of one doesn’t change the next. Like flipping a coin twice: first flip doesn’t affect second flip.

## The Casino Trap
We think events are connected when they’re not. Eg: after five heads in a row, many think ‘tails is due’. This is the gambler’s fallacy.

## The Real World Hack
Your exam was easy last time, but you study just as hard next time, because each test is independent of the last.

## The Superpower
You make decisions based on actual odds, not mistaken patterns. You don’t let past independent events fool you.

## The Mission
Flip a coin twice. Notice that the chance of heads on the second flip is still 50%, no matter what happened first.
