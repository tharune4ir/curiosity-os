## The Cheat Code
Inflation means prices go up over time. 100 today buys more than 100 next year, because things get more expensive.

## The Casino Trap
Ignoring inflation, you might think 100 saved is always the same. Actually, if inflation is 5%, your money buys less after a year.

## The Real World Hack
A movie ticket cost 80 five years ago, but now it costs 120. You see how saving without interest lost purchasing power.

## The Superpower
You plan for the real cost of things. You save or invest more, knowing what money will buy later.

## The Mission
Check the price of something (like a samosa) from a few years ago and now. Notice how inflation changed it.
