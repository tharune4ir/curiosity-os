## The Cheat Code
Luck vs Skill means figuring out what we can control and what we can’t. In games or life, some outcomes are pure chance, others come from practice and effort.

## The Casino Trap
Confusing luck and skill fools people. A beginner wins the first match and thinks it’s talent. Or a pro loses one game and feels useless. Both misjudge random swings.

## The Real World Hack
You ace a test without studying, but know it was luck, not skill. Next time you study to get real skill. You give credit to preparation or pure chance correctly.

## The Superpower
You focus on improving what you can (skill) and accept random results as just chance, not personal failure.

## The Mission
Think of one success of yours. Ask yourself: did luck play a part or was it your hard work?
