## The Cheat Code
Minimax means picking the move that maximizes your minimum gain. You focus on the worst possible outcome and make it as good as possible.

## The Casino Trap
If you ignore worst-case scenarios, you might make risky decisions. For example, you might bet all your pocket money on one number and lose everything.

## The Real World Hack
You prepare backup notes for each subject. Even if you mess up one exam, you still can pass because of your safety net.

## The Superpower
You know how to protect yourself from disasters. You make choices that keep you safe even in the worst case.

## The Mission
Next time, think 'If everything goes wrong, what can I do to still be okay?'
