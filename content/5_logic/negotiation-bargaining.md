## The Cheat Code
Negotiation means talking to find a deal that both sides like. Good negotiators look for win-win, not trick others. It’s like haggling in a market to find a fair price.

## The Casino Trap
Some try to force one-sided deals, which leads to fights. For example, a sibling demands chores without reward, then the other refuses. No agreement happens.

## The Real World Hack
You want extra playtime. You promise to help with groceries. Your parents agree. You both win: you play more, they get help.

## The Superpower
You can make any deal better. You find solutions where both people walk away happy.

## The Mission
Negotiate with a friend or parent on something small. Try to find a solution where both of you gain.
