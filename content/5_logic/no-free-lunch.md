## The Cheat Code
No free lunch means you can’t get something for nothing. Everything has a cost, even if hidden.

## The Casino Trap
You see 'free' offers and ignore the catch. A free app might sell your data instead.

## The Real World Hack
A friend says "Join my event for free, it’s fun!" Then you find out they want your personal info. It wasn’t free after all.

## The Superpower
You always check what the real cost is. If something seems too good, you ask what you’re really paying.

## The Mission
Next time something is advertised as free, ask: "What am I really giving in return?"
