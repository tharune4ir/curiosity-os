## The Cheat Code
Opportunity cost is what you miss by choosing something else. Every choice has a hidden cost of the next best thing you didn’t do.

## The Casino Trap
Ignoring the real cost of time or money. Students skip studying for games, forgetting the future benefit (passing exam) they lose.

## The Real World Hack
If you spend 100 on candy, your opportunity cost is not buying a book you wanted. You lost that benefit.

## The Superpower
You make smarter choices by considering what you're missing. You pick options with the best overall benefit.

## The Mission
Think of a decision: list what you lose by picking one option over another (like playing a game vs studying).
