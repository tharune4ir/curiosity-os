## The Cheat Code
Outcome bias is judging decisions by results. A good plan can lead to a bad result by chance, and a bad plan can get lucky. Focus on the plan itself.

## The Casino Trap
You might praise a gambler for winning or blame for losing, ignoring strategy. People say "I knew it" without realizing luck was involved.

## The Real World Hack
You flip a coin to decide something and it works out. You think the coin is magic. But the result was luck, not a good decision.

## The Superpower
You evaluate actions by reasoning, not only outcome. You learn from decisions whether they succeed or fail.

## The Mission
Take a decision that had a good or bad result. Ask: was it because of luck or a strong plan?
