## The Cheat Code
Overconfidence is thinking you're more likely to succeed than reality. You believe too much in your own ability.

## The Casino Trap
You ignore risk. Thinking 'I’ll surely win this bet' can lead to big losses if you’re wrong.

## The Real World Hack
You refuse help on homework saying 'I got this.' Next day you struggle. Overconfidence hurt your grades.

## The Superpower
You stay grounded. You study enough and double-check plans, making you more reliable.

## The Mission
Before a test, predict your score. Compare with actual. See if you were too high or too low.
