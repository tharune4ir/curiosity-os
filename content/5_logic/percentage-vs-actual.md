## The Cheat Code
Percentages show relative size. To understand deals, convert them to real numbers. For example, 50% of 10 is 5 rupees; 50% of 100 is 50 rupees.

## The Casino Trap
Ignoring the base number. If two shops say "50% off", one on 100 item and one on 1000 item, the real savings (50 vs 500 rupees) differ a lot.

## The Real World Hack
A 200 shirt is 50% off (100). A 2000 phone is 50% off (1000). The big deal is on the phone, even though both say 50%.

## The Superpower
You get the true value of deals and stats. You won’t be fooled by big percentages hiding small bases.

## The Mission
See a discount or stat, and convert it to actual numbers to really understand it.
