## The Cheat Code
In a positive-sum game, the pie can grow. If you cooperate or share, everyone can get more. Working together creates more rewards than fighting over fixed shares.

## The Casino Trap
Some people think helping you means hurting me. For example, they keep all exam tips secret fearing others get smarter. This mindset prevents win-win deals.

## The Real World Hack
You and a friend start a side business selling snacks. By cooperating, you sell more than each selling alone. Both of you make extra money. Both win more than if you competed.

## The Superpower
You spot when cooperation will boost everyone's success. You become known as someone who creates win-win deals instead of fights.

## The Mission
Next time you face a conflict, suggest a solution where everyone wins a bit, like sharing study notes or splitting chores.
