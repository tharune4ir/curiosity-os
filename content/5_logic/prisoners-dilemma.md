## The Cheat Code
Prisoner's Dilemma is a game where two friends can either cooperate or betray. If both cooperate, each gets a moderate reward. If one betrays and the other cooperates, the betrayer wins big and the cooperator gets little. If both betray, both get small punishment.

## The Casino Trap
It feels safer to betray just in case the other cheats. But if both players think this and betray, they both lose out more than if they had cooperated.

## The Real World Hack
Imagine you and a classmate must confess for cheating together. If both stay silent, both of you just get a warning and keep studying. But if one lies and betrays the other, the liar goes free and the other gets in trouble.

## The Superpower
You learn to recognize when cooperation helps both people, even if betraying looks tempting. You avoid traps where everyone loses because they didn't trust each other.

## The Mission
Think of a situation with a friend where you both could either help or hurt each other, and decide what happens in each case.
