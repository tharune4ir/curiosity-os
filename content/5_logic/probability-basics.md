## The Cheat Code
Probability is a number between 0 and 1 (or 0% to 100%) that tells how likely something is. 1 means sure, 0 means impossible.

## The Casino Trap
Many mix up chance and outcome. They might say 50% on a quiz means they will definitely get half right. But 50% just means on average, half the time.

## The Real World Hack
You check the weather: 70% chance of rain means rain is likely but not certain. You take an umbrella because it might rain.

## The Superpower
You judge risks correctly: whether something is likely or not. You make smart choices using probabilities (like carrying umbrella if rain chance is high).

## The Mission
Think of something with chance (like drawing a colored marble). Write its probability and see if your expectation matches the result after trying.
