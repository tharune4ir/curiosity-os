## The Cheat Code
Ratios compare parts. If 3 friends share 12 sweets in ratio 1:2:3, they get 2, 4, and 6 sweets respectively. Proportions keep things fair.

## The Casino Trap
Misunderstanding equal division. You might split tasks or money unevenly without realizing who does more.

## The Real World Hack
Three siblings decide chores in ratio 1:1:2. First two do 1 chore each, third does 2. It’s fair by agreement.

## The Superpower
You solve sharing problems quickly. You split bills, candies, or tasks correctly for everyone.

## The Mission
If 4 friends buy a pizza and split the bill in ratio 1:1:2:2, calculate each share.
