## The Cheat Code
Recency bias means giving too much importance to recent events. The latest thing you saw or did seems the biggest deal.

## The Casino Trap
You judge performance by the last test only. Getting low marks recently makes you think you’ll fail always, ignoring past successes.

## The Real World Hack
Your team lost its last two matches, so you think they’re a bad team now, even though they won most games before.

## The Superpower
You keep perspective by considering more history. You don’t panic over one bad event.

## The Mission
When you see a recent story, ask yourself: 'What happened earlier this year on the same topic?'
