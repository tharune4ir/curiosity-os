## The Cheat Code
Extreme results tend to be followed by more normal ones. If you do very well or poorly once, next time you’re likely closer to your usual average.

## The Casino Trap
After a big win, people might gamble thinking they’re ‘hot’. But luck usually balances out, so they often lose.

## The Real World Hack
You score very low on a test. Because you usually do better, next test you score closer to your normal average, not as low.

## The Superpower
You don’t overreact to one big result. You expect things will even out over time.

## The Mission
Think of one big success or failure of yours. How did your next attempt turn out? Notice the difference.
