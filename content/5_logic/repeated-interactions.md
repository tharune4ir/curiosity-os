## The Cheat Code
When you deal with someone again and again, you care about future too. You can’t just cheat once, because you’ll meet again. Long-term thinking changes how you play.

## The Casino Trap
Treating one-time deals as long-term can be bad. For example, if you steal candy from a friend now, you might not trust them next time. Patterns repeat.

## The Real World Hack
Your tutor remembers if you are honest each session. You keep doing homework honestly so they keep helping you. You play this long game.

## The Superpower
You make friends and partners by being good repeatedly. People trust you and want to work with you in the future.

## The Mission
Think of someone you see often, like a teacher. Next time, do something that makes them happy to work with you again.
