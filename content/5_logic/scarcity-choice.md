## The Cheat Code
When resources (time, money, things) are limited, you must choose carefully. Scarcity means there isn’t enough for everyone to get everything they want.

## The Casino Trap
If you forget scarcity, you might try to do everything and fail. People sometimes act like they have unlimited time or money.

## The Real World Hack
You have only 50 for snacks. You choose either a large samosa or a soda. You can’t have both, so you decide based on which you want more.

## The Superpower
You prioritize what matters and make smart decisions knowing you can’t have it all.

## The Mission
Think of something you want most today (like playing a game or eating a treat). Remember you can’t do everything. Plan your choice.
