## The Cheat Code
Strawman fallacy is misrepresenting someone’s argument to make it easier to attack. You argue against a weaker version instead of the real point.

## The Casino Trap
Instead of addressing the real point, you argue against something not said. This avoids real issues.

## The Real World Hack
Friend says "I want to study more." You reply "So you never want to have fun again!" That’s a strawman argument.

## The Superpower
You fight the real issue, not a fake one. You win honest debates and understand others better.

## The Mission
When someone makes a claim, repeat it in your own words first. This stops you from accidentally creating a strawman.
