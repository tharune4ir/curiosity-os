## The Cheat Code
Sunk costs are past investments that can't be recovered. Don't let them force you to keep going. Only future gains matter.

## The Casino Trap
We say "Already spent 500 on this book, so I must finish it" even if it's boring. Past cost shouldn't decide future actions.

## The Real World Hack
You paid for a movie ticket and it’s bad. Instead of sitting through, you leave, saving time even though money is already gone.

## The Superpower
You cut losses. When something isn’t working, you move on instead of wasting more time or money.

## The Mission
Think of something you invested time in but stopped. Next time, ask "If this were free, would I still continue?" If not, stop.
