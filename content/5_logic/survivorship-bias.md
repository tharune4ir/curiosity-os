## The Cheat Code
Survivorship bias means noticing only successes and ignoring failures. Hearing just winners skews your view of how likely success really is.

## The Casino Trap
You hear only success stories (like "millionaire dropout"). You think it’s common and forget all who failed.

## The Real World Hack
You read about a dropout who became rich. But many dropouts stay poor. You remember failures too, not just the fame.

## The Superpower
You consider failures as well as successes. You make realistic plans knowing success is hard to achieve.

## The Mission
Pick a success story you admire. Find someone who tried the same path but didn’t succeed.
