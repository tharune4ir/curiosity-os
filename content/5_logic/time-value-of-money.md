## The Cheat Code
A rupee today is worth more than a rupee tomorrow, because you can invest it. Money grows over time with interest or opportunities.

## The Casino Trap
Procrastinating on savings or investments. Some think 100 next year is as good as 100 today, but inflation or interest changes value.

## The Real World Hack
Your parents give you 500 now instead of next month. You invest it, and one year later it’s 550. You see why earlier is better.

## The Superpower
You know to use money or time early. You benefit from any growth instead of losing value to inflation.

## The Mission
If someone offers you 100 now or 110 in a month, choose 100 now. Save or invest it to get more.
