## The Cheat Code
Tit-for-Tat means start by cooperating, then mirror your partner's last move. If they help you, help them next. If they harm you, you harm them back.

## The Casino Trap
If you start by betraying or always betray, your friends will too and no one wins in the long run. A mean start builds distrust.

## The Real World Hack
You and your sibling are cleaning the house together. You help first, and next round your sibling helps you. After some time, you split cleaning chores fairly and even get ice cream reward.

## The Superpower
You become the kind of person others trust and want to work with. When they see you cooperate, they cooperate, creating a cycle of fairness.

## The Mission
Help someone once without any gain. If they return the favor, keep helping; if not, be careful next time.
