## The Cheat Code
Your reputation is like a score: being fair and trustworthy means people will trust and cooperate with you. If you break trust, people avoid you.

## The Casino Trap
If you betray friends often, no one will trust you, so you’ll never make deals. Bad reputation closes doors.

## The Real World Hack
You borrow a book and return it on time, so the owner is happy and lends more. Your good reputation brings rewards.

## The Superpower
You build goodwill. People help you, give second chances, and trust you with big projects or secrets.

## The Mission
Help someone without asking for anything. Observe how it affects their view of you.
