## The Cheat Code
Variance measures how spread out outcomes can be. High variance means results can swing big, low variance means results stay close to average.

## The Casino Trap
Seeing one high or low and panicking. One game’s big win doesn’t mean every game will be the same.

## The Real World Hack
You score 60, 90, 70 on three tests. It’s normal variance. You know one score isn’t destiny; over many tests you’ll see your average.

## The Superpower
You ride out ups and downs. You know one bad day doesn’t ruin everything.

## The Mission
Look at your last 5 test scores and calculate your average. Notice how the scores differ from that average.
