## The Cheat Code
Think of a zero-sum game like a fixed pie: if you get a bigger slice, others must get smaller. One person’s win is exactly another’s loss.

## The Casino Trap
Imagine thinking that your success must make someone else fail. You get a top score, so your friend thinks they scored less because of you. This misunderstanding can lead to jealousy or sabotage.

## The Real World Hack
You and a friend split a 100 prize from a quiz. Instead of fighting, you agree each gets 50. Both of you earn something. You see that sharing can benefit both, not just beating each other.

## The Superpower
You learn to spot situations where a win-win is possible. You avoid needless fights and make deals where everyone wins more.

## The Mission
Next time you and a friend both want something, try to find a way to divide it fairly so both of you benefit.
