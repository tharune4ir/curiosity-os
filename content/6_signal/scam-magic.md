## The Invisible Code
Scams are like magic tricks: they hide their secrets well. Magicians fool experts, and scammers fool experts too. Just like no one can spot every trick (even wizards have secrets), no one can catch every scam instantly.

## The Digital Trap
You feel dumb for believing a scam email. You think 'I should have known better.' The shame covers you like a cloak.

## The Shield Protocol
Tell yourself: 'Even the smartest people get tricked.' This is a secret mantra to remember. It weakens the shame cloak. Understand that scammers spend months planning their trick, just like magicians spend years honing illusions.

## The Lifeline
When you talk to someone, you might laugh and say, 'I fell for a magic trick online!' They'll probably agree and share stories. This comparison makes it lighter and helps you see it was just a trick, not stupidity.

## The Empowerment Move
Every time you open the news or a website, find a fun one-minute video explaining a magic trick (like on YouTube). Seeing real magicians get fooled or fooled spectators can remind you 'if they can be fooled, so can I sometimes.'
