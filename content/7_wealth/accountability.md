## The Wealth Secret
Taking business risks under your real name. Society heavily rewards those who stand publicly behind their work with equity.

## The Middle Class Trap
Hiding behind fake anime avatars or refusing to attach your real name to your digital creations out of an intense fear of failure.

## The Leverage Play
Building a personal portfolio website where your actual name is tied directly to the high quality of the coding or design projects you produce.

## The Superpower
Trust accumulation.

## The Actionable Cent
Ensure your professional digital profiles actually reflect your real capabilities.
