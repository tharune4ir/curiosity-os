## The Wealth Secret
Finding something cheap in one place and selling it for a higher price in another place where people need it more.

## The Middle Class Trap
Thinking you have to invent a completely new product from scratch to make money, which is incredibly hard and risky.

## The Leverage Play
Buying bulk digital design templates for cheap on a global site, and selling them individually to local shop owners who don't know how to find them.

## The Superpower
Risk-free profit.

## The Actionable Cent
Look for price differences of the exact same item on two different online marketplaces.
