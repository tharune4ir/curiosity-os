## The Wealth Secret
Money is a seed. An Asset is planting it so it grows a tree that drops free apples. A Liability is a hole in your pocket.

## The Middle Class Trap
Getting a first job and immediately buying an expensive car on loan to look rich. The car loses value instantly.

## The Leverage Play
Using 5,000 of Diwali money to buy a course to learn video editing. That skill is an asset that makes 10,000 next month.

## The Superpower
Financial freedom. You never work a job you hate.

## The Actionable Cent
Count how many things in your room are liabilities.
