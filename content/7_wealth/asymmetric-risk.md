## The Wealth Secret
A bet where the worst-case scenario is losing a little time, but the best-case scenario is making thousands of rupees.

## The Middle Class Trap
Only taking 'safe' symmetric risks, or being so afraid of failure that they never launch any project at all.

## The Leverage Play
Spending a weekend coding a simple mobile game. Cost: 10 hours. Downside: Nobody plays it. Upside: 100,000 downloads and passive income.

## The Superpower
Upside maximization.

## The Actionable Cent
Identify one zero-cost digital project you could launch this weekend.
