## The Wealth Secret
Human attention is the most valuable oil in the modern world. If you can ethically capture attention, you can print money.

## The Middle Class Trap
Believing money comes from physical factories, ignoring that billions of dollars now flow purely based on where eyeballs are looking.

## The Leverage Play
Creating highly engaging, funny educational content that makes people stop scrolling and pay attention to your message.

## The Superpower
Traffic direction.

## The Actionable Cent
Analyze a viral video. What exact trick did it use in the first 3 seconds to keep your attention?
