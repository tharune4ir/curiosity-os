## The Wealth Secret
An engaged audience that trusts you is the most valuable asset you can own. It allows you to launch any product with instant success.

## The Middle Class Trap
Paying massive amounts of money to Facebook or Google for ads every time you want to sell a single item.

## The Leverage Play
Building an email newsletter about your favorite video game. When you launch your own game later, you have 10,000 people ready to buy it for free.

## The Superpower
Zero-cost marketing.

## The Actionable Cent
Start collecting the emails of friends who like the same niche hobbies as you.
