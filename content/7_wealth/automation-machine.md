## The Wealth Secret
The ability to turn repetitive human effort into a set of 'if-then' rules that a machine can execute forever.

## The Middle Class Trap
Doing the same soul-crushing manual task every day for decades without ever trying to build a system to automate it.

## The Leverage Play
Using a tool like 'Zapier' or a simple script to automatically send your daily expenses from your email to a tracking sheet.

## The Superpower
Time reclamation.

## The Actionable Cent
Find one thing you do on your computer every day and try to find an app that does it for you.
