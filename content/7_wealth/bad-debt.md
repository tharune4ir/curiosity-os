## The Wealth Secret
Borrowing money to buy a luxury watch. It doesn't make you money, and you are stuck paying a fine (interest) for years.

## The Middle Class Trap
Using credit cards to buy expensive sneakers, entering a cycle where you work just to pay the bank's interest.

## The Leverage Play
Refusing to ever pay interest on a liability. If you cannot afford to buy the sneakers twice in cash, you don't buy them.

## The Superpower
Total debt freedom.

## The Actionable Cent
Look up the interest rate on a standard credit card (usually 30%+).
