## The Wealth Secret
Believing a corporate logo confers quality or identity, tricking the consumer into paying irrational markups for basic goods.

## The Middle Class Trap
Refusing to buy generic equivalents of basic goods, paying a 40% premium just to have a specific logo on an item.

## The Leverage Play
Analyzing the core utility of a product (e.g., a basic cotton shirt) and refusing to pay a massive premium solely for the branding label.

## The Superpower
Utility focus.

## The Actionable Cent
Compare the ingredient list of a branded staple good versus its much cheaper generic counterpart.
