## The Wealth Secret
If you can build the product (code/create) and you can sell the product (market/persuade), you become a god in the economy.

## The Middle Class Trap
Only learning one side. The coder who can't speak to humans, or the salesman who can't build anything real.

## The Leverage Play
A teenager learning Python to build a useful app, and then learning SEO marketing to get thousands to download it.

## The Superpower
Total independence.

## The Actionable Cent
Decide if you are naturally a builder or a seller, and commit to learning the opposite skill.
