## The Wealth Secret
Unregulated pooling of local funds. While some are legitimate, many act as fraudulent fronts that vanish with lower-middle-class savings.

## The Middle Class Trap
Trusting unregulated local operators (like the Saradha scam) simply because community members are involved, leading to massive loss.

## The Leverage Play
Refusing to participate in shadow banking; directing all savings into transparent, highly regulated financial institutions and demat accounts.

## The Superpower
Institutional security.

## The Actionable Cent
Research the regulatory role of SEBI in protecting Indian investors from scams.
