## The Wealth Secret
The market is infinite. Multiple people can win at the same time. Working with your 'rival' can often make the pie bigger for both.

## The Middle Class Trap
Being jealous of other people's success, thinking there is only a fixed amount of money and if they won, you lost.

## The Leverage Play
Partnering with someone who has the skills you lack. You build the software; they do the marketing. Together, you build a billion-dollar company.

## The Superpower
Synergistic growth.

## The Actionable Cent
Identify one person who has a skill that complements yours and think of a project you could do together.
