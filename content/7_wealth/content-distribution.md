## The Wealth Secret
Making the product is only 20% of the battle. Building the distribution channels (how people find it) is the other 80%.

## The Middle Class Trap
Spending six months perfecting a digital drawing, posting it once to zero followers, and quitting when nobody buys it.

## The Leverage Play
Spending equal time learning how algorithms work, how to write catchy headlines, and how to distribute the drawing to large forums.

## The Superpower
Virality mechanics.

## The Actionable Cent
Look at a successful creator and analyze where they post their links to get traffic.
