## The Wealth Secret
Digital scarce assets protected by math instead of governments. It is a high-speed, high-risk digital frontier.

## The Middle Class Trap
Buying 'dog coins' because of a tweet, instead of understanding the underlying technology of decentralized trust.

## The Leverage Play
Allocating only a tiny 1-5% of your wealth to Bitcoin as 'digital gold' while keeping the rest in safer, more traditional assets.

## The Superpower
Digital sovereignty.
