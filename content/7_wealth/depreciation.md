## The Wealth Secret
The silent loss of value in physical items. The moment you drive a new car off the lot, it loses 20% of its value instantly.

## The Middle Class Trap
Viewing a car or an expensive phone as an 'investment,' completely misunderstanding the definition of an asset that appreciates.

## The Leverage Play
Buying high-quality, slightly used items where someone else has already absorbed the massive initial depreciation hit.

## The Superpower
Value preservation.

## The Actionable Cent
Look up the resale value of a 2-year-old iPhone compared to a brand new one.
