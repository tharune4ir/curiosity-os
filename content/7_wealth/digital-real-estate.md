## The Wealth Secret
Owning a highly trafficked domain name or a massive social media handle is the modern equivalent of owning a building in Mumbai.

## The Middle Class Trap
Only renting space on platforms you don't control, where an algorithm change can instantly wipe out your entire business.

## The Leverage Play
Buying your own .com domain name and hosting your own website so you have a permanent, un-erasable home on the internet.

## The Superpower
Sovereignty.

## The Actionable Cent
Check if your personal name is available as a .com domain.
