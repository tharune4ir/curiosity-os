## The Wealth Secret
Not putting all your eggs in one basket. If you drop the basket, you still have eggs somewhere else.

## The Middle Class Trap
Going 'all in' on one meme coin or one single company stock, and losing everything when that one thing fails.

## The Leverage Play
Putting a little money in tech, a little in real estate, and a little in index funds. If tech drops, real estate keeps you safe.

## The Superpower
Risk destruction.

## The Actionable Cent
Look at how an Index Fund automatically buys 500 baskets for you.
