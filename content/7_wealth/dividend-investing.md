## The Wealth Secret
Companies paying you a 'thank you' fee just for owning their shares. It is the purest form of passive cash flow.

## The Middle Class Trap
Ignoring dividends and only focusing on the price of the stock, missing out on the engine that can pay for your daily coffee.

## The Leverage Play
Owning shares in a profitable utility company (like power or water) that pays you every three months regardless of the stock price.

## The Superpower
Automated cash flow.

## The Actionable Cent
Look up a 'dividend yield' of a major company like TCS or ITC.
