## The Wealth Secret
A moat around your castle. It stops you from having to chop down your apple trees (assets) to pay for a broken roof (emergency).

## The Middle Class Trap
Investing every penny or spending it all, so when a laptop breaks, they have to take a terrible high-interest loan.

## The Leverage Play
Keeping 6 months of basic pocket money in pure cash before you start investing in riskier things like stocks.

## The Superpower
Panic immunity.

## The Actionable Cent
Calculate your basic monthly expenses and multiply by 6. That's your shield number.
