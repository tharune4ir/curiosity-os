## The Wealth Secret
Mixing insurance with investment is like a shampoo-conditioner combo: it does a terrible job at both. The returns barely beat inflation.

## The Middle Class Trap
Buying traditional LIC endowment policies because they feel 'safe,' resulting in expensive life cover and dismal 4-6% investment returns.

## The Leverage Play
Separating the two entirely: buying pure Term Insurance for massive, cheap life cover, and investing the difference into high-growth Mutual Funds.

## The Superpower
Capital efficiency.

## The Actionable Cent
Ask your parents to compare the premium of a 1 Crore Endowment policy versus a Term policy.
