## The Wealth Secret
Competition is for losers. If you are competing directly, you are doing the exact same boring thing as everyone else.

## The Middle Class Trap
Entering highly crowded fields (like generic engineering) and fighting desperately over tiny margins by slightly undercutting competitors.

## The Leverage Play
Combining highly unique skills to create a 'category of one' where you have zero direct competitors.

## The Superpower
Monopoly creation.

## The Actionable Cent
Combine your two most unrelated hobbies and see if a unique project idea pops out.
