## The Wealth Secret
Wealth is not stolen; it is created. You get wealthy by providing society with immense value, making everyone better off.

## The Middle Class Trap
Secretly despising rich people and thinking money is evil, which subconsciously stops you from ever building wealth yourself.

## The Leverage Play
Realizing that the creator of a life-saving medicine or a fun video game became rich because they gave people something they desperately wanted.

## The Superpower
Psychological alignment.

## The Actionable Cent
Notice a local business that makes your life better and respect how they earn their revenue.
