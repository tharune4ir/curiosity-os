## The Wealth Secret
The cultural belief that putting money in a bank vault makes it completely immune to loss, ignoring the stealth tax of inflation.

## The Middle Class Trap
Celebrating a 5% Fixed Deposit return while the real inflation rate on education and healthcare is running at 10%. They are losing money safely.

## The Leverage Play
Accepting short-term volatility in the equity markets in exchange for the mathematical certainty of long-term purchasing power preservation.

## The Superpower
Risk recalibration.

## The Actionable Cent
Differentiate between the 'volatility' of a stock and the 'permanent loss of value' of uninvested cash.
