## The Wealth Secret
Paper money has no real value. It is just government paper points used to measure value. It is the measuring tape, not the house.

## The Middle Class Trap
Hoarding paper cash under a mattress thinking it is wealth, while the government prints more and the paper loses value.

## The Leverage Play
Taking your paper points and quickly trading them for real assets—like software code, a business, or stocks—that actually create value.

## The Superpower
Systemic understanding.

## The Actionable Cent
Realize that a 500 note is just an IOU from the RBI.
