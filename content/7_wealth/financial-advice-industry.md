## The Wealth Secret
Most 'Financial Advisors' are actually 'Salespeople.' They get paid a commission to sell you expensive products, not to make you rich.

## The Middle Class Trap
Trusting a bank employee's advice without realizing they are biased towards products that make the bank more money.

## The Leverage Play
Learning the basics of personal finance yourself so you don't have to rely on biased experts. You become your own advisor.

## The Superpower
Knowledge independence.

## The Actionable Cent
Check if the person giving you money advice gets paid a commission for what they sell.
