## The Wealth Secret
Buying an asset solely because everyone else on social media is talking about it, usually right at the peak of a dangerous bubble.

## The Middle Class Trap
Panicking and buying a trendy cryptocurrency at its all-time high just because neighbors claim they are getting rich overnight.

## The Leverage Play
Ignoring social media hype cycles entirely; sticking rigidly to a pre-defined, boring, and long-term index investing strategy.

## The Superpower
Emotional detachment.

## The Actionable Cent
Recall a recent trend you bought into because of FOMO that you abandoned a week later.
