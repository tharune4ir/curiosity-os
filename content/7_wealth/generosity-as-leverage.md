## The Wealth Secret
Giving away value for free (content, advice, help) creates a massive 'social debt' that the world naturally wants to pay back to you.

## The Middle Class Trap
Holding onto 'secrets' or charging for every tiny bit of help, which limits their reach and makes them look small.

## The Leverage Play
Sharing your best ideas for free on the internet. You become known as the expert, and the biggest deals come to you automatically.

## The Superpower
Magnetic influence.

## The Actionable Cent
Teach one person a cool trick you know today for free.
