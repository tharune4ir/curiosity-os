## The Wealth Secret
If a deal sounds too good to be true, you aren't the investor; you are the product. There are no shortcuts to compounding.

## The Middle Class Trap
Losing their hard-earned savings to 'double your money in 30 days' schemes because they are too impatient to wait for real growth.

## The Leverage Play
Running away as fast as possible from any investment that promises high returns with zero risk or effort.

## The Superpower
Skeptical filter.

## The Actionable Cent
If someone on the internet tells you they have a 'secret trick' to make money, ask yourself why they are telling you.
