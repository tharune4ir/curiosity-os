## The Wealth Secret
There are no get-rich-quick schemes. That is just someone else getting rich quickly off of you.

## The Middle Class Trap
Buying expensive online courses that promise to teach you how to 'make 1 Lakh a day trading,' falling for the marketer's dream.

## The Leverage Play
Realizing that if someone actually had a secret money-printing machine, they would run it quietly, not sell the secret to you for 999.

## The Superpower
Bullshit detection.

## The Actionable Cent
Unfollow anyone on social media who promises you can get rich without years of hard work.
