## The Wealth Secret
Physical things that have been valuable for 5,000 years. They don't 'grow,' but they protect you when paper money fails.

## The Middle Class Trap
Buying physical gold jewelry, which loses value due to 'making charges' and taxes, instead of buying pure gold bonds.

## The Leverage Play
Holding a small amount of gold as 'disaster insurance.' If the economy crashes, gold usually shines the brightest.

## The Superpower
Generational protection.

## The Actionable Cent
Ask your grandmother why she trusts gold more than a bank.
