## The Wealth Secret
Borrowing a tractor to plow a massive field. The tractor does the work, pays off the loan, and leaves you with a giant profit.

## The Middle Class Trap
Being terrified of all loans and never using leverage, or worse, using loans to buy things that lose value.

## The Leverage Play
Taking a small loan to buy a high-end laptop because you already have clients paying you to edit their 4K videos. The laptop pays for itself.

## The Superpower
Capital acceleration.

## The Actionable Cent
Learn the difference between producer debt and consumer debt.
