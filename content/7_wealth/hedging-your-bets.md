## The Wealth Secret
Buying an umbrella when the sun is shining, just in case. Protecting your downside so you can stay in the game long-term.

## The Middle Class Trap
Being so confident in one outcome (like a specific stock rising) that they have no plan if the opposite happens.

## The Leverage Play
Having some cash in the bank while investing in stocks. If stocks go up, you win. If they crash, you have cash to buy more.

## The Superpower
Survival.

## The Actionable Cent
Always keep a 'Plan B' for your most important financial goals.
