## The Wealth Secret
Owning a beautiful home but having zero cash in the bank because all your money goes into paying the house's mortgage and repairs.

## The Middle Class Trap
Buying a 'dream home' that is too big, leaving them with no money to invest, travel, or start a business.

## The Leverage Play
Renting a modest place that you can easily afford while using your extra cash to buy assets that actually grow your wealth.

## The Superpower
Lifestyle flexibility.

## The Actionable Cent
Calculate how much of a family's income goes purely into maintaining their house.
