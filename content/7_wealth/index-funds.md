## The Wealth Secret
Instead of betting on one horse to win the race, you bet on the entire stadium. If the stadium survives, you win.

## The Middle Class Trap
Trying to 'beat the market' by picking one lucky stock, which is as gambling-heavy as choosing one number on a roulette wheel.

## The Leverage Play
Regularly buying the Nifty 50 or S&P 500 index. You are owning the top 50 companies who are working for you simultaneously.

## The Superpower
Guaranteed market returns.

## The Actionable Cent
Look up what the 'Nifty 50' index represents in the Indian economy.
