## The Wealth Secret
Inflation is a ghost that sneaks into your piggy bank and steals a tiny slice of your money's buying power every year.

## The Middle Class Trap
Keeping all savings in a basic bank account thinking it is 'safe,' while things get more expensive around them.

## The Leverage Play
Moving money from a normal bank account into index funds so your money grows faster than the prices of movie tickets.

## The Superpower
Wealth preservation.

## The Actionable Cent
Find out how much a packet of Maggi cost ten years ago versus today.
