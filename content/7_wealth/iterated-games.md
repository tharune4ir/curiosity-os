## The Wealth Secret
All massive returns in life come from playing long-term games with long-term, trustworthy people.

## The Middle Class Trap
Cheating a friend or a client to make a quick 500 today, forever destroying your reputation and losing millions in the future.

## The Leverage Play
Partnering with the smartest, most honest kids in your school to build a robotics project, knowing trust builds over decades.

## The Superpower
Trust compounding.

## The Actionable Cent
Evaluate your current friend group. Are they playing long-term or short-term games?
