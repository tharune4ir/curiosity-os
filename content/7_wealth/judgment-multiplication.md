## The Wealth Secret
Leverage is neutral. It amplifies good judgment into massive wealth, and bad judgment into catastrophic, public failure.

## The Middle Class Trap
Getting access to a large social media audience (leverage) but using it to spread stupid rumors, destroying your reputation globally.

## The Leverage Play
Studying foundational mental models (math, game theory) to ensure that when you apply leverage to a decision, the decision is flawless.

## The Superpower
Consequence awareness.

## The Actionable Cent
Analyze a decision you made today. Imagine the outcome if multiplied by a thousand.
