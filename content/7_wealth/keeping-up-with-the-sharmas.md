## The Wealth Secret
Constantly comparing your lifestyle to your neighbors, forcing you to spend money to match their outward display of wealth.

## The Middle Class Trap
Seeing the neighbors buy a new SUV and feeling intense psychological pressure to buy an even bigger one, entering a mutual debt spiral.

## The Leverage Play
Playing a single-player game. You focus entirely on your own net worth spreadsheet, not the neighbor's driveway.

## The Superpower
Financial peace.

## The Actionable Cent
Acknowledge that you have no idea how much debt the neighbor took on to buy that car.
