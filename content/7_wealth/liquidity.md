## The Wealth Secret
How fast you can turn an asset back into spendable cash. Cash is water (fast). A house is a block of ice (very slow to melt).

## The Middle Class Trap
Putting all money into a piece of land, and when a medical emergency happens, they cannot sell the land fast enough.

## The Leverage Play
Keeping some money in liquid stocks (water) and some in physical things, balancing speed with growth.

## The Superpower
Tactical optionality.

## The Actionable Cent
Classify your family's assets into fast-melting and slow-melting categories.
