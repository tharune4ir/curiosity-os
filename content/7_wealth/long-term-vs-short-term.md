## The Wealth Secret
The difference between a farmer planting an orchard and a gambler betting on a coin toss. Wealth takes decades, not days.

## The Middle Class Trap
Constantly checking their stock app every 10 minutes, getting emotional over tiny daily moves that don't matter.

## The Leverage Play
Thinking in decades. If you wouldn't own a stock for 10 years, don't even think about owning it for 10 minutes.

## The Superpower
Time horizon expansion.

## The Actionable Cent
Ask yourself if the thing you are buying today will still be valuable 10 years from now.
