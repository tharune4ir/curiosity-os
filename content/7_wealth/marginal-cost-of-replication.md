## The Wealth Secret
The cost of making a second copy of your product. For a physical shirt, it's high. For a digital ebook, it is exactly ₹0.

## The Middle Class Trap
Spending all your time making things that have a high cost to replicate, which eats into your future profits.

## The Leverage Play
Focusing on 'digital assets'—videos, code, or writing—where you work once and can sell a billion copies for no extra cost.

## The Superpower
Zero-friction scaling.

## The Actionable Cent
Compare the cost of printing a book versus making a PDF version of that same book.
