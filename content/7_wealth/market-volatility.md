## The Wealth Secret
The stock market is a rollercoaster. It violently goes up and down in the short term, but always climbs the mountain over decades.

## The Middle Class Trap
Panicking when the market drops 10% and selling all their magic seeds at a loss out of sheer terror.

## The Leverage Play
Seeing a market crash as a massive discount sale. When everyone else is panicking, you use your cash to buy cheaper assets.

## The Superpower
Emotional detachment.

## The Actionable Cent
Look at a 20-year chart of the Nifty 50; notice how the big crashes look like tiny blips over time.
