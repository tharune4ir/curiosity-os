## The Wealth Secret
A mentor is a shortcut. They have already walked the minefield and can tell you where not to step, saving you ten years of pain.

## The Middle Class Trap
Thinking they can 'figure it all out' on their own, wasting years repeating the same mistakes that have already been solved.

## The Leverage Play
Reading the biography of a great business leader. It's essentially a ₹500 way to get a mentor's entire life story and secrets.

## The Superpower
Knowledge compression.

## The Actionable Cent
Choose one successful person and read about their biggest failure and how they fixed it.
