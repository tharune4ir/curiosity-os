## The Wealth Secret
Finding someone who has already played the level you are stuck on. They can give you the cheat code to bypass years of mistakes.

## The Middle Class Trap
Letting ego get in the way and trying to figure out incredibly complex business or coding problems completely alone in the dark.

## The Leverage Play
Sending a polite, short email to a successful 20-year-old developer asking for one specific piece of advice on a coding bug.

## The Superpower
Time-travel.

## The Actionable Cent
Find someone three steps ahead of you online and study their exact path.
