## The Wealth Secret
Credit card companies want you to only pay the 'minimum amount due' so they can trap you in decades of compound interest debt.

## The Middle Class Trap
Rejoicing that the credit card bill is only asking for a 2,000 minimum payment, ignoring the 36% annual interest accumulating on the remaining balance.

## The Leverage Play
Setting up an auto-pay system that completely clears the entire credit card balance in full every single month, zero exceptions.

## The Superpower
Compound defense.

## The Actionable Cent
Read the fine print on a credit card statement showing how long it takes to clear debt using only minimum payments.
