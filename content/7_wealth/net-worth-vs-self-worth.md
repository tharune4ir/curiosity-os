## The Wealth Secret
Your value as a human is not tied to your bank balance. Confusing the two leads to misery regardless of how much money you have.

## The Middle Class Trap
Feeling 'worthless' during a financial setback and feeling 'superior' when they have money, tied to a mental rollercoaster.

## The Leverage Play
Building an internal sense of value based on your skills and character so that even if you lose all your money, you know how to build it back.

## The Superpower
Internal stability.

## The Actionable Cent
List three things you love about yourself that have nothing to do with money.
