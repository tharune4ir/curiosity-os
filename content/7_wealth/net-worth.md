## The Wealth Secret
The true scoreboard of wealth. It is everything you own (Assets) minus everything you owe (Liabilities).

## The Middle Class Trap
Judging someone's wealth by the car they drive (income), not realizing they owe 30 Lakhs to the bank (negative net worth).

## The Leverage Play
Tracking your actual net worth on a piece of paper every month, ignoring what clothes people wear and focusing on their math.

## The Superpower
Financial truth.

## The Actionable Cent
Calculate your personal net worth right now (Savings minus any money you owe friends).
