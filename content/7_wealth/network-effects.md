## The Wealth Secret
A product becomes vastly more valuable as more people use it. A telephone is totally useless if you are the only one on Earth who owns one.

## The Middle Class Trap
Trying to build isolated projects that do not benefit from a community of users interacting and sharing with each other.

## The Leverage Play
Creating a study group server where every time a new student joins and brings their notes, the entire server becomes exponentially more valuable.

## The Superpower
Self-sustaining growth.

## The Actionable Cent
Map out how a popular multiplayer game benefits every time a new player logs in.
