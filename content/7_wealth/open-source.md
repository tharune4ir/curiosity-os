## The Wealth Secret
Millions of developers have already built massive blocks of code and given them away for free. You just have to snap them together.

## The Middle Class Trap
Trying to write every single line of code from scratch, wasting months reinventing the wheel when the wheel is available for free online.

## The Leverage Play
Downloading a free open-source library to handle the login screen of your app, saving you three weeks of frustrating coding work.

## The Superpower
Community leverage.

## The Actionable Cent
Look up what 'GitHub' is and see how much free code is available to the public.
