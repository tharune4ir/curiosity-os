## The Wealth Secret
Your time is your only non-renewable asset. Spending five hours to save ₹100 is a mathematically terrible trade.

## The Middle Class Trap
Traveling two hours across town to save ₹50 on a bag of rice, while their time could have been used to learn a high-value skill.

## The Leverage Play
Outsourcing repetitive tasks (like laundry or basic data entry) as soon as your hourly earned rate is higher than the cost of the help.

## The Superpower
Time arbitrage.

## The Actionable Cent
Calculate your 'hourly rate' by dividing your monthly goal by 160 hours. Is your current task worth that much?
