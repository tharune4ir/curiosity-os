## The Wealth Secret
Every rupee spent on a video game is a rupee that cannot be sent to a factory to work for you. You can't spend the same coin twice.

## The Middle Class Trap
Buying a 5,000 branded jacket and thinking it only cost 5,000, forgetting the 50,000 it would have become if invested.

## The Leverage Play
Waiting 48 hours before buying something you saw on Instagram. If you still want it, okay. If not, you invest the cash.

## The Superpower
Emotional discipline.

## The Actionable Cent
Next time you buy a snack, imagine what that money could be worth in 10 years.
