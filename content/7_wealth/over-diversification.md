## The Wealth Secret
Spreading a tiny amount of money across too many different assets, diluting potential gains into complete irrelevance.

## The Middle Class Trap
A beginner investor buying thirty different mutual funds with a 5,000 budget, achieving nothing but tracking confusion and extra fees.

## The Leverage Play
Concentrating early capital into one or two broad-market index funds, or using the money to acquire one specific high-leverage skill.

## The Superpower
Focused compounding.

## The Actionable Cent
Recognize that protecting wealth requires diversification, but building initial wealth requires focus.
