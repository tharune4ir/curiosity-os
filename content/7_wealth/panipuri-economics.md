## The Wealth Secret
Buying raw ingredients for 10, applying labor, and selling the experience for 50. The markup is the reward for assembly.

## The Middle Class Trap
Never understanding how local businesses actually make money, viewing transactions purely as a consumer.

## The Leverage Play
Buying cheap digital stock footage, editing it into a high-quality video ad, and selling the assembled product for a massive markup.

## The Superpower
Margin extraction.

## The Actionable Cent
Calculate the cost of ingredients for a local street food item versus its selling price.
