## The Wealth Secret
Capital (money) and Labor (people) require someone to give you permission. People must follow, banks must lend.

## The Middle Class Trap
Chasing 'manager' titles or huge business loans before proving you actually have good judgment or skills.

## The Leverage Play
Focusing completely on building your own skills and accountability first, making investors naturally want to give you their capital.

## The Superpower
Resource attraction.

## The Actionable Cent
Realize that leading others requires proving your own extreme competence first.
