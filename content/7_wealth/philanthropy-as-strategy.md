## The Wealth Secret
Giving away money is a sign of abundance. It tells your brain (and the world) that you have more than enough, which attracts even more.

## The Middle Class Trap
Thinking they can only give back 'once they are rich,' missing the psychological power of starting with even ₹10 today.

## The Leverage Play
Setting aside 1% of your pocket money to help a cause you care about. It builds the character needed to manage millions later.

## The Superpower
Abundance alignment.

## The Actionable Cent
Donate a small amount to a local charity or a person in need this week.
