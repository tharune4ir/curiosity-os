## The Wealth Secret
The market changes violently. The people who survive are the ones who can instantly learn a new game when the old one breaks.

## The Middle Class Trap
Refusing to learn about AI because 'it wasn't in the syllabus,' clinging to an outdated skill while the world moves on.

## The Leverage Play
Realizing that traditional coding might die, so immediately pivoting to become an expert in managing AI agents instead.

## The Superpower
Future-proofing.

## The Actionable Cent
Identify one piece of technology that will probably be obsolete by the time you graduate.
