## The Wealth Secret
A scam that pays fake returns to early investors using the money deposited by new investors, inevitably collapsing when recruitment stops.

## The Middle Class Trap
Believing a charismatic relative who promises 'guaranteed 20% monthly returns' without understanding the underlying business.

## The Leverage Play
Instantly rejecting any investment that guarantees risk-free high returns, relies on recruiting members, or lacks official SEBI registration.

## The Superpower
Fraud detection.

## The Actionable Cent
Identify the mathematical impossibility of sustaining a 20% monthly return forever.
