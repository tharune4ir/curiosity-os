## The Wealth Secret
The Principal is the original apple. The Interest is the seed inside. If you eat the seed, you can't grow a new tree.

## The Middle Class Trap
Taking the profit from a good investment and immediately spending it on a party, resetting the compounding snowball back to zero.

## The Leverage Play
Setting your investments to 'auto-reinvest,' meaning every seed dropped is immediately planted back into the ground.

## The Superpower
Maximum wealth velocity.

## The Actionable Cent
Ensure any savings account you have leaves the interest inside to grow.
