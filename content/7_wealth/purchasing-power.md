## The Wealth Secret
Money is just a ruler. Over time, the ruler shrinks. You need more rupees to buy the exact same amount of rice.

## The Middle Class Trap
Feeling rich because their salary went up 5%, but failing to realize the cost of living went up 8%. They actually got poorer.

## The Leverage Play
Demanding equity instead of just a salary, because equity prices adjust to inflation while salaries always lag behind.

## The Superpower
True value perception.

## The Actionable Cent
Stop measuring wealth in rupees; measure it in how much time it buys you.
