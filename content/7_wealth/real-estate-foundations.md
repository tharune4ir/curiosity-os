## The Wealth Secret
Owning physical land. They isn't making any more of it. It is a slow, steady, and extremely heavy asset.

## The Middle Class Trap
Thinking their own home is an 'investment' when it actually takes money out of their pocket every month (maintenance, tax).

## The Leverage Play
Buying land that is in the path of future city growth, or using a REIT to own commercial property without needing crores of cash.

## The Superpower
Tangible stability.

## The Actionable Cent
Find out what a REIT (Real Estate Investment Trust) is and how it lets you own tiny pieces of malls.
