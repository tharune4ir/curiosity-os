## The Wealth Secret
Trimming the trees that grew too big and using the clippings to feed the smaller trees. Keeping your assets in balance.

## The Middle Class Trap
Letting one successful stock become 90% of their wealth, and then losing everything when that one stock eventually fails.

## The Leverage Play
Once a year, selling a little bit of your 'winner' stocks to buy more of your 'loser' bonds, ensuring you always buy low and sell high.

## The Superpower
Algorithmic discipline.

## The Actionable Cent
Review your savings every six months to make sure they aren't all in just one category.
