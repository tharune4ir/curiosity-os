## The Wealth Secret
A salary is renting out your time. Equity is owning the machine that prints money while you sleep.

## The Middle Class Trap
Fighting for a 10% raise at a job they hate, completely ignoring the need to own a piece of a business or property.

## The Leverage Play
Starting a YouTube channel or an app. Even if it makes zero money today, you own 100% of the digital real estate.

## The Superpower
Decoupled earnings.

## The Actionable Cent
Research the difference between an employee's salary and a shareholder's dividend.
