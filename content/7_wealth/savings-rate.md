## The Wealth Secret
The percentage of your money you keep. Your savings rate matters ten times more than your investment returns when you start.

## The Middle Class Trap
Obsessing over finding a stock that gives 15% instead of 10%, while spending 95% of their salary on junk.

## The Leverage Play
Focusing on saving 50% of your pocket money. A high savings rate makes you rich long before the stock market does.

## The Superpower
Wealth acceleration.

## The Actionable Cent
Calculate what percentage of your total allowance you actually saved last month.
