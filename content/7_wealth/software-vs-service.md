## The Wealth Secret
A service requires you to do work for every new client. Software allows you to build once and sell to 100,000 clients instantly.

## The Middle Class Trap
Building a business where you are 'the expert' who has to show up for every meeting, effectively creating a job for yourself.

## The Leverage Play
Instead of 'consulting' for businesses on how to use AI, you build a simple AI tool that businesses can just pay to use themselves.

## The Superpower
Uncapped growth.

## The Actionable Cent
Think of one thing you currently do for a friend that you could turn into a template.
