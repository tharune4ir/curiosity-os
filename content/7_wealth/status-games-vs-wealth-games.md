## The Wealth Secret
Status is your rank in the tribe (looking rich). Wealth is the assets you own (being rich). Most people sacrifice wealth to buy status.

## The Middle Class Trap
Buying a luxury car to impress neighbors who don't actually care about you, using money you haven't even earned yet.

## The Leverage Play
Wearing a plain T-shirt while your bank account is full of index funds. You win the wealth game while everyone else is losing the status game.

## The Superpower
Social freedom.

## The Actionable Cent
List one thing you bought recently just because you wanted people to see you with it.
