## The Wealth Secret
Continuing to pour money or time into a failing project simply because you have already invested heavily in it.

## The Middle Class Trap
Staying in an irrelevant, dying college degree path because two years of tuition are already paid, sacrificing the future to honor the past.

## The Leverage Play
Ruthlessly cutting losses on failed endeavors. If a project is dead, you abandon it immediately and pivot capital and time into better opportunities.

## The Superpower
Objective pivoting.

## The Actionable Cent
Identify one activity you dislike but continue doing just because you started it.
