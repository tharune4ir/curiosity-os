## The Wealth Secret
When everyone wants an umbrella in a storm and there are only two, the price skyrockets. Rarity creates value.

## The Middle Class Trap
Studying generic subjects just because everyone else is doing it, creating a massive supply of identical graduates with low pay.

## The Leverage Play
Learning a highly rare skill, like AI prompt engineering or advanced 3D modeling, making companies fight to hire you.

## The Superpower
Market gravity.

## The Actionable Cent
Identify one skill that is currently in high demand but very few people know.
