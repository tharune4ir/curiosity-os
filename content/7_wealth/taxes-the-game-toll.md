## The Wealth Secret
The toll you pay to the government to play the game of economics. If you learn the rules, you can legally pay less toll.

## The Middle Class Trap
Earning a massive salary and paying 30% of it in taxes because they don't know how to use legal tax-saving investments.

## The Leverage Play
Starting a small registered business for your freelance work so you can legally deduct the cost of your internet bill from your taxes.

## The Superpower
Income retention.

## The Actionable Cent
Ask your parents what '80C' means during tax season.
