## The Wealth Secret
The best way to master the philosophy of money is to explain it to someone else. You find your own gaps in knowledge immediately.

## The Middle Class Trap
Guarding what they know because they are afraid of others getting ahead, which stops their own brain from growing.

## The Leverage Play
Applying the 'Feynman Technique.' You learn a concept like 'Liquidity' and try to explain it to an 8-year-old.

## The Superpower
Conceptual clarity.

## The Actionable Cent
Explain one concept from DISHA OS to a family member tonight.
