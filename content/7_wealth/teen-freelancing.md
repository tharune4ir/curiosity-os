## The Wealth Secret
The internet allows a 14-year-old in India to bypass age limits and sell digital skills directly to clients in America.

## The Middle Class Trap
Thinking you have to wait until you are 22 with a college degree before you can start earning your first rupee.

## The Leverage Play
Creating an anonymous profile on Fiverr to offer thumbnail design or transcription services, getting paid in dollars.

## The Superpower
Geographic arbitrage.

## The Actionable Cent
Browse a freelance site to see what simple digital tasks people are actively buying.
