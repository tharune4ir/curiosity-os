## The Wealth Secret
Leverage is using a massive stick to move a heavy boulder. It amplifies your effort so one unit of work produces 1,000 units of output.

## The Middle Class Trap
Relying entirely on raw physical effort. Thinking that the only way to earn twice as much money is to work twice as many hours.

## The Leverage Play
Spending upfront time to build an automated tool or video that works continuously in the background while you go play sports.

## The Superpower
Effort multiplication.

## The Actionable Cent
Identify a repetitive daily chore and figure out how a tool could do it faster.
