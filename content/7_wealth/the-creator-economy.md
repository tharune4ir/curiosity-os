## The Wealth Secret
Society heavily rewards the producers of media. The glowing screen is a one-way mirror separating creators from consumers.

## The Middle Class Trap
Spending six hours a day brainlessly scrolling through short videos, acting solely as the product being sold to advertisers.

## The Leverage Play
Flipping the mirror. You research a cool science topic and create one educational video a week instead of just watching them.

## The Superpower
Attention capture.

## The Actionable Cent
Turn off your phone for 24 hours and write a script instead of consuming one.
