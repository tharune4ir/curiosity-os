## The Wealth Secret
'Fear Of Missing Out.' It is a biological urge to jump into a stampede. In investing, the stampede usually runs off a cliff.

## The Middle Class Trap
Buying a stock only after it has already gone up 500% and everyone on social media is talking about it.

## The Leverage Play
Being 'Fearful when others are greedy, and greedy when others are fearful.' You ignore the hype and follow the math.

## The Superpower
Psychological iron.

## The Actionable Cent
The next time everyone is talking about a 'hot tip,' walk away and do your own research.
