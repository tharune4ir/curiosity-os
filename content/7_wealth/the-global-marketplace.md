## The Wealth Secret
Thanks to the internet, you aren't just selling to your neighbors. You are selling to 8 billion people across the planet.

## The Middle Class Trap
Thinking small and local, competing with the shop next door instead of building a product that helps someone in Japan or Brazil.

## The Leverage Play
Selling a digital art pack on a global site like Gumroad. You earn in dollars while living in a place where rupees go further.

## The Superpower
Unlimited customer base.

## The Actionable Cent
Realize that someone on the other side of the world probably has the exact same problem you do.
