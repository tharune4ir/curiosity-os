## The Wealth Secret
Safety isn't about avoiding all risks; it's about being able to survive the risks you take. A ship is safe in the harbor, but that's not what ships are for.

## The Middle Class Trap
Staying in a dead-end job or keeping all money in a savings account because it feels 'safe,' while inflation slowly kills their future.

## The Leverage Play
Taking calculated risks, like starting a side hustle or investing in stocks, because you have used your emergency fund to build a safety net.

## The Superpower
Calculated courage.

## The Actionable Cent
Identify one 'safe' thing you are doing that is actually holding you back.
