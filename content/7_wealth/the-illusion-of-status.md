## The Wealth Secret
Status games are a trap. To look like a winner, someone else must look like a loser. People buy things to impress people they don't even like.

## The Middle Class Trap
Financing a 1 Lakh iPhone on an EMI plan just to project a fake social standing to peers, while actually being broke.

## The Leverage Play
Ignoring the status game completely. Realizing true wealth is invisible (it is the assets you haven't sold, the cars you didn't buy).

## The Superpower
Social immunity.

## The Actionable Cent
Reflect on a recent purchase: did you buy it for utility, or for how it makes you look?
