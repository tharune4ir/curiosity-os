## The Wealth Secret
Building wealth isn't a race you finish; it's a game you keep playing. You want to make decisions today that keep you in the game tomorrow.

## The Middle Class Trap
Sacrificing their health, family, or integrity for a short-term 'win,' only to find the game is empty once it's over.

## The Leverage Play
Choosing a career and projects that you genuinely enjoy so much that you would do them even if you were already a billionaire.

## The Superpower
Endless motivation.

## The Actionable Cent
Ask yourself if you would still want to do your main project if it took five more years to succeed.
