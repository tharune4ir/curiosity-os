## The Wealth Secret
The choice between spending money now to feel good today, or planting it to be free forever. Every rupee is a vote for your future self.

## The Middle Class Trap
Thinking that 'life is short' is an excuse to spend everything today, ignoring the fact that life is also very long if you are broke at 70.

## The Leverage Play
Automating your investments so you never even see the money. You can't spend what you don't have in your wallet.

## The Superpower
Temporal balance.

## The Actionable Cent
Determine one thing you spend money on that gives you zero happiness 24 hours later.
