## The Wealth Secret
Wealth is what you don't see. An investor is a business owner who lets the smartest people in the world work for them.

## The Middle Class Trap
Thinking like a consumer who asks 'How much does it cost?' instead of an investor who asks 'What is the return?'

## The Leverage Play
Treating every ₹100 as a tiny employee. You don't spend it; you hire it to go and bring back ₹5 more at the end of the year.

## The Superpower
Patient observation.

## The Actionable Cent
Decide today to become a part-owner of the world's best companies through stocks.
