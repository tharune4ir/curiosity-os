## The Wealth Secret
The idea that saving ₹100 on coffee will make you a millionaire. It's a distraction. Small savings don't matter if your big expenses are wrong.

## The Middle Class Trap
Stressfully cutting out small joys like a ₹50 coffee while overpaying ₹5 Lakhs for a house or ₹2 Lakhs for a car they don't need.

## The Leverage Play
Focusing on the 'Big Three': Housing, Transport, and Taxes. If you get those right, you can buy as many lattes as you want.

## The Superpower
Priority focus.

## The Actionable Cent
Identify your top 3 largest monthly expenses and see if you can reduce the biggest one.
