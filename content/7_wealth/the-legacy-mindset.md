## The Wealth Secret
Building something that outlives you. Not just money, but ideas, systems, and values that help people even after you are gone.

## The Middle Class Trap
Living entirely for themselves, focusing only on their own consumption and short-term comfort.

## The Leverage Play
Writing a book or building a business that operates on principles that can continue to provide value for 100 years.

## The Superpower
Transcendence.

## The Actionable Cent
Think of one idea you would like to be remembered for.
