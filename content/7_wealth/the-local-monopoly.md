## The Wealth Secret
If you are the absolute best at one very specific thing in your town or niche, you can charge whatever you want.

## The Middle Class Trap
Trying to be 'okay' at everything, ending up exactly the same as ten thousand other kids applying for the exact same job.

## The Leverage Play
Becoming the only kid in your entire city who knows how to repair and customize high-end drones.

## The Superpower
Price control.

## The Actionable Cent
Find a weird, microscopic niche that nobody else in your school is paying attention to.
