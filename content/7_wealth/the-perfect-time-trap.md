## The Wealth Secret
Waiting for the 'perfect market conditions' or a 'higher salary' before beginning to invest. 'Next month' never actually arrives.

## The Middle Class Trap
The procrastinator who earns 2 Lakh but spends it all, constantly promising to start investing 'once things settle down'.

## The Leverage Play
The disciplined teenager who automates a simple 500 SIP today, mathematically outperforming the high-earning procrastinator over thirty years.

## The Superpower
Automated discipline.

## The Actionable Cent
Set up a system where a fraction of your allowance is locked away on the 1st of every month.
