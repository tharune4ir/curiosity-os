## The Wealth Secret
Every 'Yes' you say to a low-value project or a boring social event is a 'No' to your own dreams. Guard your focus like a dragon guards gold.

## The Middle Class Trap
Trying to please everyone and saying 'Yes' to every request, ending up with a calendar full of other people's priorities.

## The Leverage Play
Having a 'Default No' policy. You only say 'Yes' to things that are a 'Hell Yeah!' Everything else is a distraction from your mission.

## The Superpower
Focused velocity.

## The Actionable Cent
Identify one thing you said 'Yes' to this week that you actually wanted to say 'No' to.
