## The Wealth Secret
Every time you keep a promise, you make a deposit. Every time you lie or fail, you make a massive withdrawal.

## The Middle Class Trap
Thinking they can 'cheat' the system or cut corners to make a quick buck, not realizing they are bankrupting their future reputation.

## The Leverage Play
Being the person who always delivers high-quality work on time. People will pay a premium just to work with someone they trust.

## The Superpower
Brand equity.

## The Actionable Cent
Do exactly what you said you would do today, even if it's a small task.
