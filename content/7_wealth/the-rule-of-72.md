## The Wealth Secret
Divide 72 by your interest rate, and that is exactly how many years it takes for your money to double.

## The Middle Class Trap
Trusting a bank manager's confusing words instead of doing 5 seconds of simple math to see if an investment is good.

## The Leverage Play
Realizing that a 6% bank FD takes 12 years to double, but a 12% mutual fund takes only 6 years. You choose the faster clone.

## The Superpower
Instant financial x-ray vision.

## The Actionable Cent
Apply the Rule of 72 to your parents' savings account rate.
