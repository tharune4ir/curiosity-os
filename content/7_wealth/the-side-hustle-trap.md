## The Wealth Secret
Engaging in low-yield, time-consuming secondary jobs that distract from building true, scalable equity.

## The Middle Class Trap
Spending weekends doing manual data entry for pennies, burning out without actually increasing specific knowledge or leverage.

## The Leverage Play
Foregoing the pennies to spend weekends learning code or building media assets that will eventually generate massive rupees automatically.

## The Superpower
Strategic patience.

## The Actionable Cent
Evaluate if a current task is trading time for linear pennies or building an exponential system.
