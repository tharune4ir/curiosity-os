## The Wealth Secret
The cost of an item isn't just the price tag. It's the cost of the storage, the insurance, the maintenance, and the mental energy it takes to own it.

## The Middle Class Trap
Filling their house with 'stuff' that requires constant cleaning and care, becoming a slave to their own possessions.

## The Leverage Play
Practicing 'Minimalism.' Owning only what you truly love or truly need, freeing up your physical and mental space for creating value.

## The Superpower
Mental lightness.

## The Actionable Cent
Sell or giveaway five things in your room that you haven't used in a year.
