## The Wealth Secret
You are the average of the five people you spend the most time with. If your friends talk about gossip, you'll stay small. If they talk about assets, you'll grow.

## The Middle Class Trap
Staying in a social circle that mocks ambition or thinks building wealth is 'evil' or 'too hard.'

## The Leverage Play
Joining a Discord or a group of other teens who are actively building businesses or learning high-value skills like coding.

## The Superpower
Environmental gravity.

## The Actionable Cent
Find one person who is further ahead in their journey than you and follow their work.
