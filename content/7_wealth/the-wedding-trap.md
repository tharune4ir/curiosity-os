## The Wealth Secret
Spending decades of potential wealth on a single multi-day party due to societal pressure and the fear of community judgment.

## The Middle Class Trap
Taking a 30 lakh loan for a lavish wedding to satisfy cultural norms, creating a decade-long EMI burden that destroys your future.

## The Leverage Play
Opting for a modest ceremony and deploying the massive saved capital as a down payment on an asset or seeding an investment portfolio.

## The Superpower
Generational wealth.

## The Actionable Cent
Calculate the future value of 30 lakh compounded at 10% over twenty years.
