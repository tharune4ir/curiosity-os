## The Wealth Secret
A ₹100 note today is worth way more than a ₹100 note ten years from now because today's money can be planted and grown.

## The Middle Class Trap
Caring only about how much you save, completely ignoring when you start saving.

## The Leverage Play
Demanding to be paid today for your freelance work instead of next month, so you can put the money to work immediately.

## The Superpower
Mathematical speed.

## The Actionable Cent
Choose to invest today rather than buying a fast-food meal.
