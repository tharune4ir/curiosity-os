## The Wealth Secret
A computer can be a toy (playing games, watching Netflix) or a tool (writing code, editing videos, building wealth).

## The Middle Class Trap
Spending 1 Lakh on a gaming PC and using it exclusively to play games, generating absolutely zero value from a powerful machine.

## The Leverage Play
Using the exact same gaming PC to render high-quality 3D animations and selling them as a freelance artist online.

## The Superpower
Asset conversion.

## The Actionable Cent
Look at your smartphone. Are you using it 90% as a toy or as a tool?
