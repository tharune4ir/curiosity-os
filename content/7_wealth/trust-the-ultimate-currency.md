## The Wealth Secret
Low trust environments are expensive (lawyers, contracts, delays). High trust relationships move at the speed of light.

## The Middle Class Trap
Being 'clever' or 'sneaky' to win a small transaction today, failing to see that they just destroyed a billion-dollar relationship.

## The Leverage Play
Building a reputation for being 'too honest.' People will give you money, secrets, and opportunities because they know you aren't a snake.

## The Superpower
Transaction speed.

## The Actionable Cent
Be 100% honest in a situation today where it might be slightly inconvenient to do so.
