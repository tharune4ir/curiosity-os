## The Wealth Secret
The ultimate definition of wealth is the ability to wake up and say, 'I can do whatever I want today.' Money is just the tool to buy that 'Yes.'

## The Middle Class Trap
Thinking wealth is about having a gold-plated toilet, not realizing that a billionaire who works 100 hours a week at a job they hate is actually poor.

## The Leverage Play
Designing your life to have low fixed costs and high passive income, so the 'Freedom' part of the equation is solved as early as possible.

## The Superpower
Total autonomy.

## The Actionable Cent
What is the first thing you would do today if money was no longer an issue?
