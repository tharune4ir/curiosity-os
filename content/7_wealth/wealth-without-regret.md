## The Wealth Secret
There is no point in having ₹100 Crores if you have zero friends, an unhealthy body, and a guilty conscience.

## The Middle Class Trap
Sacrificing their physical health or their family relationships in the pursuit of 'making it,' only to realize they traded a diamond for a rock.

## The Leverage Play
Building wealth 'Holistically.' You exercise, you spend time with loved ones, and you grow your assets at the same time.

## The Superpower
Total fulfillment.

## The Actionable Cent
Ensure you did something for your health and your soul today.
