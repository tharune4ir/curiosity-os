## The Wealth Secret
The first million is the hardest because you are still learning the game. Every million after that is just the machine running on autopilot.

## The Middle Class Trap
Giving up when it feels too hard in the beginning, not realizing that the growth is exponential, not linear.

## The Leverage Play
Staying focused and disciplined for the first 5 years. Once you break the barrier, the wind starts blowing at your back.

## The Superpower
Inertia.

## The Actionable Cent
Commit to staying in the game for at least 3 years before you judge your results.
