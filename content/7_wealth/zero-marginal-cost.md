## The Wealth Secret
The cost to produce the first video is high (time). The cost to distribute the one-millionth copy is absolutely zero.

## The Middle Class Trap
Starting a physical business (like baking cakes) where making the 100th cake requires the exact same sweat and ingredients as the first.

## The Leverage Play
Shifting to digital products (like a recipe ebook) where selling the 100th copy costs absolutely nothing in time or materials.

## The Superpower
Infinite profit margins.

## The Actionable Cent
Compare the business model of a physical bookshop to an app store.
