## The Invisible Architecture
Every single thing inside your computer — your name, your selfie, your favorite song — is stored as a combination of just two symbols: 0 and 1. Why? Because inside the computer are billions of tiny switches. A switch can only be OFF (0) or ON (1). By combining millions of these switches in patterns, computers can represent any number, letter, color, or sound. Your name 'Ravi' is just a specific pattern of millions of tiny light switches being ON or OFF.

## The Consumer Trap
People think computers are magical and mysterious. They have no idea that at the deepest level, everything is just a ridiculous number of light switches flipping on and off incredibly fast.

## The Builder Hack
A builder converting an image for a website knows that a high-resolution photo is just millions and millions of 0s and 1s. By compressing the image (cleverly reducing the switches needed), they make their website load 10x faster — which means better Google ranking and happier users.

## The Superpower
Nothing in computing feels like magic anymore. If it's inside a computer, it's just 0s and 1s. This gives you a calm, fearless relationship with technology.

## The First Line Of Code
Google 'binary translator'. Type your name. See it transform into its 0 and 1 form. That pattern IS your name to the machine.
