## The Black Box Revealed
AI cannot be creative in the original human sense — it has no lived experience, no desires, no emotional truth. But it can massively amplify the creative signal that you provide. If you have a spark of an idea, AI can explore 100 variations in minutes. If you have a style, AI can apply it across infinite contexts. Think of AI as a creative energy amplifier: it takes your 1-watt creative signal and broadcasts it at 1,000 watts. But if your signal is zero, it amplifies zero.

## The Lazy Human Trap
Students ask AI to 'be creative' on their behalf. AI creativity is statistically average creativity — it tends toward the center of all creative work it has seen. Real creative breakthroughs come from individual human experience, unconventional perspectives, and emotional truth. These are yours to provide.

## The Symbiosis Hack
Develop your creative seed library. Carry a small notebook and write down: unusual things you noticed today, lines from a dream you half-remember, a conversation fragment that stuck with you, a feeling you could not explain. These are creative seeds that AI cannot generate on its own. Bring one seed to AI and ask it to grow 20 variations. Then choose the one that feels most true to your experience.

## The Future Proof Superpower
Human creative originality — rooted in unique experience, culture, and emotion — will be the scarcest resource in a world flooded with AI-generated content. The creative person who can provide the unique human signal and amplify it with AI will create work that stands out in a sea of the generic and average.

## The Prompt Experiment
Write 3 sentences describing a very specific personal memory — a smell, a feeling, a moment. Share them with AI and say: 'Use the emotional texture and specific sensory details of these sentences to write a short poem about a completely different topic I will give you: [choose a topic].' The AI uses your emotional specificity as a creative template.
