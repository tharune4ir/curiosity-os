## The Black Box Revealed
A feedback loop is a cycle where output becomes input for the next cycle. In learning, the most powerful feedback loops are: attempt → get specific feedback → improve → attempt again. AI makes this loop faster than any human tutor can. You can run 10 feedback cycles in an hour — getting precise, personalized feedback on each iteration. This is the learning superpower AI enables when used actively, not passively.

## The Lazy Human Trap
Students use AI in one-shot mode: ask once, get answer, done. They do not iterate, do not get feedback on their work, do not improve the same piece multiple times. The power is in the loop, not the single exchange. Single-shot AI use is like going to the gym once and expecting to get fit.

## The Symbiosis Hack
Apply the 'Three Loop Rule': For any piece of work, run at least 3 AI feedback cycles before submitting. Cycle 1: AI evaluates the structure and logic. Cycle 2: AI evaluates the language and clarity. Cycle 3: AI plays the role of your strictest teacher and finds remaining weaknesses. Three loops will transform anything from average to excellent.

## The Future Proof Superpower
Rapid iteration is the defining feature of effective product development, scientific research, and creative production. The fastest learners and most successful creators run more loops than everyone else. AI compresses the time per loop from days (waiting for human feedback) to seconds. Use this compression strategically.

## The Prompt Experiment
Take a paragraph you have written. Run it through 3 feedback loops with AI, making genuine edits after each round. Take a screenshot of the original and the final version. Compare them side by side. This visual comparison will show you the concrete learning value of iteration.
