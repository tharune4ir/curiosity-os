## The Black Box Revealed
Superalignment is a research agenda (notably championed by OpenAI before significant internal changes) that aims to solve the problem of aligning AI systems that are smarter than humans — before those systems exist. The key challenge: if AI is smarter than any human, humans cannot fully verify whether the AI is aligned with their values. It is like asking a student to grade their own exam when the student is smarter than all the teachers. New mathematical approaches are needed to verify AI alignment without relying on humans being smarter than the AI.

## The Lazy Human Trap
People think AI safety can wait until we have super-intelligent AI. But safety techniques must be developed and tested on today's systems so they are ready to scale. Developing alignment methods for superintelligence by studying superintelligence is too late — it is like developing safety procedures for a nuclear plant by studying the accident that already happened.

## The Symbiosis Hack
Follow interpretability research — the effort to understand what is happening inside AI neural networks by studying activation patterns and attention heads. Tools like Anthropic's interpretability work and academic research in mechanistic interpretability are trying to make the black box less black. This is genuinely exciting mathematics and computer science that undergraduate students can contribute to.

## The Future Proof Superpower
If superintelligence is possible (a genuine uncertainty), then solving superalignment before it arrives may be the most important technical project in human history. A student who builds expertise in alignment and interpretability now will be working on problems at the absolute frontier of human knowledge — regardless of how the AI timeline unfolds.

## The Prompt Experiment
Ask AI: 'Explain the scalable oversight approach to superalignment. How does debate and amplification work as a solution to the verification problem? What are its weaknesses?' See if it can explain cutting-edge alignment research accurately and clearly.
