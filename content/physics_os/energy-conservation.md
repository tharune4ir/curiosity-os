## The Invisible Reality
Energy can never be created or destroyed – it only changes form. If you push a swing, the push transforms your muscle energy into the swing’s motion (kinetic energy). When the swing rises, it stores energy (potential). If it rubs or hits the air, some energy becomes heat or sound. In all cases, the total energy stays the same【61†L579-L588】. This hidden accounting keeps track of every motion, heat, and light in the universe.

## The Everyday Encounter
When you ride a bicycle downhill, you feel how your speed (energy of motion) increases even though you didn’t pedal harder. That extra speed came from the hill’s height (stored energy). Or when you brake, the bike slows and the brake pads heat up – you converted kinetic energy into heat. Boiling water on the stove is another example: chemical energy from the fuel (or electricity) changes into heat in the pot.

## The Sub-Landscapes
1. Kinetic vs Potential: Moving objects have kinetic energy, raised objects have potential energy. 2. Work & Transfer: Pushing, lifting, or heating moves energy from one form to another (e.g., electrical work runs a fan). 3. Conservation Law: In a closed system, energy just switches forms; the total amount stays fixed.

## The God Mode Tech
Power plants, engines, and batteries all rely on energy conservation. For example, coal or nuclear power plants convert heat to electricity, but the total of heat+electricity equals the fuel’s chemical (or nuclear) energy. Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity. Understanding this law was key to designing all our engines and electric grids.

## The Fascinating Truth
Even the entire universe follows this rule. The total energy left over from the Big Bang is still here today in some form. According to physics, energy in a closed system remains constant【61†L579-L588】. That means the energy in your phone, your car, and our Sun all ultimately came from transformations of the same primordial energy.
