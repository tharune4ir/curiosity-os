## The Matrix Truth
Factory processes are going digital: 3D printing, robotics, and AI in production lines. Manufacturing is moving from cheap labor to smart automation.

## The Leverage
Anyone who masters factory automation can outcompete traditional producers. It’s how countries reclaim industry and how startups can prototype fast.

## Skill Stack
CAD design, robotics, additive manufacturing, control systems.

## Economic Reality
Industrial engineer, 3D printing entrepreneur, operations manager. Capital-intensive but vital for modern economies.

## Proof of Work
Design and 3D-print a small part (like a bracket). Assemble it into a functional device (like a phone stand) and document the build.
