## The Matrix Truth
We are in the golden age of space: rockets are reusable, satellites are swarms, and deep-space missions are planned. Space is becoming accessible to private industry.

## The Leverage
Building rockets or satellites means joining trillion-dollar space markets. It’s ultimate high ground for national security, communications, and exploration.

## Skill Stack
Flight mechanics, propulsion, structural engineering, orbital dynamics.

## Economic Reality
Aerospace engineer, launch startup founder, satellite operator. Huge initial investment, government contracts.

## Proof of Work
Simulate a simple rocket flight: use software to model trajectory of a rocket or drone. Validate results and refine your design.
