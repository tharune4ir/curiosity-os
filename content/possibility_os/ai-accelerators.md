## The Matrix Truth
Special chips (like GPUs and TPUs) speed up AI models enormously. Hardware design is catching up to the needs of deep learning.

## The Leverage
Designing or using specialized AI hardware gives you a speed advantage. It’s crucial for deploying cutting-edge models in real time.

## Skill Stack
Computer architecture, VLSI design, parallel programming, CUDA/OpenCL.

## Economic Reality
Chip designer, cloud computing architect, AI lab engineer. Large tech companies dominate hardware, but startups innovate too.

## Proof of Work
Benchmark a small neural network on GPU vs CPU. Show how much faster the GPU is, explaining the parallel computations.
