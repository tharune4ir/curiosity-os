## The Matrix Truth
Stocks and crypto are now often traded by algorithms reacting in milliseconds. Complex math models scan data and execute trades without human intervention.

## The Leverage
A good trading model can make or lose millions. Mastering it means financial markets are at your fingertips.

## Skill Stack
Statistics, machine learning, financial markets, Python/R.

## Economic Reality
Quantitative analyst, hedge fund trader, fintech entrepreneur. High-risk/high-reward, needs real market data.

## Proof of Work
Backtest a simple trading strategy (like moving average crossover) on historical stock data. Show the results of your simulation.
