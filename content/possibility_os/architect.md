## The Invisible Reality
Architects design buildings with both art and math. They imagine how a structure will stand and look. It's like drawing a house in Lego on paper: they sketch how each part fits. They use geometry to calculate sizes and ensure safety, making sure the design is both beautiful and strong.

## The Mind-Blowing Hook
Famous buildings around the world (like skyscrapers or museums) come from architects' blueprints. With modern tools, architects create 3D models on computers. They also consider environment: sustainable architecture is growing, where buildings generate energy and use eco-friendly materials.

## The Player's Life
You meet clients to discuss needs, then draw plans using CAD software. You collaborate with engineers to ensure structure can be built. You also oversee construction, ensuring workers follow your design. Each day involves sketching, modeling, and communicating ideas clearly.

## The Skill Tree
Drawing and design skills, understanding of physics, and attention to detail.
