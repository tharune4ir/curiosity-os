## The Matrix Truth
AI gives machines the ability to learn and make decisions. Your smartphone, cars, and apps will all use AI to get smarter.

## The Leverage
If you can build AI, you can automate creative work and decision-making. Every industry needs AI experts to stay competitive in the future.

## Skill Stack
Machine learning (Python, TensorFlow), statistics, data engineering, neural networks.

## Economic Reality
AI engineer, data scientist, tech startup founder. Software side has low setup cost but high competition.

## Proof of Work
Train a neural network on a simple dataset (e.g., classify images of animals) and post your model and code.
