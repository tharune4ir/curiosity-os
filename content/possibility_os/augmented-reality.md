## The Matrix Truth
AR overlays digital content onto the real world. With a smartphone or glasses, data becomes part of our physical environment.

## The Leverage
Creating AR applications changes how people work and play by blending real and virtual. It’s a frontier in marketing, education, and enterprise tech.

## Skill Stack
Computer vision, mobile development, 3D graphics, UX design.

## Economic Reality
AR developer, UX designer, marketing technologist. Platforms like smartphones make entry easier, but hardware limits persist.

## Proof of Work
Build a simple AR app using a toolkit (like ARKit/ARCore) that places a virtual object on a real surface, like a table. Share your demo.
