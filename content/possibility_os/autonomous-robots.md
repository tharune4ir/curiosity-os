## The Matrix Truth
Robots will navigate and act without humans directing every move. From drones to factory bots, autonomy is the next step in robotics.

## The Leverage
Perfect an autonomous system and you create a 24/7 worker for dangerous or repetitive tasks. Defense, delivery, and exploration industries pay top dollar.

## Skill Stack
AI for robotics, sensor fusion, control systems, path planning.

## Economic Reality
Autonomy engineer, robotics consultant, defense tech developer. Early adopters get big contracts, tech is evolving fast.

## Proof of Work
Program a robot (like a robot vacuum or small drone) to navigate a simple course using sensors, then improve its efficiency.
