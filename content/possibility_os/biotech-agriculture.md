## The Matrix Truth
Crops are being engineered for climate and nutrition. From drought-resistant genes to lab-grown meat, biology boosts farming output.

## The Leverage
Solve food security by engineering plants or cells. You tap into a market of billions and fight hunger with tech.

## Skill Stack
Genetic engineering, plant physiology, data analytics, sustainable farming.

## Economic Reality
Agritech engineer, GMO crop specialist, sustainable farmer. Partnerships with big agriculture, shifting regulations.

## Proof of Work
Research a drought-resistant crop variant. Analyze how its yield compares under normal vs low-water conditions.
