## The Matrix Truth
As biotech advances, ethical questions arise (e.g., designer babies, bio-hacking). Society debates what should be allowed.

## The Leverage
Guiding ethical biotech development is crucial for public acceptance. Experts influence regulations and public opinion.

## Skill Stack
Philosophy, biology basics, law, public speaking.

## Economic Reality
Bioethicist, policy advisor, academic researcher. Often in advisory roles or NGOs.

## Proof of Work
Write a reasoned essay on one biotech ethical dilemma (like CRISPR babies) presenting pros and cons.
