## The Matrix Truth
Fabrication using living cells: growing leather in vats, printing organs on bio-printers. Biology is the new manufacturing material.

## The Leverage
A biotech fab specialist can create sustainable products of any kind. It’s like programming factories made of cells.

## Skill Stack
Tissue engineering, bioprinting, synthetic biology, process engineering.

## Economic Reality
Bioprinting engineer, clean protein producer, biotech CEO. Very capital-intensive, but biotech labs are springing up.

## Proof of Work
Research a bioprinting method and draft a plan to print a simple tissue (like a liver patch) outlining materials and steps.
