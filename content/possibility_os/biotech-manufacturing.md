## The Matrix Truth
Manufacturing is going biological: using cells to produce drugs, fuels, and materials. Bioreactors replace factories for certain products.

## The Leverage
Engineering biology to make products cheaply can disrupt oil and pharma industries. It’s like programming factories using living machines.

## Skill Stack
Bioprocess engineering, genetic engineering, fermentation tech, quality control.

## Economic Reality
Process engineer, biomanufacturing manager, pharmaceutical scientist. Large facilities, strict regulations, but high demand.

## Proof of Work
Plan a small fermentation experiment: grow yeast or bacteria with a target output (like bioethanol) and measure yield changes with conditions.
