## The Matrix Truth
Engineering living systems at scale: biofuels, engineered bacteria, lab-grown meat. It blends biology with technology for industry and health.

## The Leverage
Leading in biotech gives access to global pharma and food markets. You turn biology into factories for products that used to be rare or expensive.

## Skill Stack
Genetics, bio-process engineering, fermentation, data analysis.

## Economic Reality
Biotech engineer, pharmaceutical researcher, agritech entrepreneur. Intensive lab work, strict regulation.

## Proof of Work
Grow yeast with different sugar sources and measure fermentation. Use those results to optimize biofuel or baking processes.
