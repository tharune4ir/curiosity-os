## The Matrix Truth
Blockchain is a distributed ledger that no single party controls. It enables trust-less transactions and smart contracts on the internet.

## The Leverage
If you master decentralized systems, you can transform finance, supply chains, and property records. It’s a new foundation for digital trust.

## Skill Stack
Cryptography, distributed systems, smart contract programming (Solidity), decentralized apps.

## Economic Reality
Blockchain developer, crypto entrepreneur, financial technologist. Volatile markets, but huge investment flows.

## Proof of Work
Write a simple smart contract (e.g., a token or voting system) and deploy it on a test blockchain network. Show it working.
