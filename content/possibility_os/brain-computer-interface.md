## The Matrix Truth
Devices can connect directly to our brain signals. This could let disabled people control computers or even enable 'telepathy' between minds.

## The Leverage
Mastering this tech means bridging biology and machines. It’s a field with huge impact on medicine and human ability.

## Skill Stack
Neuroscience, signal processing, embedded systems, machine learning.

## Economic Reality
BCI researcher, neurotech entrepreneur, medical device engineer. High R&D costs, pioneering field.

## Proof of Work
Use an open EEG dataset and write code to interpret a brain signal (like distinguishing left vs right imagined movement). Share findings.
