## The Matrix Truth
The long-term goal: simulate a whole human brain in a computer. This is extremely complex, but even partial models advance neuroscience and AI.

## The Leverage
Leading brain emulation projects could redefine AI by recreating intelligence. It’s a moonshot that attracts top neuroscientists and engineers.

## Skill Stack
Neuroscience, computational modeling, high-performance computing, data management.

## Economic Reality
Neuroscientist, AI research lead, supercomputing engineer. Mostly academic and national projects.

## Proof of Work
Simulate a small neural network that mimics a simple brain function (like pattern recognition). Explain how it parallels biology.
