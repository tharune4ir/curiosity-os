## The Matrix Truth
Economics designed to reuse all resources: recycling, upcycling, and waste-to-energy. Products are designed for durability and recyclability from the start.

## The Leverage
Creating circular business models means winning in sustainability. Consumers and governments push for it, rewarding innovators.

## Skill Stack
Supply chain, materials science, design, environmental economics.

## Economic Reality
Sustainability consultant, startup founder, government advisor. Supported by green policies and emerging markets.

## Proof of Work
Analyze the lifecycle of a common product (like a phone): how materials flow and what recycling would involve. Suggest improvements.
