## The Matrix Truth
Economic models are adjusting for climate impact and carbon costs. Investments flow into sustainability as we price emissions and build green markets.

## The Leverage
Designing economic incentives for climate solutions shapes policy worldwide. You enable companies to profit from cleaning the planet.

## Skill Stack
Econometrics, policy analysis, environmental science, finance.

## Economic Reality
Climate economist, sustainability consultant, impact investor. Work with governments or green funds.

## Proof of Work
Analyze carbon tax or renewable subsidy data from a country. Show its effect on emissions or clean energy growth.
