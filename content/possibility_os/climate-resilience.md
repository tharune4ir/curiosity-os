## The Matrix Truth
Designing systems (cities, agriculture, infrastructure) to withstand climate impacts (floods, heat). Resilience means adapting to change, not just preventing it.

## The Leverage
Experts here help societies survive disasters. You’ll collaborate with governments and companies on multi-billion dollar infrastructure.

## Skill Stack
Risk analysis, civil engineering, urban planning, environmental science.

## Economic Reality
Consultant, policy advisor, city planner. Often publicly funded projects with long timelines.

## Proof of Work
Model an extreme weather event impact: e.g., simulate how a city’s drainage holds up in a heavy rain and suggest improvements.
