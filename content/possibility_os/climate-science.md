## The Matrix Truth
Studying the climate system and human impact on it is urgent. Modeling weather, ice melt, and carbon cycles informs every policy.

## The Leverage
Scientists who predict climate outcomes guide global strategy. It’s both academic prestige and lifesaving work as the planet changes.

## Skill Stack
Climate modeling, oceanography, physics, data analysis.

## Economic Reality
Climate modeler, environmental consultant, research scientist. Mostly funded by governments and NGOs.

## Proof of Work
Run a basic climate model (many open tools exist) to see how CO2 levels affect temperature over decades. Plot your results.
