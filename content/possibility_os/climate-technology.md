## The Matrix Truth
We need tech to combat climate change: carbon capture, resilient crops, geoengineering. Science and engineering are at war with rising temperatures.

## The Leverage
Solving climate tech is solving humanity’s biggest crisis. Innovations here get government and private support like nothing else.

## Skill Stack
Environmental science, engineering, data analytics, policy understanding.

## Economic Reality
Climate engineer, environmental startup founder, sustainability consultant. Growing funding, complex regulations.

## Proof of Work
Analyze local weather or CO2 data (satellite or ground sensors) to spot a trend. Propose a tech solution like a green roof or sensor network.
