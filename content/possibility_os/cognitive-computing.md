## The Matrix Truth
This is AI that mimics human thought (like IBM Watson). It involves language understanding, reasoning, and memory in software.

## The Leverage
Building cognitive systems allows automation of expert tasks (like medical diagnosis). It’s next-level AI for decision support.

## Skill Stack
Machine learning, NLP, knowledge representation, software architecture.

## Economic Reality
AI researcher, enterprise AI consultant, tech product manager. Often tied to big data analytics in corporations.

## Proof of Work
Use a library (NLTK or spaCy) to build a simple Q&A chatbot or sentiment analyzer. Share example interactions.
