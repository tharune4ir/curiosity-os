## The Matrix Truth
Content strategy is planning what stories to tell across platforms. Good content attracts attention in social media and marketing.

## The Leverage
Crafting narratives that resonate can build brands and movements. It’s how ideas gain traction online.

## Skill Stack
Writing, SEO, audience research, analytics.

## Economic Reality
Content strategist, copywriter, marketing consultant. Freelance or agency work, very low initial investment.

## Proof of Work
Create and publish a blog post or short video on a tech topic, then analyze how many people it reached and why.
