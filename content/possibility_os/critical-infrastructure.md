## The Matrix Truth
Power grids, water supply, and communications are critical infrastructure. Digitizing and protecting them is national security.

## The Leverage
Working here means you influence basic services. Investing in infrastructure tech yields stable returns and strategic importance.

## Skill Stack
Civil engineering, network security, systems engineering, policy knowledge.

## Economic Reality
Infrastructure planner, utility engineer, security auditor. Government-regulated, long-term projects.

## Proof of Work
Analyze your city’s power grid or water system: map components and suggest where sensors or analytics could improve reliability.
