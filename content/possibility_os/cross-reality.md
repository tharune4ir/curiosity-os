## The Matrix Truth
Cross reality blends AR, VR, and MR (mixed reality). It will let users jump between worlds seamlessly or have mixed experiences.

## The Leverage
Creating cross reality experiences opens new media formats and collaboration spaces. It’s the next evolution of the internet interface.

## Skill Stack
Unity/Unreal, 3D design, spatial computing, networking.

## Economic Reality
XR developer, multimedia artist, tech evangelist. Platforms still maturing, but funding pouring in.

## Proof of Work
Prototype an AR/VR demo: for example, place a virtual character in your living room using an AR framework. Record and share it.
