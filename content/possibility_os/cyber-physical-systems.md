## The Matrix Truth
These systems integrate computation with physical processes. Examples include smart factories and robotic assembly lines connected by the Internet.

## The Leverage
Build expertise in CPS and you can automate entire industries. It’s where software meets machines and yields new efficiency.

## Skill Stack
Embedded systems, real-time software, sensors, control engineering.

## Economic Reality
Systems engineer, industrial automation specialist, IoT solutions architect. Large industrial clients, safety-critical work.

## Proof of Work
Connect a sensor to a microcontroller and make it trigger an action (like a buzzer). Demonstrate data flow from physical to digital.
