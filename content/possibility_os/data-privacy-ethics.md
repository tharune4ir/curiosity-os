## The Matrix Truth
As we collect more data, ethical rules and privacy laws become key. Misuse of data can lead to scandals; trust and law are critical.

## The Leverage
Guiding tech development with ethics gains user trust and avoids legal trouble. Experts shape policies and standards in every industry.

## Skill Stack
Ethics, law (GDPR), security, communication.

## Economic Reality
Compliance officer, ethicist, privacy consultant. Often in tech or government roles, increasingly important.

## Proof of Work
Review a recent data privacy incident (like a major breach). Summarize what went wrong and propose a policy fix.
