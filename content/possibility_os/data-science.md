## The Matrix Truth
Data is the new raw material. Analyzing massive datasets reveals patterns hidden to the human eye.

## The Leverage
If you can extract insights from data, you guide business and research with facts. It’s like having a microscope on the whole economy.

## Skill Stack
Statistics, Python (pandas, scikit-learn), SQL, data visualization.

## Economic Reality
Data analyst, machine learning engineer, consultant. High demand across industries, moderate entry cost.

## Proof of Work
Find a public data set (COVID trends, stock prices, sports stats) and build a model that predicts a key outcome. Share your code.
