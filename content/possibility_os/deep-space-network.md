## The Matrix Truth
We now have networks that communicate with spacecraft at vast distances. This enables Mars rovers, interplanetary probes, and future moon bases.

## The Leverage
Building space communication systems means defining the infrastructure of space exploration. It’s a niche but high-impact field.

## Skill Stack
RF engineering, astronomy, data transmission, network planning.

## Economic Reality
Aerospace engineer, network architect for space, research scientist. Large government and NGO projects.

## Proof of Work
Simulate a simple satellite link: calculate latency and data rate between Earth and a Mars-distance spacecraft using given formulas.
