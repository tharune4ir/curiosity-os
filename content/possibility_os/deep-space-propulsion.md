## The Matrix Truth
Beyond chemical rockets: ion drives, nuclear thermal, or light sails. These advanced engines could reach other planets in months or years.

## The Leverage
If you develop a breakthrough engine, you open interplanetary travel. It’s both a national prestige and a commercial space frontier.

## Skill Stack
Plasma physics, aerospace engineering, nuclear engineering, simulations.

## Economic Reality
Aerospace propulsion researcher, space startup CTO. High R&D budgets (space agencies), long development.

## Proof of Work
Simulate a rocket: use a model to compare chemical vs ion propulsion over long distances, showing travel time differences.
