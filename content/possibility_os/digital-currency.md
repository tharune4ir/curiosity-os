## The Matrix Truth
Cryptocurrencies and central bank digital currencies are changing money. Cash is moving to secure digital forms controlled by code or government.

## The Leverage
Understanding digital money lets you create new financial products and markets. It’s a chance to redefine how value is stored and exchanged.

## Skill Stack
Cryptography, economics, blockchain development, regulation.

## Economic Reality
Crypto trader, blockchain startup founder, central bank analyst. Wild volatility, high risk, but major hedge funds involved.
