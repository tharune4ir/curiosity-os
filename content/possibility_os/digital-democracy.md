## The Matrix Truth
Technology is changing politics: online campaigns, e-voting, and data-driven policy. Voting and governance platforms can engage citizens globally.

## The Leverage
Building fair digital voting systems or civic tech changes how societies function. It’s high impact for good governance and societal trust.

## Skill Stack
Software development, political science, cybersecurity, UX design.

## Economic Reality
Civic technologist, policy analyst, NGO leader. Influence can come from lean startups or government alliances.

## Proof of Work
Design a simple voting app mockup with security features (like anonymous tokens). Or propose an online town hall format.
