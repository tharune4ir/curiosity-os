## The Matrix Truth
Marketing is now driven by data: social ads, SEO, influencers, and analytics. Companies compete for your attention online using very targeted strategies.

## The Leverage
Anyone who understands digital reach can start a profitable venture or help businesses grow fast. It’s one of the quickest ways to scale a product today.

## Skill Stack
SEO/SEM, content strategy, analytics tools, social media advertising.

## Economic Reality
Digital marketer, growth hacker, startup founder. Low entry cost, high competition, measurable ROI.

## Proof of Work
Create a small online campaign: pick a topic and use free tools (like Google Trends and social media) to reach 100 readers or views. Report results.
