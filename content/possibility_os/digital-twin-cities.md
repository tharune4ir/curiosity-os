## The Matrix Truth
Creating a full digital replica of a city to simulate traffic, energy, and infrastructure scenarios. Urban planning goes virtual.

## The Leverage
Leaders here let cities test policies and designs in silico before real-world changes. It’s the ultimate smart-city tool.

## Skill Stack
GIS, simulation software, data integration, IoT.

## Economic Reality
Urban tech planner, smart city consultant, software platform developer. Often public-private partnerships, high impact.

## Proof of Work
Collect open data of your city (map, traffic, weather) and use a simple simulator to predict traffic flow after a road change.
