## The Matrix Truth
A digital twin is a virtual replica of a physical system. Engineers can simulate and optimize factories, buildings, or even organs using this mirror.

## The Leverage
Creating digital twins cuts R&D costs and downtime. It’s like having a sandbox copy of reality to test improvements safely.

## Skill Stack
Simulation software, IoT, data analytics, system modeling.

## Economic Reality
Simulation engineer, consultant, smart building designer. Used in manufacturing and urban planning.

## Proof of Work
Model a simple system (like an HVAC unit or machine). Simulate how changing one parameter (speed, size) affects performance.
