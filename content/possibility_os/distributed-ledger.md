## The Matrix Truth
Beyond Bitcoin: distributed ledgers (blockchains) can record any exchange (digital assets, contracts, identities) without central servers.

## The Leverage
Mastering ledger tech means reshaping trust in any field (finance, supply chain, identity). Early builders can capture new decentralized markets.

## Skill Stack
Blockchain protocols, consensus algorithms, peer-to-peer networks, security.

## Economic Reality
Blockchain architect, platform developer, consultant. Hype-driven but real applications emerging in finance and logistics.

## Proof of Work
Set up a private blockchain network (using a platform like Ethereum). Execute a sample transaction or smart contract on it.
