## The Matrix Truth
Delivery drones will carry packages and medicine to your home. This requires robotics, logistics, and airspace management all at once.

## The Leverage
Mastering drone delivery means dominating logistics for e-commerce and healthcare. It’s a new industrial revolution for last-mile transport.

## Skill Stack
Aerodynamics, robotics, logistics software, battery technology.

## Economic Reality
Drone engineer, logistics planner, startup CEO. Highly regulated, but big efficiency gains.

## Proof of Work
Build or simulate a drone flight path: use Google Maps and tools to plan an obstacle-free route to deliver a package safely.
