## The Matrix Truth
Education is moving online: interactive courses, AI tutors, and global classrooms. Knowledge can be accessed anywhere from any device.

## The Leverage
Building a platform or content means influencing millions of learners. It’s how education scales cheaply and adaptively.

## Skill Stack
Web development, instructional design, multimedia production, analytics.

## Economic Reality
Edtech developer, course creator, education consultant. Low tech cost, high competition but global reach.

## Proof of Work
Create a short online course or tutorial series on a tech topic you know. Host it on a site like GitHub Pages or YouTube.
