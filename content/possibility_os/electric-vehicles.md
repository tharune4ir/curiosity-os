## The Matrix Truth
Cars and bikes will be electric. Battery tech and power electronics are the new automotive frontier. This trend will disrupt oil, cities, and infrastructure.

## The Leverage
Master EV tech to reshape transportation. First movers can capture major auto markets as legacy car companies struggle to adapt.

## Skill Stack
Electrical engineering, battery chemistry, embedded systems, mechanical design.

## Economic Reality
EV engineer, charging infrastructure planner, mobility entrepreneur. Rapid industry growth, high R&D costs.

## Proof of Work
Build a simple electric drivetrain model: attach a motor to a battery and measure power and range under load. Document performance.
