## The Matrix Truth
Storing energy (batteries, supercapacitors) solves intermittency of renewables. Better batteries power phones, cars, and grids sustainably.

## The Leverage
Mastering storage means unleashing full potential of renewable energy. It’s a bottleneck solution that the world is racing to solve.

## Skill Stack
Electrochemistry, materials science, systems engineering, safety testing.

## Economic Reality
Battery R&D engineer, grid storage planner, EV company executive. Huge investments (gigafactories), competitive field.

## Proof of Work
Charge and discharge a battery (even small ones) while measuring efficiency. Try different charge rates and note changes.
