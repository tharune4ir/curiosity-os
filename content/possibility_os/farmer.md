## The Invisible Reality
Farmers grow plants and raise animals to provide food. They understand seasons and weather: planting crops when rains come and harvesting before frost. They treat the land like a garden and livestock like parts of a machine: feed, water, and care to get the best yield. They also balance nature: for example, rotating crops so soil stays healthy.

## The Mind-Blowing Hook
Modern farmers use technology like GPS tractors and drones to plant precisely. They work on producing more food on less land using science. Global agriculture feeds billions, and farmers now study genetics to create better crops (like drought-resistant wheat).

## The Player's Life
You wake early to feed animals or check fields. You plow, sow seeds, and water crops. You also monitor for pests or diseases, perhaps using natural remedies or chemicals. During harvest, you operate machinery to gather crops. You record yields and may sell products at markets or to companies.

## The Skill Tree
Plant biology or animal care knowledge, mechanical skills (to run farm machines), and perseverance (working outdoors in all weather).
