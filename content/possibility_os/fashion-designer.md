## The Invisible Reality
Fashion designers create clothing and style. They see fabric and color like a palette: combining textures and patterns to make attractive outfits. They sketch designs and then cut cloth (like sculpting fabric) to sew together. They must think about comfort and trends, mixing art and practicality.

## The Mind-Blowing Hook
Fashion affects everyone. Designers set trends for seasons in cities like Paris and New York. They may use technology like 3D printing for accessories. Big fashion shows display their work to global audiences.

## The Player's Life
You sketch clothes, choose fabrics, and make samples. You might work with tailors or seamstresses to sew designs. You also keep up with trends by attending shows. Each day can include drawing new ideas or fitting garments on models.

## The Skill Tree
Artistic vision for style, knowledge of textiles, and sewing or pattern-making skills.
