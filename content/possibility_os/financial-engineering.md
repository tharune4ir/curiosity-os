## The Matrix Truth
Using math and computer models to create new financial instruments. Complex derivatives or risk models can hedge and speculate at scale.

## The Leverage
If you can design a better financial product, firms will pay handsomely. It’s quantitative finance for big banks and hedge funds.

## Skill Stack
Probability, stochastic calculus, programming (C++, Python), finance theory.

## Economic Reality
Quant, risk manager, Wall Street developer. High entry barrier (often PhDs), extremely high salaries for top performers.

## Proof of Work
Implement a Monte Carlo simulation to price an option (or simple derivative). Show how price changes with volatility.
