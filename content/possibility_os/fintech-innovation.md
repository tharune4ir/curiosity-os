## The Matrix Truth
Finance is being rebuilt on technology. From mobile payments to algorithmic wealth management, fintech disaggregates banks into software.

## The Leverage
Leading fintech means lowering barriers to capital and payments worldwide. It can create billion-dollar companies by rethinking money flow.

## Skill Stack
Blockchain, data security, app development, financial modeling.

## Economic Reality
Fintech founder, investment banker turned coder, payment startup CTO. Investor-funded, regulatory challenges.

## Proof of Work
Prototype a simple peer-to-peer payment app or crypto wallet with minimal features. Demonstrate a secure money transfer on a test network.
