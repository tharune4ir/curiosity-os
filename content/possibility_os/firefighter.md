## The Invisible Reality
Firefighters fight fires and rescue people. They understand fire like a chemical reaction: fuel plus oxygen equals flames. They wear protective gear and use hoses to cool down fires, sometimes ventilating roofs to let smoke out. It's like controlling an out-of-control heater: they apply water or foam to absorb heat, and they work in teams for safety.

## The Mind-Blowing Hook
Firefighters respond to thousands of emergencies: not just fires but also accidents and disasters. They use tools like hydraulic cutters (the 'jaws of life') to rescue trapped people. Modern fire stations run on quick response, and firefighters keep fit to carry heavy equipment.

## The Player's Life
You train daily in drills and gym work. In a call, you put on gear quickly and drive to the scene. You may extinguish flames, break down doors, or carry injured people out. You also inspect buildings for hazards and teach fire safety in the community.

## The Skill Tree
Courage and teamwork, physical strength, and fire science (understanding how fires spread).
