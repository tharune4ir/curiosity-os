## The Matrix Truth
We can now fix genetic diseases by editing the DNA of living cells. Using viruses or nanoparticles, we deliver new genes to treat conditions at the source.

## The Leverage
Mastering gene therapy means curing diseases that were once hopeless. It’s biotech’s cutting edge with life-saving potential.

## Skill Stack
Genetics, virology, CRISPR gene editing, clinical research.

## Economic Reality
Medical researcher, biotech developer, clinical director. Long trials, heavy regulation, massive potential rewards.

## Proof of Work
Review a recent gene therapy case study. Write a summary of how they edited the gene and the outcome.
