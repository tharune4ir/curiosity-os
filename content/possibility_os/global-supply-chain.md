## The Matrix Truth
Goods now move across the world in days, not months. Technology optimizes shipping routes, inventory, and tracking from factory to doorstep.

## The Leverage
Improving supply chains saves massive costs and reduces waste. Tech leaders in logistics (like optimizing Amazon’s network) earn huge market share.

## Skill Stack
Operations research, analytics, logistics software, IoT tracking.

## Economic Reality
Supply chain manager, logistics tech founder, consultant. Backbone of global trade, demand for efficiency.

## Proof of Work
Map a product’s journey (from raw material to user) and identify two points where technology could speed up or cut cost.
