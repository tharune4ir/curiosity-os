## The Matrix Truth
Factories are going green with recycling, solar power, and low-waste processes. Sustainability is a core design goal now.

## The Leverage
Companies that build eco-friendly production lines save costs and win consumers. It’s also required by increasingly strict regulations.

## Skill Stack
Industrial engineering, energy management, waste reduction tech, lifecycle analysis.

## Economic Reality
Sustainability engineer, operations director, green tech consultant. ROI from efficiency often attractive to investors.

## Proof of Work
Audit a small production process (like a craft or hobby project). Identify materials and energy use, then suggest greener alternatives.
