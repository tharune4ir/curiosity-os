## The Matrix Truth
HCI studies how people use technology. It ensures devices and software are intuitive, accessible, and satisfying to use.

## The Leverage
Innovators here make tech that people actually want to use. It spans all products, from phones to AR goggles.

## Skill Stack
UX design, psychology, prototyping, coding.

## Economic Reality
UX researcher, interface designer, product manager. All tech products need HCI expertise.

## Proof of Work
Run a quick usability test: have 3 people try an unfamiliar app or site and record their difficulties. Suggest improvements.
