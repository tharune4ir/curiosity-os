## The Matrix Truth
Movies, games, and live events will become fully interactive experiences. Think VR concerts or holographic theater, not passive screens.

## The Leverage
Creating new forms of media sets cultural trends. Industry leaders here redefine how people consume content and spend leisure time.

## Skill Stack
Storytelling, game/audio design, VR/AR tech, event production.

## Economic Reality
Creative director, game designer, event technologist. Heavy R&D in XR, but exploding interest from media companies.

## Proof of Work
Write and film a short 360° video or VR experience (smartphone 360 camera). Share the link so others can view it in VR.
