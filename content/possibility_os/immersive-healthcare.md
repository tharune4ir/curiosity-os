## The Matrix Truth
VR and AR are used in medical training and therapy (like VR for pain management). Healthcare is becoming a digital, interactive experience.

## The Leverage
Building tech for health has life-or-death stakes and big budgets. It’s the most impactful way to apply innovation to saving lives.

## Skill Stack
Medical knowledge, VR/AR dev, psychology, UX design.

## Economic Reality
Medical app developer, hospital tech lead, wellness startup founder. Gains support from health systems, regulatory approval needed.

## Proof of Work
Prototype an AR overlay for a simple medical procedure (e.g., a digital guide on surgery). Explain its benefits.
