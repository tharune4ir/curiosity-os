## The Matrix Truth
Designing interactive products (apps, installations) shapes user behavior. Good UX/UI design bridges technology and psychology.

## The Leverage
A talented designer can make tech accessible and engaging. They are key in product teams and can launch innovative experiences.

## Skill Stack
UX/UI tools (Sketch, Figma), user research, basic coding (HTML/CSS/JS), psychology.

## Economic Reality
UX designer, product designer, creative director. Freelance or studio work, high demand in software and product companies.

## Proof of Work
Design a simple app interface for a new idea (sketch on paper or use a design tool) and explain user flow.
