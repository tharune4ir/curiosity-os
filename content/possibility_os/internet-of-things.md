## The Matrix Truth
Every device from fridges to streetlights can be smart and connected. IoT creates a network of sensors that generate massive data streams.

## The Leverage
Building IoT solutions means optimizing cities and factories. Early IoT integrators can gain efficiency advantages and new service models.

## Skill Stack
Embedded systems, networking (MQTT/CoAP), data analytics, security.

## Economic Reality
IoT engineer, solutions architect, product manager. Hardware-software blend, partnerships between tech and traditional industries.

## Proof of Work
Build a simple IoT device: e.g., a sensor (temperature or motion) that sends data to a server or phone and displays results.
