## The Matrix Truth
New materials (like graphene, nanomaterials, composites) change what’s possible in hardware. Everything from flexible phones to space elevators depends on them.

## The Leverage
Inventing a stronger, lighter, or smarter material can create new industries. Think of transforming construction, medicine, electronics.

## Skill Stack
Chemistry, physics, nanofabrication, microscopy.

## Economic Reality
Materials engineer, product developer, research scientist. Requires lab facilities, cross-industry demand.

## Proof of Work
Experiment with common materials: try combining materials (like carbon fiber with resin) or measure strength. Report results.
