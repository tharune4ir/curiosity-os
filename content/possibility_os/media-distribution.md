## The Matrix Truth
Streaming platforms and social apps have replaced TV networks. Content distribution is instantaneous and global, and algorithms decide what we watch.

## The Leverage
Innovating in distribution (like new streaming tech) can disrupt entire entertainment industries. Content demand is unlimited.

## Skill Stack
Content delivery networks, UX/UI, video compression, data analytics.

## Economic Reality
Product manager at a streaming service, content strategist, startup founder. Backed by big media budgets.

## Proof of Work
Set up a small video streaming server (using open-source tools) and play video to a friend. Track buffering vs network speed.
