## The Matrix Truth
Small loans and financial services for low-income entrepreneurs. Microfinance empowers people excluded from traditional banking.

## The Leverage
Scaling microfinance means pulling people out of poverty and unlocking rural economies. It’s high social impact with financial innovation.

## Skill Stack
Finance, social entrepreneurship, risk analysis, mobile payments.

## Economic Reality
Microfinance banker, NGO leader, fintech entrepreneur. Often nonprofit or social enterprise models with reinvestment focus.

## Proof of Work
Research a successful microloan case (like a farmer who got a loan for equipment). Analyze how the loan was repaid and its effects.
