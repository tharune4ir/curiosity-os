## The Matrix Truth
We can manipulate matter at the scale of atoms and molecules. This allows creation of ultra-strong materials, targeted drug delivery, and more.

## The Leverage
Small-scale control leads to huge breakthroughs. Imagine building machines smaller than a cell, or materials 100 times stronger than steel.

## Skill Stack
Materials science, chemistry, microscopy, programming for simulations.

## Economic Reality
Materials scientist, nano-engineering researcher, tech startup founder. Intensive lab work, government grants.

## Proof of Work
Model a nanomaterial property: use simulation software to see how a tiny lattice structure might filter water or carry medicine.
