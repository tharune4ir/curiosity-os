## The Matrix Truth
Direct interfaces (like smart implants or brain waves on a headset) are merging human and computer. Controlling devices by thought is nearing reality.

## The Leverage
This tech redefines disability solutions and human capability. It’s the next step in wearable tech and medical devices.

## Skill Stack
Neuroscience, hardware design, machine learning, bio-signal processing.

## Economic Reality
Neuroengineer, biotech startup founder, medical device developer. Requires clinical trials, but high impact funding.

## Proof of Work
Experiment with EEG: use an open EEG dataset and try to detect basic commands (like imagining movement vs rest).
