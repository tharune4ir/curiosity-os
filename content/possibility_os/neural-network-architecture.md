## The Matrix Truth
Designing neural nets (the layers and connections) is an art. New architectures (like transformers) can make AI much more powerful.

## The Leverage
Innovating on network design can improve every AI application. Mastering this is how research teams stay ahead in image, text, and voice recognition.

## Skill Stack
Deep learning frameworks, math (calculus, linear algebra), Python, GPU computing.

## Economic Reality
AI researcher, ML engineer, tech lead. Research labs and big tech invest heavily in new architectures.

## Proof of Work
Experiment with an open-source neural net on a dataset (like MNIST or CIFAR). Modify the architecture and compare performance.
