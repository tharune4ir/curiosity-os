## The Matrix Truth
The oceans cover most of Earth and hold potential energy, minerals, and data (sensors). Submersibles and marine robotics explore this frontier.

## The Leverage
If you can harness the sea (like tidal energy or mining seabed), you open a vast resource. Ocean tech advances help climate resilience and resources.

## Skill Stack
Marine engineering, robotics, geology, renewable energy.

## Economic Reality
Marine engineer, oceanographer, clean energy developer. Challenges from harsh environment, but huge untapped value.

## Proof of Work
Design a concept of a wave-energy device or underwater sensor network, explaining how it would survive ocean conditions.
