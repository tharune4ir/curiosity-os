## The Invisible Reality
Paramedics are like on-the-spot doctors who work in ambulances. They are trained to give emergency care, such as CPR or stopping bleeding, often in chaotic situations. They think quickly: using first aid tools and medicines inside an ambulance (which is like a mobile hospital), they stabilize patients' conditions on the way to the hospital.

## The Mind-Blowing Hook
Paramedics can be the difference between life and death in accidents or sudden illnesses. Right now, they use high-tech ambulances with heart monitors and portable ventilators. They often team with emergency doctors to save lives in the crucial first minutes.

## The Player's Life
You drive ambulances or reach patients quickly, assess injuries, and provide immediate treatment. You work alongside firefighters and police at scenes. You might give injections or set broken bones, always communicating with the hospital about patient status.

## The Skill Tree
Emergency first aid skills, calmness under pressure, and physical fitness (handling equipment and sometimes carrying patients).
