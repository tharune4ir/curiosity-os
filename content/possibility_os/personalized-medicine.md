## The Matrix Truth
Healthcare tailored to your genetics and lifestyle is becoming standard. Doctors use AI and gene data to pick treatments that work for you.

## The Leverage
Leaders in this field can shift medicine from one-size-fits-all to individually optimized. Huge impact on health outcomes, healthcare costs.

## Skill Stack
Genomics, AI for healthcare, clinical research, data ethics.

## Economic Reality
Medical data analyst, biotech startup founder, clinician-scientist. Stringent privacy laws, big pharma collaborations.

## Proof of Work
Analyze a toy health dataset (like heart rate or blood test results) and build a model to predict risk factors for a condition.
