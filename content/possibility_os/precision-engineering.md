## The Matrix Truth
Building parts with micron accuracy: semiconductors, micro-optics, custom prosthetics. High precision tech underlies modern chips and sensors.

## The Leverage
Experts here build the critical components for cutting-edge devices. If you enable micro-nano fabrication, you fuel tech advances everywhere.

## Skill Stack
Mechanical design, optics, CAD/CAM, quality control.

## Economic Reality
Precision engineer, semiconductor fab technician, aerospace component designer. High barriers, but key industry roles.

## Proof of Work
Design a simple precision part (like a tiny gear or lens holder) in CAD and simulate its fit or tolerance.
