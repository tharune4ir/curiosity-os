## The Matrix Truth
Building relationships is essential. Platforms like LinkedIn and networking events connect talent with opportunity faster than cold outreach.

## The Leverage
Mastering networking accelerates your career and business growth. Top professionals often attribute success to who they know.

## Skill Stack
Communication, social media, personal branding, empathy.

## Economic Reality
Recruiter, business development, community manager. Low tech cost, high personal effort needed.

## Proof of Work
Create a strong online professional profile, join two interest groups, and attend a virtual networking meetup. Reflect on new connections made.
