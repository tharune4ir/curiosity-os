## The Matrix Truth
Computers can use the weird rules of quantum physics. Qubits can represent 0 and 1 at the same time, letting us solve problems way faster.

## The Leverage
Mastering quantum tech means cracking currently unsolvable problems in chemistry and cryptography. It’s like giving yourself superpowers in simulation and security.

## Skill Stack
Quantum mechanics, linear algebra, Python (Qiskit or Cirq), algorithms.

## Economic Reality
Quantum hardware engineer, quantum algorithm researcher. Massive lab costs, cutting-edge startups.

## Proof of Work
