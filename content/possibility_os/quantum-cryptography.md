## The Matrix Truth
Using quantum physics, we can create unbreakable encryption (quantum key distribution). Future networks will be secure against any computer attack.

## The Leverage
Securing data with quantum means absolute trust in banking, government, and personal privacy. Being a pioneer here means guarding the digital world of tomorrow.

## Skill Stack
Quantum mechanics, information theory, optics, network engineering.

## Economic Reality
Security researcher, quantum network engineer, cryptography consultant. Niche field with government interest.

## Proof of Work
Simulate a basic quantum key exchange protocol (like BB84) using a programming library, and explain why it’s secure.
