## The Matrix Truth
Sensors using quantum effects achieve extreme precision (like detecting minute magnetic fields). They are used in navigation, medical imaging, and more.

## The Leverage
If you build devices with quantum sensitivity, you can revolutionize measurements. This unlocks new science and better tech (like MRI or brain scans).

## Skill Stack
Quantum physics, optics, electronics, instrumentation.

## Economic Reality
Sensor physicist, R&D engineer, medical device innovator. Specialized markets (health, defense, space) with high R&D investment.

## Proof of Work
Demonstrate a principle: for example, use a magnet and wire to show how a tiny change in magnetic field induces a voltage.
