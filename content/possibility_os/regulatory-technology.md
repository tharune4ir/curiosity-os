## The Matrix Truth
RegTech uses software to comply with financial and data regulations. It makes legal compliance automated and cost-effective.

## The Leverage
Companies that simplify compliance can serve every regulated industry. Governments are outsourcing rule enforcement to code.

## Skill Stack
Finance law, software development, data security, analytics.

## Economic Reality
RegTech developer, compliance officer, policy startup CEO. Growing field as regulations expand globally.

## Proof of Work
Build a form validator that checks a fake financial report against simple compliance rules (like correct fields present).
