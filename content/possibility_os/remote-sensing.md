## The Matrix Truth
Satellites and drones gather data about Earth: climate patterns, crop health, or natural disasters. We monitor the planet remotely.

## The Leverage
Working in remote sensing means contributing to climate science and security. All big companies and governments use this data.

## Skill Stack
Geospatial analysis, sensor tech, data science, environmental science.

## Economic Reality
Remote sensing analyst, GIS specialist, drone data startup. Platforms like NASA or Google Earth Engine provide data, driving innovation.

## Proof of Work
Use a public satellite image and analyze it (e.g., NDVI for vegetation). Show how changes in the image indicate an environmental factor.
