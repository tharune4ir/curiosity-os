## The Matrix Truth
Energy from the sun, wind, or geothermal heat is now cheapest and fastest-growing. The grid is shifting away from fossil fuels.

## The Leverage
Experts who scale up clean energy solutions are shaping the climate future. Leading in renewables means energy independence and huge markets.

## Skill Stack
Electrical engineering, power systems, data analysis, sustainable design.

## Economic Reality
Solar engineer, clean-tech entrepreneur, policy advisor. Strong government support, long timelines to profitability.

## Proof of Work
Build a small solar panel setup or wind turbine model. Measure its power output and optimize angle or design for max energy.
