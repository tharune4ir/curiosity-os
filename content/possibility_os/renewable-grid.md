## The Matrix Truth
Electric grids are moving to 100% renewables with storage and smart distribution. The old coal plants are being replaced by wind, solar, and batteries.

## The Leverage
Mastering smart grids and storage unlocks the clean energy economy. It’s the foundation for electrifying transport, industry, and homes.

## Skill Stack
Power engineering, smart grid tech, energy storage, data analytics.

## Economic Reality
Power systems engineer, utility technology manager, energy analyst. Heavy infrastructure work, public-private investment.

## Proof of Work
Model an electricity grid with solar and battery inputs. Show how adding storage smooths out supply variations.
