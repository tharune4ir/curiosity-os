## The Matrix Truth
Robots combine mechanics, electronics, and AI to perform tasks. As components get cheaper, robots move from factories to homes and remote fields.

## The Leverage
Building robots means automating dangerous or boring jobs. The first person to deploy robots in a new area can dominate that industry.

## Skill Stack
Mechanics, embedded programming (C/C++/Python), sensors/actuators, control theory.

## Economic Reality
Robotics engineer, automation consultant, drone startup founder. Capital-intensive prototyping, rapidly growing demand.

## Proof of Work
Build a simple robot: for example, a line-following car with Arduino. Share the circuit and code that controls its motors.
