## The Matrix Truth
High-speed internet from space is now real with low-orbit satellite constellations. Remote areas and developing markets can be connected directly from orbit.

## The Leverage
Building global networks from space leaps ahead of terrestrial limits. Leading it means bridging the digital divide and serving billions.

## Skill Stack
Network engineering, satellite communications, RF engineering, data routing.

## Economic Reality
Network engineer, space startup strategist, telecom analyst. Large capital expense but huge untapped markets.

## Proof of Work
Calculate the coverage pattern of a constellation: given altitude and separation, find how many satellites cover Earth.
