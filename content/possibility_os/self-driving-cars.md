## The Matrix Truth
Vehicles will drive themselves using AI, sensors, and maps. This disrupts taxi, delivery, and personal transport industries.

## The Leverage
Creating the first safe autonomous system is a path to a massive market. It combines AI, sensors, and controls in a real-world robot.

## Skill Stack
Computer vision, AI, mechanical systems, control theory.

## Economic Reality
Autonomous vehicle engineer, robotics firm founder, sensor specialist. High R&D costs, regulatory hurdles, but the prize is huge.

## Proof of Work
Use simulation tools (like CARLA) or a scaled model car to implement lane keeping or obstacle avoidance. Share performance data.
