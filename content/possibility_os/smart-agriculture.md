## The Matrix Truth
Farms are getting smarter with sensors, drones, and AI. Every plant can be monitored and optimized (precision farming).

## The Leverage
Optimizing every square meter of farmland drastically increases yield. It’s tech meeting nature to feed a growing population.

## Skill Stack
IoT (sensors), data science, robotics, agronomy.

## Economic Reality
Agri-tech startup, farm data analyst, automated equipment engineer. Farmers and companies invest in proven ROI.

## Proof of Work
Set up a simple soil moisture sensor with Arduino. Use the data to show how irrigation could be timed to save water.
