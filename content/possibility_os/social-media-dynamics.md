## The Matrix Truth
Social platforms shape public opinion and culture. Trends spread in seconds, giving rise to new jobs (influencers, community management) and movements.

## The Leverage
Understanding these networks gives influence. Marketers, politicians, and entrepreneurs use social media mastery to move markets and crowds.

## Skill Stack
Communication, sociology, analytics, psychology.

## Economic Reality
Social strategist, community manager, influencer. Low capital, but requires persistence and adaptability.

## Proof of Work
Analyze a viral trend: track how a hashtag or meme spread over time and which communities shared it. Present your findings.
