## The Matrix Truth
Robots can interact with humans using speech and gestures. Social robots assist in care, education, and entertainment.

## The Leverage
Creating robots that people trust and relate to opens markets in healthcare and services. It’s engineering plus psychology.

## Skill Stack
AI, machine learning, human-robot interaction, design.

## Economic Reality
Robotics designer, product manager, therapy tech founder. Niche field combining tech and human factors.

## Proof of Work
Program a chatbot or virtual agent (like using Dialogflow) and test it with friends, then refine to improve its responses.
