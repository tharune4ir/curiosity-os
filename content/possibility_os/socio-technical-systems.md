## The Matrix Truth
These are networks where technology and social structures co-evolve (like social networks or workplace tech cultures). They require understanding both people and tech.

## The Leverage
Masters of socio-technical design build sustainable solutions that work in society, not just tech. It’s key for any large-scale project adoption.

## Skill Stack
Systems thinking, sociology, software, management.

## Economic Reality
Systems engineer, change manager, consultant. Companies use this lens to improve collaboration and technology rollout.

## Proof of Work
Choose a technology (like remote work tools) and analyze how it changes social behavior at work. Present recommendations.
