## The Matrix Truth
Robots and software now manage warehouses and shipments. Automation streamlines getting products to customers.

## The Leverage
Automating logistics cuts costs for big retailers and manufacturers. Leaders here dictate e-commerce and global trade efficiency.

## Skill Stack
Robotics, ERP systems, data analytics, operations research.

## Economic Reality
Logistics engineer, warehouse automation specialist, supply chain analyst. Adoption by major companies, ROI-driven.

## Proof of Work
Map a warehouse process and propose a robotic solution: e.g., an AGV path or sorting algorithm, showing time saved.
