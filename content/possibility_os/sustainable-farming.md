## The Matrix Truth
Farming tech now uses less water and land through techniques like vertical farms, hydroponics, and precision irrigation.

## The Leverage
Innovating agriculture means feeding more people with fewer resources. It’s critical for future food security and climate adaptation.

## Skill Stack
Agronomy, IoT sensors, data analytics, mechanical systems (greenhouses).

## Economic Reality
Agritech entrepreneur, farm manager, environmental consultant. Often local projects with global interest and grants.

## Proof of Work
Design a mini hydroponics setup: grow a plant in nutrient solution and monitor growth vs soil. Optimize nutrients or light.
