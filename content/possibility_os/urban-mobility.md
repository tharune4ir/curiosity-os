## The Matrix Truth
Cities need smarter transport: electric buses, bike-sharing, on-demand shuttles. Mobility as a service is overtaking private car ownership.

## The Leverage
Innovating in how people move in cities can reduce pollution and commute times. It’s a big market with government and private interest.

## Skill Stack
Transportation engineering, data analytics, app development, urban planning.

## Economic Reality
Mobility planner, startup CEO, public transit consultant. Often public-private projects, can scale with user base.

## Proof of Work
Design a mini mobility model: simulate a bike-share system in a city block and optimize station placement based on demand.
